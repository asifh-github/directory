Monsieur Hamière

Breed Marseille White

The "Marseille White" Pig hails from the southern part of France and is one of the most numerous of al pig breeds, widely used in crossbreeding for intensive pig farmimg around the world. It is notorious for its bad temper and distinctive loud squealing. Today it is one of the most favoured by pig breeders, leading to uniformity of pigs for meat on large scale.
