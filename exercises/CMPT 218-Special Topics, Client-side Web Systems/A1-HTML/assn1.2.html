Dan Gog

Breed Husum Red Pied

The "Husum Red Pied" is a rare breed of domestic pig with nick name "Danish Protest Pig". It originates in North Frisia in Southern Schelswig in the beginning of the 20th century. Due to its red color, its broad white vertical belt and the trace of a white horizontal belt resembling the colors of Denmark, it was made a symbol of thier cultural identity.
