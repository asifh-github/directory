Full Job Description

Publix is able to offer virtual employment for this position in the following states: FL, GA, AL, TN, SC, NC, VA

As part of the Digital Product team work with Product Managers to define measurable objectives for channels and features. Work with developers to implement tracking of those objectives on Publix.com and Publix app.

Responsibilities:
  • Work with stakeholders to define success metrics for web and app features
  • Translate desired metrics in to detailed requirements to be carried out by developers and analytics SMEs
  • Participate in sprint cycles and work with developers to get requirements implemented
  • Review work of developers and sign off when complete
  • Manage configuration of tag management tools (Adobe Launch and AEP)
  • Mange Adobe Analytics admin console
  • Document changes to tracking

Skills:
  • Expert proficiency with Adobe Analytics or Google Analytics both in terms of analysis and tool management
  • Expert proficiency with the principles of analyzing customer behavior across digital platforms
  • Strong familiarity with web technology. Developer level proficiency not necessary
  • Comfortable interacting with developers
Required Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or a related analytical field or equivalent experience
  • Minimum 5 to 7 years business systems analysis experience
  • Minimum 2-3 years analytics implementation experience
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills with the ability to effectively synthesize and visualize recommendations.
  • Demonstrated experience working with UX and Product teams to improve digital customer experiences.
  • Ability to excel in a fast-paced, cross-functional environment.
  • Ability to manage time effectively and consistently meet deadlines.
