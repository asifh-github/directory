Full Job Description

Location: North America, USA, Remote
Ref: DETECTIONNA01209
Division: Smiths Detection
Job Function: Research Development

  • Develop automated detection algorithms and related products for 3D and 2D imaging based detection systems in software packages and programming languages/environments such as Matlab, C++, and Python
  • Provide Domain knowledge support for technical areas such as image processing, feature detection, 3D image reconstruction, and/or segmentation as directed, representing Smiths Detection and providing feedback to internal and external customers
  • Provide technical support of key testing activities, including support of laboratory and field testing activities
  • Interact with external technical personnel, industry groups, testing standards groups, selected customers, and internal management to provide feedback into product development, design, and certification efforts
  • Collect sample image, library data, and technical information to support algorithm development projects
  • Take part in US-Based testing and development of detection algorithm software including executing test plans and experiments in a laboratory setting
  • Must Comply with and ensure department compliance with Company health, safety, and environmental policies
  • Must comply with all applicable US export and security regulations

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Design and test new detection solutions to meet customer specifications or in-house project requirements.
  • Analyze customer specifications or project requirements to create the required plans and frameworks for algorithm and machine learning development success
  • Collaborate with global teams of in the system software and algorithm development domains.
  • Design and execute data collection protocols to ensure requirements for development efforts are met in a timely and cost-effective manner.
  • Support Demos and Customer presentations of Low Energy Imaging equipment.
  • Support traditional detection development work through data collection, labeling, and algorithm development.
  • Compose and edit highly technical documentation, correspondence, and literature
  • Maintain and communicate status of projects to the management team.
  • Work independently using written feedback, general standards, and guidelines.
