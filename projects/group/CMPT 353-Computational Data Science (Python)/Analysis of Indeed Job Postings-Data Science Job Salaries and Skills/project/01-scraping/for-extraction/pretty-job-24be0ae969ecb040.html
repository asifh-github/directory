Overview:
Develop, configure, maintain, test and monitor processes in support of a reliable, scalable, and evolving data & analytics platform in support of Children's Mercy goals of becoming a data driven organization.

The Rising T1DE Alliance is a cutting-edge Type 1 Diabetes research team. One of our key innovations is an Azure data lakehouse (D-Data Dock) that integrates, improves, and analyzes a broad range of diabetes data. Improving and maintaining this infrastructure is the primary responsibility of this Data Engineer position. This D-Data Dock is currently internal-only with goals to implement at other diabetes centers. The Data Engineer will help prepare for and realize these outside implementation goals.

Responsibilities:
  • Maintaining and improving an internally-built data lakehouse
  • Develop a testing plan for continued development of the data lakehouse and build additional monitoring and data validation into the existing pipelines
  • Respond to internal requests for one-time reports as well as long-term improvements.
Qualifications:
