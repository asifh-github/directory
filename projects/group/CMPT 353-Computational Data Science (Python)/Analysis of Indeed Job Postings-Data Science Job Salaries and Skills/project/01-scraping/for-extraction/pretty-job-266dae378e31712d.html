Position: Data Analyst, Operations Data Analytics and Business Intelligence

Employment Type: Full Time

Location: Remote


Tiber Overview

Tiber Capital Group is one of the top 10 aggregators of single-family rental homes in the United States. Our team is committed to renovating to the highest standards, and providing residents with comfortable homes to raise their families in. With more than 8,000 homes across some of the country’s fastest-growing markets, our residents appreciate the flexibility and freedom of living in a superior single-family home without the obligations of home ownership. Our portfolio has continued to grow considerably over the past 2 years. We see increasing demand for our professionally managed single-family rental homes, and we anticipate continued growth in all our markets.

The Position

You will assist Tiber’s Operation Data Analytics Team in developing data-centric use cases to support the firm’s acquisition, renovation, and property management teams. You will work to source, validate, and analyzed new datasets, as well as leverage Tiber’s internal data to create customized reports, models and visualizations for department leadership and senior management. A successful candidate will be highly organized, detailed-oriented, self-motivated, as well as demonstrate the ability to respond effectively to changing workloads and priorities.

About the Role

  • Dive into vast amounts of internal and external data to identify, refine, communicate, and launch reporting or data driven tools at a rapid pace.
  • Perform data evaluation, analysis and validation for recurring operational reports and dashboards; build presentations, documents and materials to support informed decision making.
  • Build scalable self-service analytical models to be used by all cross functional partners in analyzing both the current state of the business and forecasting options.
  • Partner with various teams to define data metrics and develop a deep understanding of interdependencies between cross-functional systems, datasets, and processes.
  • Support the business in pairing metrics with business and process knowledge to improve management visibility, find opportunities for business optimization, and streamline processes.

Who you Are

  • Bachelors or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Finance, Real Estate or related fields
  • 3-5 Year(s) of business/operations analysis experience
  • Proficient in Phyton, SQL and Power BI required, experience with other programming languages preferred.
  • Proficient in Excel and strong working knowledge of MS Office
  • Excited to work on several different, interrelated projects with a variety of stakeholders in a fast-paced environment
  • Effective written and oral communication skills and ability to concisely explain your methods, reasoning, and conclusions
  • Strong problem-solving skills, very creative mindset, and unwavering attention to detail
  • A collaborative working style with a strong focus on execution and results
