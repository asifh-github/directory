Full Job Description

We are looking for a Data Scientist passionate about all things data. This person has experience working in a startup environment. The role supports methodology development for multiple production systems.
TECHNOLOGY :

  • Python, Spark, SQL
  • Modern deep learning and boosted tree frameworks
  • AWS, GCP, and On-prem Ecosystem
  • Kubernetes, Docker

RESPONSIBILITIES :

  • Partner with leaders, engineers, and data scientists to lead methodology development of production systems.
  • Communicate and drive adoption of new tech across our data science and engineering department.
  • Break down product initiative requirements, identify dependencies and create implementation plans.
  • Collaborate with product management and engineering leaders to understand requirements and gain alignment on product scope ahead of implementation.
  • Act as a product expert to answer questions, explain results, and evangelize our products to customers or internal clients.

QUALIFICATIONS :

  • PhD or 2 years professional experience with a Master's

2+ years of production experience with:

  • A scripting language like Python.
  • Developing methodologies that influence products reaching customers.
  • Working cross-functionally delivering products to clients.
  • Rigorous statistics background in applied settings.
  • Developing methodologies for business solutions.
  • Writing SQL statements.
  • Communication with upper management.
  • Probability and statistics competency

Other Qualifications (Preferred but NOT required):

  • Experience specifically with Pyspark and working with large data sets.
  • Experience handling PII data in compliance environments like CCPA or GDPR
  • Experience with causal problems
  • Experience with graph problems
