Operation HOPE sees social justice through an economic lens. We are dedicated to ﬁnancial dignity and inclusion as we equip adults and young people with the ﬁnancial education, coaching and connections to secure a better future - providing guidance through personal aspirations and life’s challenges - facilitating a journey to ﬁnancial independence.

Operation HOPE is currently seeking a DATA SCIENTIST to analyze and manage data for actionable insights. The candidate best suited for this role knows how to extract meaning from and interpret data, which requires both tools and methods from statistics and machine learning, as well as being human. S/he spends a lot of time in the process of collecting, cleaning, and munging data, because data is never clean. This process requires persistence, statistics, and software engineering skills—skills that are also necessary for understanding biases in the data, and for debugging logging output from code.

Once the data is in shape, a crucial part is exploratory data analysis, which combines visualization and data sense. The preferred candidate can find patterns, build models, and algorithms—some with the intention of understanding usage and others to serve as prototypes that ultimately get baked back into our work. The Data Scientist may design experiments and is a critical part of data-driven decision making. This role will communicate with Teammates, Program Operations staff and Leadership in clear language and with data visualizations so that even if colleagues are not immersed in the data themselves, they will understand the implications.

ESSENTIAL JOB DUTIES:

  • Identifying the data-analytics problems that offer the greatest opportunities to the organization
  • Determining the correct data sets and variables
  • Collecting large sets of structured and unstructured data from disparate sources
  • Act as lead expert in predictive analytics, statistical modeling, big data, data mining applications, enterprise analytics, data-driven decision making, data visualization and data storytelling
  • Cleaning and validating the data to ensure accuracy, completeness, and uniformity
  • Manage and offer recommendations for the organization’s data architecture
  • Devising and applying models and algorithms to mine the stores of big data
  • Analyzing the data to identify patterns and trends
  • Interpreting the data to discover solutions and opportunities
  • Communicating findings to stakeholders using visualization and other means
  • Work on multiple projects concurrently, will self-manage their tasks with minimal supervision, and may be asked to lead small projects
  • Collaborate with other team members on scoping solutions and project decision points
  • Make strategic recommendations on data collection, integration and retention requirements, incorporating business requirements and knowledge of best practices
  • Operate in various development environments (Agile, Waterfall, etc.) while collaborating with key stakeholders
  • Identify opportunities for leveraging company data to improve and drive business solutions
  • Mentor other team members
  • Keep abreast of the latest technology developments in data science
  • All other duties as assigned

QUALIFICATIONS

  • High aspirations for all people to be successful and empowered financially
  • Minimum undergraduate degree or higher in Data/Computer Science, Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, Physics, Social Sciences or relevant equivalent experience
  • 3-5 years of data management and analysis experience in the credit risk, telecommunications, financial services, marketing, fraud, or insurance analytics arena, or 1-2 years of experience if you hold an advanced degree (MS or PhD)
  • 2+ years of experience with data querying/handling tools (Spark, Python, SQL, Spotfire, Tableau or other industry-standard tools – preference to candidates with PowerBI)
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience with Azure and Dynamics 365 (If the candidate has a Microsoft Dynamics certification or holds similar certifications or knowledge less than 5 years of experience is negotiable)
  • Minimum of 1-year experience analyzing large (multi-TB to PB), complex data sets for consumer or commercial data
  • Experience developing and deploying Data Matching and Entity Resolution algorithms and/or have previously worked on record linkage capabilities
  • Theoretical understanding of the algorithms behind real-world machine learning and artificial intelligence applications
  • Works closely with other development team members to understand complex product requirements and translate them into software designs
  • Demonstrates competencies in fundamentals of software engineering and best practices
  • Experience with model feature creation, selection and value imputation
  • Broad experience building different types of advanced analytic solutions
