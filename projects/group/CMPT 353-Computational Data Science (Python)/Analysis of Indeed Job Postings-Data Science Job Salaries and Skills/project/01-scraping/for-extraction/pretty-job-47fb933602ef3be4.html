Full Job Description

Position Overview:
The data science team is responsible for transforming raw data into useful data systems by supporting data capture, processing, automation, and operationalization of large data pipelines for the development of high-throughput models and analysis as well as disseminating results to main stakeholders across the company.
In addition to learning about modern data analysis and forecasting techniques, this role will have a primary responsibility for a project that will drive value for the company.


This role will start May/June 2023 - August 2023 and work out of our Saint Louis, MO office.


Essential Functions:
  • Special Research Project
  • This project will focus on estimating weather persistence across major crops and geographies and verify if/how those patterns have changed over time.
  • This project will require the candidate to collect internal and external data on crop cycles and crop calendars for major crops and geographies, manipulate internal weather data and devise a method to efficiently display results and conclusions.
  • The work will comprise of weekly cadence meetings and a final presentation of results.


Skills/Experience Requirements:
  • Studying Data Science, Data Analytics, Economics, or related field
  • Familiarity with statistical modeling techniques while able to pay close attention to data preparation and exploration
  • Familiarity with Python’s standard analytical packages
  • Self-motivation with the Ability to Work Independently


Bunge (NYSE: BG) is a world leader in sourcing, processing and supplying oilseed and grain products and ingredients. Founded in 1818, Bunge’s expansive network feeds and fuels a growing world, creating sustainable products and opportunities for more than 70,000 farmers and the consumers they serve across the globe. The company is headquartered in St. Louis, Missouri and has 25,000 employees worldwide who stand behind more than 350 port terminals, oilseed processing plants, grain facilities, and food and ingredient production and packaging facilities around the world.
