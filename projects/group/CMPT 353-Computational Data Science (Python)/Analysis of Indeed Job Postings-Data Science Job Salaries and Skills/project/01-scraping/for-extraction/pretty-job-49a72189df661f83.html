What you'll do

When our square shaped burgers made their first sizzle on the scene more than 50 years ago, people knew our approach wasn’t like any other. Same goes for the way we support our employees. Our culture of openness, flexibility, and inclusiveness allows everybody to flourish in their own way. If you’re looking for a career where you can be part of the action as we continue to grow our iconic brand – We got you!

As a Lead Data Engineer, you will be responsible for developing and executing all aspects of integrations between the various (source) systems and the Wendy’s Data platform. In the context of delivering strategic Business Intelligence and Advanced Analytics use cases, you will be responsible for leading the design of data pipelines and owning their development, testing and release working with internal and external engineering resources.

Responsibilities

The primary focus of this role is to lead the design and development of data movement (ETL) into and within the Wendy’s data platform both on premise and in cloud, ETL architecture design and working closely with the extended BI & Data Management team for opportunities to deliver “Data as a service”. As the Lead Data Engineer, you will be expected to design and build operationally robust data pipelines that can be supported efficiently and minimize escalations to you. Another key element of the role is to contribute towards best practices and frameworks to deliver a scalable data platform using newer tools and technology.

Partner with other BI Team and Business stakeholders to understand data engineering requirements and provide scalable solutions in alignment with business technology strategy and architecture.

Develop and maintain end-to-end integrations of Wendy’s data platform with various source systems and design data pipelines to fulfil Business Intelligence and Analytics needs of the organization

Develop and build solutions on the Wendy’s data platform using various Data Engineering technologies such as Informatica, Snaplogic, Talend among others

Perform technical analysis, level-of-effort estimation, design, development, implementation of applications

Ownership of all technical aspects of the Wendy’s Data platform, including custom development

Keep up to date on industry trends and recommend solutions beyond to meet business needs.

Help lead the technical direction of the team, assist in road mapping enhancements for Data Warehouse and Data Pipelines

Provides input and insight into the overall strategic roadmap for assigned products

Approves final iteration plan for assigned products.

Approves implementation strategy of enhancements, features, and integrations

Provides input on IT vendor partners to ensure effective service delivery

Definition of when a deliverable is completed

Approve or reject deliverables related to projects and operation improvements
