Role Snapshot

This role serves the Government Health Administrators (Medicare) division through statistical data analysis to identify existing issues and support divisional strategies of process and technology innovation and profitable growth through the adoption of new industry analytical approaches to ensure continued quality improvement.

How do I know this opportunity is right for me? If you:

  • Enjoy working with BIG DATA!
  • Love analyzing data for trends and outcomes.
  • Are creative, innovative, and get excited about using data differently!
  • Seek opportunities to utilize your personal responsibility to interpret and analyze data from various sources and recommend best approaches!
  • Like to present data analysis to various both internal and external key stakeholder.
  • Relish collaboration with your peers and other subject matter experts.
  • Have the drive to manage your own projects from start to completion while balancing accuracy and timeliness!
  • Keep current with industry quality analytic approaches and proficiency with business intelligence tools.

What will I gain from this role?

  • Working with a forward thinking and highly collaborative team.
  • Getting to do fun and exploratory data analysis that doesn’t require cleaning data!
  • Learning about the Medicare Program.
  • Serving Medicare beneficiaries and actively protecting the Medicare Trust Fund.
  • Experience working in an environment that serves our Nation’s military, veterans, Guard and Reserves and Medicare beneficiaries.
  • Working in a continuous performance feedback environment.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Bachelor's Degree in relevant field (Preferably: Data Science, Healthcare Informatics, Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, Computer Science, etc.) or equivalent post high school and/or related work experience.

Preferred Qualifications

  • 2 or more years of experience with business intelligence/analytic tools or programming languages used to interpret results and summarization for presentations. Examples of these are R, Python, SAP Business Objects, Statistical Analysis System (SAS), Structured Query Language (SQL), Google Analytics
  • Experience with Medicaid, Medicare, TRICARE, VA or any insurance claims data
  • Professional healthcare analytic experience
  • Exposure to evaluation of business processes
  • Master's degree in relevant field

Your Team

The InSight Data & Analytics Team exists to utilize innovative data and analytics strategies to identify and prevent fraud, waste, and abuse of the Medicare trust fund. We support internal clinical departments in detecting problematic billing behavior amongst providers and beneficiaries. We also support program management departments in identifying statistical trends and insights from business data. The team consists of 1 Data Analytics Manager and 4 other Healthcare Informatics Analysts.
