Data Science & Statistics Intern/Co-Op


YOUR TASKS AND RESPONSIBILITIES

The primary responsibilities of this role, Data Science & Statistics Intern/Co-Op, are to:

  • Develop code in multiple programming languages (e.g., Python, R, etc.)
  • Support mining of process data from IT systems and databases;
  • Develop workflows for mining, preprocessing and visualization of Data;
  • Evaluate and apply different machine learning methods;
  • Interact with DSS members to support activities and projects in the areas of bioprocess monitoring, root cause analysis, process understanding and process improvements;
  • Communicate outcome of development activities to the team;
  • Document project outcome in a short technical report;
  • Focus is internal, works within own department and interfaces with external groups on specific tasks/assignments, has satisfactoryinterpersonal skills and communicates results to peers and supervisors effectively.
