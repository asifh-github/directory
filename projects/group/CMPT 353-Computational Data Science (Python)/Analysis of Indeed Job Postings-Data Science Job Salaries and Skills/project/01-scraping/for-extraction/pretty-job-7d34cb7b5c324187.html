Company Overview

Flock Safety provides the first public safety operating system that empowers private communities and law enforcement to work together to eliminate crime. We are committed to protecting human privacy and mitigating bias in policing with the development of best-in-class technology rooted in ethical design, which unites civilians and public servants in pursuit of a safer, more equitable society.

The Role

Flock is looking for a passionate Ops Data Scientist to explore, mine, and build data, and leverage it using advanced analytical techniques to help drive critical business decisions.

How you'll make an impact:

  • Perform analyses on large datasets using statistical and AI techniques to derive insights
  • Design and build seamless decision support tools that leverage advanced analytical techniques
  • Understand and augment key data sources to be leveraged in decision making
  • Work with teams from across the business to execute on data and analytics needs to help them better understand tactical and strategic opportunities

We're looking for people who have:

  • BS/MS in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering or related field, or equivalent industry experience
  • 3+ years proven experience as a data scientist, ML engineer, or BI engineer
  • Strong experience using Python and its core AI/ML/OR packages
  • Strong SQL and data processing skills
  • Experience modeling data to drive analytics
  • Experience in Tableau
  • Ability to communicate and drive for solutions to complex and abstract problems
  • Effectively communicate, and seek to understand and be understood
