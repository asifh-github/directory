Position Description:
Serco’s Training and Experience Program (STEP) is designed to provide undergraduate university and college students paid accredited internship opportunities to gain meaningful, hands-on work experience in a professional environment. Our goal is to help you build a career roadmap that provides you with challenging and rewarding learning experiences with Serco.

Serco offers internship opportunities across the following three career tracks: Business, Technical, and Engineering. Our 2023 internship opportunities are available throughout the U.S. and Canada, beginning on May 22nd and concluding on August 11th, with 1 week off July 3rd-7th. You will work with your manager to identify the best working schedule for you, working no more than an average of 36 hours a week with the added perk of half-days on Fridays.

In addition to gaining hands-on experience, you will attend training sessions taught by skilled leaders in our company to better prepare you for a full-time career. Furthermore, we believe great ideas come from all levels of the company! Each year we challenge our interns to work in diverse teams to develop, propose and execute on a project that improves our ways of working. Throughout this project you will work with Senior Sponsors and meet with members of our Executive Team.

STEP Program Benefits include:
  • Paid, accredited internship with possibility of attaining college credit (~3 credits)
  • Half days on Fridays
  • Paid training sessions covering a wide variety of professional development topics – resume building, presentation skills, operational excellence, and more!
  • Networking & mentoring opportunities with upper management/Senior Sponsors+
Is this the internship opportunity for you?
  • Are you passionate about data centricity, machine learning, and gaining significant exposure to the emerging profession of data science?
  • Do you seek to work for a company committed to positively impacting the communities it serves?
  • Are you an innovative, driven worker looking to make an impact in the data science community?
If your answer to these questions is “Yes,” the Data Science Intern position is an excellent opportunity for you. By evaluating the impact of data on Serco’s business and proposing innovative data centric solutions, you will discover your place at Serco this summer and have a lasting impact on our organization and our world!

Serco offers an exciting internship opportunity for an inquisitive junior data scientist to join in the development of data-centered projects through our Executive-sponsored Data Operations program, which is responsible for leading Serco’s analytical and business intelligence projects including the company’s adoption of digital modernization strategies. In keeping with this overarching aim, the junior data scientist will be required to interpret high-level work requirements, and complete tasks within a team environment. You will harness your mastery of Data Science and consult on various aspects of data operations and other projects.

The Data Science Intern will work out of our Herndon, VA headquarters on a hybrid schedule. This is an excellent opportunity for someone who is innovative and driven by data stewardship.

At Serco, we take seriously our responsibility in creating new value in critical business processes where data is an asset. We are committed to fostering a culture where corporate values and data stewardship matter. To align with that, Serco has developed our Executive-sponsored Data Operations Program. As the Data Science Intern, you will focus your support on data analytics - evaluating, refining, and learning how to manage data-driven projects which are geared at furthering the business's interests.

Over the course of the summer, you will network and interface with critical business leaders to include our CEO and members of our Executive Management Team (EMT). You will report to Dr. Tim McGee [ LinkedIn ]. Dr. Tim McGee leads Serco’s team of data scientists and the Data Operations initiative with over 24 years of experience in the public sector.

In this role, you will:
  • Conduct ongoing business intelligence using R, Python or Java.
  • Propose solutions to enhance Serco’s data stewardship.
  • Assist in the preparation of reports, graphs, proposals, studies, and correspondence.
  • Innovate machine learning principles, exploratory data analysis, statistical inference and modeling, machine learning, and high dimensional data analysis.
  • Research industry standards for data science.
  • Share research with our North American and Global leadership.
  • Work closely with Senior Leaders and report directly to the Manager of Data Operations.
