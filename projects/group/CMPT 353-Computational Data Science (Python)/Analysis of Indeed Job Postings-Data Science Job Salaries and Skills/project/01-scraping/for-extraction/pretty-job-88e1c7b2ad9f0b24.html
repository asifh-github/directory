Mastercard's AI Operations team uses a data driven approach to detect anomalies, forecast trends, and enable AIOps for Mastercards businesses.

Interns on this team may be involved with:

  • Identifying, onboarding, and discovering data sets using Extract/Transform/Load tools
  • Leveraging big data analytics tools to identify patterns, trends, and anomalies using statistical analysis and machine learning
  • Creating reports and dashboards to visualize data to communicate findings to key stakeholders
  • Liaising with global user community to identify opportunities to improve current programs and develop understanding of customer needs

All about You

  • Currently enrolled in a bachelor’s degree with a strong focus and interest in technology (machine learning, big data, data science, automation, data analytics and visualization) with a graduation date of Fall 2023 or beyond
  • Analytical, investigative and problem-solving skills
  • Strategic thinker with ability to derive and translate data analytics to meet business goals
  • Sound written and verbal communication skills
  • Good presentation skills – ability to present complex topics to a diverse audience
  • Project management skills, highly organized with strong attention to detail
  • Must be able to work independently in developing and mapping out solutions
  • Must be able to work in a fast paced and dynamic environment, handle multiple tasks, consistently meet established deadlines, and deliver exceptional results
  • Experience and proficiency in programming and scripting in languages such as BASH scripting, Python, SQL, JavaScript
  • Experience with Tableau, Domo, Splunk, Microsoft PowerBI, Excel or other comparable business intelligence and data visualization tools a plus
  • Experience or familiarity with Statistical Analysis, Machine Learning , Big Data analysis, and RESTful API integrations a plus
