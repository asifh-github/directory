Colgate-Palmolive invites you to apply for the Spring 2023 Data Engineer Internship for the Global Information Technology (GIT) organization. This internship is paid and runs on a six-month basis, with opportunity to extend. Each Intern is assigned to a unique, global team and project upon offer working in areas related to Data Engineering including data pipelines, visualizations, ETL (extract, transform, and load), and/or data platforms.


Who are you?

  • You are a collaborator- In this role, Interns get opportunities to network with multiple cross-functional teams. These relationships thrive on mutual trust and integrity to achieve deliverables and execute against the strategy and goals.

  • You are an innovator- You look for opportunities to improve and seek creative solutions and you’re able to adapt and adjust the strategy and direction of your work if necessary.

  • You are curious- You’re curious about what works and what does not and are willing to take the initiative to find out.


Qualifications:

  • Authorized to work in the U.S. without requiring visa sponsorship now or in the future

  • Must be currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree program

  • Majoring in Computer Science, Data Science, Data Analytics, Software Development, Database Management, or related

  • A minimum GPA of 2.8

  • Ability to work in a high-pressure, fast-paced environment

  • Availability to work a minimum of 20 hours per week

  • Familiarity with data warehouse and visualization tools (Snowflake, SQL, Domo, or similar)

  • Strong knowledge of Python

  • Remote option in Eastern or Central Standard Time with preference to be hybrid in our Piscataway, NJ office

Salary Range $21.50 - $27.00 USD

Pay is based on several non discriminatory factors including but not limited to experience, education, skills and office location. In addition to your salary, Colgate-Palmolive offers a performance based bonus and competitive benefits package.
