Full Job Description

This summer internship will offer college and university students an outstanding opportunity to gain hands on experience within the pharmaceutical industry. This program allows students to work closely with business leaders while acquiring industry insight. Interns will be tasked with both individual and group projects. The Sunovion Internship Program will offer additional workshops and seminars for your career and professional development. We are seeking dedicated individuals within multiple areas across the organization.
Specific Internship Description:
This internship will be crafted with the business need and candidate interest in mind but could include any of the following:
  • Tune the speaker diarization model to enhance the diarization performance and improve the rater vs patient classification model for audio recordings from clinical interviews for clinical studies.
  • Extract the selected raw acoustic features from the patients' and raters' speech to model the distributions of selected acoustic features and benchmark against other clinical measures, gain insights into how innovative data science technologies (e.g., machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing) can be applied to large integrated clinical trial data, and/or
  • develop solutions, algorithms, share the progress and obstacles
  • train the models, develop data products
Learning Goals:
  • Gain insights into functioning of a clinical development department at mid-size pharmaceutical organization
  • Learn about clinical trials and gain insights into how data science can be applied in clinical development.
  • Opportunities to develop new methodologies or solutions to generate insights and improve business processes.
  • Learn from access to various AWS services, SAS, R, Python, and various CONDA environments with deep learning frameworks such as TF, PyTorch, MXNet, Chainer, and BI solution like Spotfire
  • Gain understanding of working in a cross functional, matrixed, and partnered organization
  • Develop soft skills like communication (oral and written) and presentation skills
  • Develop time management, project management skills and the ability to be responsible for multiple priorities
Minimum Requirements:
  • Enrolled in Postgraduate/PhD degree program in Data Science, Computer Science or related
  • Must have completed their sophomore year of study
  • Authorization to work in the U.S. without requiring sponsorship
  • Prior experience developing or tuning speaker diarization and speaker identification models is highly desirable. Experience running acoustic analysis is desirable.
  • Experience using AWS services such as Sagemaker (python or R), Lambda, S3, RDS. Knowledge of Docker.Strong communication skills and passion for continued development
