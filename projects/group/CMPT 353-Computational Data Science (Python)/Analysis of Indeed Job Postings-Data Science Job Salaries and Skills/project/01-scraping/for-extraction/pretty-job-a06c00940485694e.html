A *bout the Role

Kisi is looking for a Data Engineer to join our team to help monitor, sync, and clean all internal data. You will work closely with our data department to make sure that the information being provided to them is accurate and you will efficiently work to minimize discrepancies. This will be an operational position reporting directly to our CEO and is remote friendly ( candidates in the United States must be based in one the following states: AK, AL, CT, DE, FL, IA, KS, MA, MD, MT, NC, NH, NJ, NY, OH, OR, PA, TN, WA).

*
Responsibilities: *

  • Ensure that all company data makes sense and is filled out correctly across all internal platforms
  • Clean and update all data entering our data warehouse
  • Monitor and analyze any errors within our data and work to implement solutions to prevent any mistakes moving forward

*
Requirements: *

  • Strong analytical skills and a data driven personality
  • Experience working within Excel/Google Sheets and SQL, scripting experience
  • Interest in startups and an entrepreneurial mindset
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
