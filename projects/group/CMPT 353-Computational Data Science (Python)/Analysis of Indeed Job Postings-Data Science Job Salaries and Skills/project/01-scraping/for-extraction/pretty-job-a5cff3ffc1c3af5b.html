Mutual of America Financial Group

Job Title: Data Scientist

Location: Boca Raton, FL


Who We Are:

At Mutual of America Financial Group (“Mutual of America”), our mission is to “help people build the assets they need to achieve greater financial security.” Our mission is built upon a strong foundation of integrity, excellence, and social responsibility. Since 1945, we have specialized in providing small and mid-sized not-for-profit organizations and companies with pension– and retirement-related services, along with leading customer support. We work diligently to understand the needs of plan sponsors. We provide them with competitively priced solutions so they can help their employees achieve their financial planning goals.


We’re committed to bringing talented and motivated people together to help our customers plan for a financially secure future.


Role Summary:

Mutual of America continues a transformation journey to develop and deploy data-driven, data science enabled solutions across multiple business verticals, as it implements its Artificial Intelligence vision. Our team requires that we work across several areas, being data science, data engineering, visualizations, machine learning, deep learning NLP, MLOps, user experience, interaction with business users and integration of our solutions onto user applications, therefore it offers the opportunity to expand one’s knowledge onto all areas needed to build value for the business. The Data Scientist will report into the Vice President, Artificial Intelligence.


Responsibilities:

  • The Data Scientist will be responsible for contributing to related efforts, including data discovery, exploratory analysis, integration, transformation (wrangling, scaling, etc.), algorithm selection and experimentation, model tuning and solution deployment.
  • The Data Scientist is expected to measure model performance, improve it, and adjust the process based on feedback received.
  • The Data Scientist will be working with various teams across the enterprise, have a positive attitude, patience, and work effectively.

Required Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in data science, machine learning, or similar field
  • 2 plus years of relevant work experience
  • Proficiency in Python, SQL and MS Office Applications
  • Knowledge and experience in statistical, data mining and machine learning techniques: Regression, Random Forest, Boosting, Trees, text mining, social network analysis, clustering, etc.
  • Must be eligible to work in the United States without sponsorship

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with autoML and auto data science tools (e.g., Dataiku’s DSS is highly preferred)
  • Experience with the AWS machine learning stack and AWS data pipelines and related services (Sagemaker, EC2, S3, ElasticSearch, etc.)
  • Familiarity with deep learning packages and deep learning architectures (such as Keras, TensorFlow, PyTorch, NLP techniques, CNNs, RNNs, LSTMs, Transformers, etc.)
  • Familiarity with data visualization tools (e.g., Qlik, Tableau, PowerBi)
  • Application development (full stack, emphasis on UI)

What we offer you:

  • Competitive base salary
  • Annual Bonus
  • Comprehensive Benefits Package (medical, dental, and vision) that starts day one of employment
  • 401(k) Match: You will be eligible to receive up to 9% of your pay (salary and incentive compensation) in company contributions. A non-elective employer contribution of 3%, plus a 100% employer match on employee contributions of up to 6% of pay.
  • Parental Leave: 8 weeks fully paid
  • Paid time off: 20 days plus two floating personal holidays
  • Matching Gift Program: Mutual of America matches donations of $25 or more to your favorite organizations
