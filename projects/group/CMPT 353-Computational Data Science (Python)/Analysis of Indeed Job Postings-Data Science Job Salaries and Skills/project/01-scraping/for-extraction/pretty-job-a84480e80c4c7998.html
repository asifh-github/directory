Job Description:
SUMMARY
A brief description summarizing the overall purpose and objectives of the position and the results the worker is expected to accomplish.

The Reservoir Data Analyst Intern will perform basic analyst duties under the direction of the Director of Reservoir Engineering & Technology, primarily focused on working within large, complex engineering datasets, helping developing visualizations to display information and contributing in development of new innovative statistical methods and tools to analyze engineering data.


ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
The tasks, duties, and responsibilities of the position that are most important to get the job done.
  • Work within complex engineering dataset for use by company engineers and technicians in various technical processes;
  • Contribute in development discussions for enhancing current engineering dataset and analyst tools;
  • Begin exploring processes and tools focused on multi-variate statistical analysis inclusive of regression, probability, automation and machine learning algorithms;
  • Build out visualizations in Spotfire Analyst as requested by engineering professionals;
  • Perform and assume other duties and responsibilities as directed by the Director of Reservoir Engineering & Technology;
  • Demonstrate a commitment to safety and environmental compliance in all job aspects.

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ATTRIBUTES
The specific minimum competencies required for job performance.
  • Preferred knowledge in: Microsoft Access; Microsoft Excel; SQL coding; Spotfire; Relational database management; Oil & Gas basics;
  • Ability to perform mathematical calculations with focus on understanding statistics and probabilities;
  • Able to capture, format and organize large amounts of data;
  • General knowledge of data modeling and analysis, production of visuals (charts, graphs, etc.) and dashboards;
  • Able to work on a team and take direction from multiple sources;
  • Desire to learn and has a curious disposition;
  • Exhibits natural attention to detail and is process oriented.


WORKING CONDITIONS
The environment in which the job is performed, especially any unique conditions outside a normal office environment.
  • Consistent with that of a normal office environment;
  • Expected to work 3 days a week in office and 2 days remotely.

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS
The minimum level of education, experience, and certifications required to perform the job.
  • Enrollment in college/university, majoring in Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, Data Science or related field;
  • Ability to work minimum of 30 hours a week;
  • GPA of 3.0 or higher on 4.0 scale.
