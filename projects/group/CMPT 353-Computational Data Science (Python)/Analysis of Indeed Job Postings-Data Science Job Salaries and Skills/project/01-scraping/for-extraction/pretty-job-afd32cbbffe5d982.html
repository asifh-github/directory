Full Job Description

Summary:

The Data Scientist is responsible for the research, creation and implementation of analytical solutions designed to optimize marketing communications and/or drive marketing insights for existing clients. This individual will participate in the design and development of processes to automate analytical functions as well as contribute and assist in the creation of new products and services rooted in analytical work. The Data Scientist will consult with Client Success in the development and usage of predictive modeling products with the goal of maximizing client results. Finally, they will interpret promotional results and provide recommendations to enhance performance.


Job Responsibilities:

  • Assist in the design, development and enhancement of processes to make Wiland’s Enterprise analytical platform robust and to scale to meet company growth. This includes, but is not limited to, development of new analytic and modeling capabilities as well as consulting with internal technology personnel.
  • Assist in the evaluation of various statistical techniques including proprietary algorithms in the context of prospect and customer models.
  • Complete research and analysis to provide insights leading to new product and services.
  • Review and perform quality checks on existing data sources. Assist in leveraging existing data sources to their fullest potential for use within models and targeting decisions. This includes the generation of new information as well as the creation of models to be used as inputs in other processes.
  • Assist in evaluation of new data sources within existing processes and make recommendations for licensing.
  • Provide input to the Predictive Client Solutions Team and Client Success to assist in the fulfillment of client orders including recommendations for model prioritization and offline customized selects.
  • Present analytical results internally to the Analysis Team, Client Success and Senior Management as needed. Develop presentation skills and business knowledge to eventually deliver Client presentations and consultations


Experience, Education & Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree (or higher) in a quantitative discipline (Statistics, Mathematics, Economics or Computer Science) with strong work experience. Must be able to produce college transcripts showing minimum 3.0 GPA.
  • 5+ years’ experience with R, Python, or related statistical software package including extensive programming skills.
  • Hadoop, Spark or other big data implementation experience desirable
  • 5+ years’ experience developing successful predictive models using regression analysis, decision trees and/or neural networks in an academic setting or prior work.
  • 3+ years’ experience with databases, data access tools, and SQL.
  • 3+ years’ analytics experience in database marketing, data science, analysis and modeling.
  • 1+ years’ experience demonstrated professional presentation skills with an emphasis on effectively communicating analytical results to key stakeholders.
  • Strong interpersonal and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent written and verbal skills.
  • Understanding of business processes and the utilization of technology to drive those processes.
  • Collaborative filtering and automatic tree generation methods.
  • Clustering techniques (principal components analysis, k-means, etc.).
  • Analytical and algorithm design abilities with ordinal and nominal data.
