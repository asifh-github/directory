Job Summary

Langan is seeking a Data Scientist/Analyst Intern or Co-op to join its collaborative Information Technology team. This individual will serve a key function in performing data management and building applications. In this role, you will have the opportunity to gain practical experience working on projects for an engineering consulting firm.

Job Responsibilities

  • Collect and interpret data;
  • Manipulation and visualization of business data using data visualization techniques and tools;
  • Perform data analysis to discover trends and patterns;
  • Utilize Power Apps to help build applications;
  • Define new data collection and analysis processes; and
  • Perform other duties as requested.

Qualifications

  • Student actively pursuing a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or related field;
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0;
  • Knowledge of and interest in PowerApps and PowerBI and/or Tableau is preferred;
  • Knowledge of .NET, JavaScript, HTML, Python, and CSS is preferred;
  • Ability to provide an outstanding work product with strong attention to detail;
  • Ability to proactively organize, prioritize, and multitask effectively in a fast-paced environment;
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills; and
  • Self-motivated, adaptable, and dependable.
