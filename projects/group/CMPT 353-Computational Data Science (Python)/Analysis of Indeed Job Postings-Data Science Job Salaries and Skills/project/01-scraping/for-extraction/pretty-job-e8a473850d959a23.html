Software Engineering & Technology University Program (S.E.T.U.P)
S.E.T.U.P (Software Engineering & Technology University Program) is an 18-month entry-level technology program that is designed to develop and retain high-potential talent.
The goal of this program is to build a team of technology professionals who are prepared and positioned to contribute to BNY Mellon's technology strategy and initiatives. This program is an immense development and learning opportunity for students with a passion for Technology.
Program highlights
The S.E.T.U.P program is an opportunity not only to learn and grow, but an experience full of diverse community connections, executive engagement, and memorable events. You unlock your full potential here! How? You’re empowered to solve problems and brainstorm ideas in your own unique way.
Collaboration: BNY offers the opportunity to have a direct impact on a wide range of projects, and to create and deliver successful solutions to our clients through a collaborative team setting.
Mentorship : Support for career development and networking from an array of corporate leaders, such as executive mentors, peer mentors, and program managers.
Learning & Development: Robust onboarding and training curriculum designed specifically for S.E.T.U.P participants. Gain cross-functional skills by attending learning & development sessions designed by subject matter experts.
After successful completion of the program, high performing SETUPs are aligned to a technology team that would help them utilize and advance the skills acquired during the program.
The possibilities are limitless here - you’ll gain the skills required to advance your career and have a significant impact on our business !!!

Primary Responsibilities:
Apply scientific methods to find solutions to real business problems.
Perform data analysis, feature engineering, and advanced methods to prepare and develop decisions based on data.
Data mining using state-of-the-art methods
Provide insight into observed business outcomes through analytics.
Perform data profiling to identify and understand anomalies in data.
Automate data analysis and streamline analytical processes
Provide recommendations based on data trends uncovered when possible
Stays abreast of organization and management changes and has in-depth knowledge of company practices relevant to data science products.
Grow and develop skills across the 3 domain specialties: Machine Learning, Feature Engineering and Advanced Analytics capabilities. Stressing expertise in the core functional areas: Computer Programming, Math & Analytic Methodology, Distributed computing, and communications of complex results.
