About this Position:

The Data Science team is on the mission to design, develop, and deploy AI / ML solutions aligned with our 2030 vision where data is at the center of everything. Data generated in our organization and products are rich and diverse and driving insight from them is a very exciting and challenging task. The Data Scientist role is creating a solid data science foundation for our fast-growing area of the business, to enable the company tackle engineering and business challenges by designing analytics and AI / ML solutions. This opportunity involves defining the path to discover trends, patterns and hidden insights from data while collaborating with the most brilliant talents in the automotive industry to build products and services.

Responsibilities include:

  • Use mathematical techniques to arrive at an answer using data. Translate analysis results into business recommendations and solutions leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning models as appropriate
  • Conduct study and proof of technology on emerging advancements in data analytics
  • Provide support to lead the design and development of the Data Analytics COE center
  • Engage broadly with the organization to identify, prioritize, frame, and structure complex and ambiguous engineering challenges
  • Prepare and deliver training to analytical and data science stakeholders
  • Extract actionable insights from broad, open-ended questions
  • Partner with project team members to identify and articulate opportunities, see beyond the data to identify solutions that will raise the bar for decision making
  • Implementing data visualization best practices, governance, performance tuning, create/maintain design related artifacts to deliver business results
  • Work with various database technologies and data sources to drive actionable insight

Who we are seeking:

Required Work Experience:

  • 4-6 years’ experience in BI, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • 3+ years in scripting (ex. Python, SQL)
  • Strong knowledge of data structures (structured/unstructured) and data modelling

Required Education:

  • BS in Technical discipline such as Computer Science, Physics, Mathematics, and Engineering

Desired skills:

  • Skilled in the use of analytic tools for discerning patterns in data. Also, able to use one or more tools for operating on large data sets, such as Python, R, and SQL
  • Experience creating and using advanced machine learning algorithms and statistics: regression, simulation, scenario analysis, modelling, clustering, decision trees, neural networks, etc.
  • Data science tools such as Open Data Hub (Seldon, Kafka, Prometheus, Dataiku etc.)
  • Strong experience in ML Ops (ex. GitHub) and Agile methodologies
  • Deep learning - machine learning that is a neural network with three or more layers, which helps to “learn” from large amounts of data
  • Proficient understanding of data models, data marts, and data concepts
  • Experience in data analysis, wrangling, validation, and data cleansing.
  • Cloud/big data tools (ex. blob storage, Redshift, Snowflake, Kafka, Hadoop, Spark, Hive etc.)
