Location – Denver, CO

At Modivcare, our people are our greatest asset and integral to the services we provide. We are recruiting the next generation of leaders to help us build an exceptional experience for yourself and build a better Modivcare together. Our internship program is designed to give you a solid foundation of skills based on your interest to develop your career as a future leader.

Modivcare is looking for a diligent and energetic Data Scientist Intern to support the Corporate Insights & Analytics team with projects and tasks that support the company's continued growth and operations. You will engage in mentorship and networking opportunities with leaders across the organization, and you will obtain experience and insight into our Corporate Insights & Analytics operations.

You will report to the Director of Data Insights & Analytics. The internship is full-time, approximately 10 weeks and begins Summer 2023.

You will...

  • Support the Data Insights & Analytics team with day-to-day operations
  • Work collaboratively with various departments across the organization
  • Use statistical analysis, supervised & unsupervised learning and exploratory data analysis to help uncover insights to relevant business problems
  • Gain hands-on technical experience with Data Science techniques, programming skills, and the use of relational data bases
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand and address relevant business problems
  • Perform other duties as assigned

We are excited to speak to someone with the following…

  • High School Diploma or GED
  • Enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's degree program in Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, Physics, Chemistry, Biostatistics, Computer Science, Economics or related degree program
  • Willingness to tackle new projects and provide feedback
  • Open to feedback and investing in your own continuous improvement
  • Alignment with Modivcare's values of Respect, Trust, Reliable, Compassion, Safety and Transparency
  • Proven leadership history in a club or organization
  • Affiliation with organization committed to diversity and inclusion

Modivcare. Because we care...always.

Modivcare Inc. ("Modivcare") (Nasdaq: MODV) is a healthcare services company that provides a suite of integrated supportive care solutions for public and private payors and their patients. Our value-based solutions address the social determinants of health (SDoH), enable greater access to care, reduce costs, and improve outcomes. We are a leading provider of non-emergency medical transportation (NEMT), personal and home care, and nutritional meal delivery. Modivcare also holds a minority equity interest in CCHN Group Holdings, Inc. and its subsidiaries ("Matrix Medical Network"), which partners with leading health plans and providers nationally, delivering a broad array of assessment and care management services to individuals that improve health outcomes and health plan financial performance.
