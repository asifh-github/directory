About Dexcom:

Dexcom, Inc. empowers people to take control of diabetes through innovative continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems. Headquartered in San Diego, California, Dexcom has emerged as a leader of diabetes care technology. By listening to the needs of users, caregivers, and providers, Dexcom simplifies and improves diabetes management around the world.

Throughout the pandemic, one of our key priorities has been to keep employees as safe as possible. At this time, we plan for most of our summer internships to be virtual, with exceptions of essential groups. We ask for our intern candidates to be flexible to a virtual format or residence in the location of the position throughout the duration of the internship.

Summary:

You will be part of the team that supports enhanced continuous health sensing design by applying analytics to clinical datasets to generate insights. You will process and analyze clinical datasets of sensor time series data and report on system performance in our clinical trial participants. Your contributions will drive continuous improvement in current health sensing systems and drive design of next-generation health sensing systems. You will process prepare periodic reports, create and maintain analytical tools, and make presentations to a large cross-functional audience.

Your contributions will aid in Dexcom’s mission to ‘empower people to take control of diabetes’ .

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Support ad-hoc requests by running analyses and present results to stakeholders
  • Create, maintain, and support metrics, reports, analyses, and dashboards with datasets of various sizes and file formats
  • Develop ability to troubleshoot anomalies in datasets
  • Create and maintain documentation of work processes
  • Recognize and adopt best practices in reporting and analysis: data integrity, test design, analysis, validation, and documentation
  • Perform other related duties and assignments as required
  • Comply with all company processes and policies

Basic Qualifications:

  • General understanding of statistical analysis, time series, signal processing, and data mining techniques
  • Strong knowledge of statistical programming languages in MATLAB
  • Knowledge and direct experience handling datasets of various sizes and degrees of cleanliness
  • Excellent communication (verbal and written) and interpersonal skills with an ability to effectively communicate with both business and technical teams
