Full Job Description

The Role:

The Data Analyst Intern will process, analyze, interpret, and distribute statistical related data and information from a wide variety of sources to both our internal and external customers.

What you’ll do:

The Data Analyst Intern will use various techniques e.g. statistical analysis, data mining, explanatory and predictive modeling.

Essential Functions:

The Data Analyst Intern will help perform the following tasks:

  • Shadow team members to learn and assist with data modeling and analytics.
  • Convert old queries and/or reports to modern data stack equivalents.
  • Create or update documentation.
  • Attend meetings and take notes as needed.


Who you are:

  • Experience or current studies majoring in a relevant area, such as Data Analysis, Data Science, or Data Engineering.
  • General knowledge of quantitative analytic techniques.
  • Understanding of engineering disciplines
  • Experience in software programing
  • Knowledge of SQL Server, Power BI and Synapse
  • Knowledge of extended Microsoft tools and SmartSheet
  • Healthcare and/or pharmacy experience a plus


What are the perks?

  • Shadowing, mentoring, and training opportunities with our experienced team of system design professionals
  • Hands-on application of system design principles in a fast-paced work environment
  • Opportunity to attend weekly meetings and occasional networking events
  • Compensation available
