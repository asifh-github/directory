Data Science Intern Summary:
The Data Science Intern will perform advanced quantitative analysis to:

  • Identify impacts and to formulate ideas, options, and recommended actions.
  • Utilize extensive knowledge of and experience with programming languages, technologies, and techniques to build models to advise stakeholders of findings.
  • Drive more informed business decisions and incorporate the use of data to effectively communicate complex technical and analytical business concepts.
  • Identify potential sources of data, extracts, and prepares data to ensure the most complete, highest quality data is used.

  • Assists Data Science team with work assigned to them. This often includes, but is not limited to, project work and coverage of day-to-day activities.
  • Conducts research and/or other work for projects assigned.
  • Develops business acumen through interactions and shadowing with other professionals.
  • Attends business unit meetings as assigned.
  • Creates and delivers presentations.
  • Actively participates in summer internship program events including Lunch & Learns.
