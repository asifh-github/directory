Full Job Description

Direct Supply is building the future of healthcare technology with industry-leading products, solutions and platforms to help improve the lives of millions of seniors and those who care for them.
As a Data Science Intern, you’ll develop data pipelines, statistical and ML models, visualizations, dashboards, applications and reports to deliver on business initiatives. As a member of one of our Data and Analytics teams, you’ll have the opportunity to learn from experienced developers and engineers while working with advanced technologies like cloud computing and machine learning.
This internship is year-round, accommodating a part-time schedule during the school year and full-time during breaks and summer. This position may also lead to full-time employment upon graduation for a superior performer.

What You’ll Do and Impact:
  • Design and build visualizations and models for current business projects.
  • Work in an agile team environment to build tools to support mission critical Data and Analytics Products.
  • Research new technologies to find innovative solutions for business problems.

What You’ll Need:
  • Enrolled in a Bachelor’s degree in Economics, Mathematics, Computer Engineering, Computer Science or Software Engineering, preferably as a freshman or sophomore
  • Demonstrated basic software development skills
  • Ability to work 20 hours during the school year and full time during the summer
