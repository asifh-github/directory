Full Job Description

Are you passionate about exploring where, how, and why data can drive business decisions? Interested in how organizations use analytics and data to innovate, grow and transform? Driven to help establish, define, and evangelize how data science can help drive a strategy? Tokio Marine HCC has an exciting opportunity for a Data Science Intern for our corporate office in Houston, TX.

The Opportunity:

  • Research and develop statistical learning models for data analysis
  • Collaborate with our actuarial department, stakeholders, and our Chief Actuary to devise innovative and forward-thinking solutions for our clients’ needs
  • Keep up-to-date with the latest technology trends • Communicate results and ideas to key stakeholders
  • Implement new statistical or other mathematical methodologies as needed for specific models or analysis

Requirements:

  • Currently pursuing a Masters degree in Computer Science, Applied Statistics, Applied math, Data Science, or a related field (Strong students with Bachelors degrees will be considered as well.
  • Practical experience with multiple statistical languages (SAS, R, Pandas), data processing, database programming, and data analytics
  • Solid background in data mining and statistical analysis
  • Experience with programming languages such as Python and R
