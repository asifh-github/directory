Department Overview A laboratory organization responsible for the testing and certification of wireless communication devices.
Job Description
This is a mixed internship role in support of the test labs and engineering data analysis. The candidate intern will spend 5050 time split between these two functions. In the labs role, the candidate will contribute to the reliability testing of portable and mobile twoway communication products, and improve the testability of current generation devices. Utilize best in class test tools to verify operations, functionality, specification, and durability. In the data analysis role, the intern would work with the engineering teams in developing a data portal to collect, process, and analyze data collected during the product development cycle.
