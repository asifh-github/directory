Are you a problem solver, explorer, and knowledge seeker – always asking, “What if?”

If so, then you may be the new team member we’re looking for. Because at SAS, your curiosity matters – whether you’re developing algorithms, creating customer experiences or answering critical questions. Curiosity is our code, and the opportunities here are endless.

What we do
We’re the leader in analytics. Through our software and services, we inspire customers around the world to transform data into intelligence. Our curiosity fuels innovation, pushing boundaries, challenging the status quo and changing the way we live.

What you’ll do
We’re looking for a Data Scientist to contribute to the research and development of innovative, market-centric products.

You will:
  • Analyze customer data and build reliable and scalable cloud-based SaaS system for solving high value business problems relevant to revenue and profit optimization.
  • Transfer client business requirements into a tangible product solution that addresses the specific customer requirements to ensure highest quality products and positively affect sales and client retention.
  • Create and deploy new intellectual property to innovatively fulfill the needs of customers.
  • Develop high-quality analytical algorithms (i.e. using machine learning, predictive analytics) with responsibility for research, programming, testing, and documenting.

What we’re looking for
  • Masters or higher degree in Econometrics, Economics, Statistics, Biostatistics, Applied Mathematics, or a related quantitative field.
  • Strong analytic programming ability with minimum of three years of software development experience in one or more programming language (C/C++, Python, JAVA).
  • Knowledge of advanced statistical concepts, techniques, and appropriate use for techniques such as regression, properties of distribution and statistical tests.
  • Knowledge of a variety of machine learning techniques (clustering, decision tree learning, artificial neural networks, etc.) and their real-world advantages/drawbacks.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to resolve problems independently, escalates appropriately and multitask.
  • You’re curious, passionate, authentic and accountable. These are our values and influence everything we do.

The nice to haves ( optional)
  • Ph.D. in Econometrics, Economics, Statistics, Biostatistics, Applied Mathematics, or a related quantitative field.
  • Experience in analytic software solution development.
  • Good understandings of containers, Kubernetes, and cloud-computing.
  • Experience in developing distributed, multi-threaded computing systems.

Why SAS
  • We love living the #SASlife and believe that happy, healthy people have a passion for life, and bring that energy to work. No matter what your specialty or where you are in the world, your unique contributions will make a difference.
  • Our multi-dimensional culture blends our different backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives. Here, it isn’t about fitting into our culture, it’s about adding to it - and we can’t wait to see what you’ll bring.
