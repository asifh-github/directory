About the Position

The Golden State Warriors are looking for a Data Scientist to join the growing Business Strategy and Analytics team. In this role, you will help the organization achieve our mission of becoming a global leader in experiences and entertainment, by working collaboratively with organizational leaders and a team of data analysts. This position reports to the Director, Business Strategy and Analytics.

Come share your expertise while learning more about this growing sports and entertainment organization that values your passion and initiative!

This is a full-time position based onsite in San Francisco, CA.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop, deploy and monitor predictive models
  • Build features for predictive models
  • Partner with data analysts to integrate the results into decision making
  • Analyze large data sets to discover trends and patterns
  • Maintain a pulse on the current state of data science and analytics

Required Experience & Skills

  • Bachelor's degree, preferably in Computer Science or related technical field, or equivalent work experience
  • Minimum 4 years working on data team
  • Expertise with scripting languages such as Python or R
  • Advanced knowledge of and experience applying data science to solve business problems, such as predictive modeling, machine learning,
  • Experience with SQL and relational databases
  • Experience working in cloud environments (e.g. GCP)
  • Experience writing scalable ETL/ELT
  • Experience operating predictive models in production
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite
  • Ability to build positive working relationships with business partners and peers at all levels within the organization
  • Excellent written and verbal communication; strong presentation skills with the ability to influence technical and non-technical partners
  • Ability to balance multiple projects at once in a fast-paced work environment
  • Passion and curiosity for the sports and entertainment industry
