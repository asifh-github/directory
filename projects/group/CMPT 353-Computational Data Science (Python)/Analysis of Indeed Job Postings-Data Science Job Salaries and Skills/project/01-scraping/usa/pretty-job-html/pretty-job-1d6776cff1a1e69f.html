As a Data Scientist/Analyst, you will be responsible for the analysis, management, and manipulation of structured data while reviewing trend analysis, performing normalization operations and assuring data quality.

You will use business intelligence, query, analytic and statistical software to build solutions, perform root cause analysis and interpret data. You will work with descriptive and regression-based analytics and apply data-model enforced solutions on a large scale to achieve business critical accuracy in support of industry regulation standards.

The Data Scientist/Analyst will be responsible for collecting, preparing, analyzing, and acting upon data to ensure data integrity. You will partner with analytics consultants and business operations teams to perform your duties. This role will give you the opportunity to use your analytical knowledge and skill to derive insights which will drive actions and generate value from that intelligence by initiating new analytic solutions and by providing input to operations processes.

You’ll enjoy the flexibility to work remotely* from anywhere within the U.S. as you take on some tough challenges.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Daily administration and proactive monitoring of business-critical data in Salesforce and other related integrated databases
  • Manage process queues and mitigate any exception output within expected SLAs
  • Collaborate and communicate with business partners
  • Manage the quality of data processed through customer submitted data sources
  • Perform accuracy checks and resolve exceptions produced by submission processes
  • Leverage customized reports and dashboards using the base tools provided by the Analytics team
  • Perform proactive data cleansing, merging, de-duplication activities
  • Leverage Salesforce platform, Snowflake, ETL, and Einstein analytics tools
  • Manage and update multiple databases for consistency
  • Research and resolve data anomalies associated with regulated data
  • Create data dictionaries to document data types, associated business rules, and remediations paths
  • Test and correct errors, and refine changes to database
  • Perform end to end diagnostic analytics to identify data anomalies to be resolved and fixed at source
  • Assist in development of tools to measure provider directory elements compared to Salesforce to ensure accuracy and alignment
  • Answer user questions
  • Confers with co-workers to determine impact of database changes on other systems


You’ll be rewarded and recognized for your performance in an environment that will challenge you and give you clear direction on what it takes to succeed in your role as well as provide development for other roles you may be interested in.

Required Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of experience in manipulating large data sets while understanding both technical and business impacts (demographics, complex data models, multi-relational data)
  • 3+ years of SQL or equivalent data capture experience
  • 1+ years of Python experience (including data focused libraries such as pandas)
  • Experience working in multiple data platforms
  • Proficiency with MS Office suite

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of experience in health care data analysis
  • Proficiency using Salesforce or other CRMs
  • Salesforce Admin experience
  • Snowflake Experience
  • Computer programming basics to interpret and understand logic/code behind

Soft Skill Match:

  • Analytical and problem-solving skills, with attention to detail
  • Demonstrated written and verbal communication skills
  • Demonstrated self-motivation and initiative to excel in a fast-paced environment & pivot priorities as business needs shift
  • Ability to collaborate with others across multiple teams
  • Demonstrated ability to collaborate with end users in various departments
