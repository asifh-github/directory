Job Description:

At Caterpillar, we work to help our customers build a better world. Without our team of talented, bright and driven individuals, we wouldn’t be the Caterpillar we are today. Now, this is your chance to join our team and do work that matters. We want you to help us enable customer success, make progress possible around the world and help our communities grow and thrive. We are looking for a dynamic, organized self-starter to join our Product Support and Logistics Division as a Data Scientist II .

The Product Support and Logistics Division (PSLD) is one of the most innovative divisions within Caterpillar. In PSLD, as within all of Caterpillar, our priority is our customers and helping them build a better world. We touch nearly every element of the services value stream from procurement to design and manufacturing to distribution. PSLD is responsible for Aftermarket Parts Distribution of Cat Parts, Integrated Logistics, segment Procurement and the design and manufacture of Wear Components (Undercarriage and GET) and Maintenance Components (Seals, Tubes & Hardware, Hose & Couplings, Fluids and Filters).

We’re committed to our customers, who build a better world with our products, services and solutions. We understand and show the value of why they should always choose us. We’re exploring new ideas and opportunities – innovating to discover the breakthroughs necessary for tomorrow’s growth.

Through it all, we are one team – creating and delivering world-class components and solutions superior to the competition.

Learn more about the Caterpillar Experience .

The principal responsibility of the Data Scientist is to support Wear Components by analyzing and validating data with the purpose of driving insights and improvement opportunities for product group alignment and identifying opportunities for growth of advanced improvements. This person would work closely with Caterpillar teams to ensure more accurate data and improved/automated authoring of data.

Job Duties/Responsibilities may include, but are not limited to:

  • Gather, develop, document business procedures and guidelines for defining data.

  • Develop and deliver training guidelines for product group personnel to be fully effective in both existing and future systems, visualizations, and applications.

  • Lead transformation and improvement projects of functional support processes and maintaining high visibility to the customers’ goals to consult on process options and head off potential data and reporting problems.

  • Provide team support for implemented solutions and analysis.

  • Analyze large quantities of data from various data sets, recommend solutions, make timely decisions, and communicate effectively with internal teams.

Basic Qualifications:

  • A four-year degree (Bachelors) from an accredited college or university

  • 3+ years of professional experience in Data Analytics, Power BI and SQL Concepts

  • Prior experience in designing and developer software applications in an Object-Oriented Programming language such as Java, Python, or C++

  • Experience building and maintaining ETL data pipelines and workflows such as Tidal and Snaplogic

  • Prior experience with deploying and maintaining software using public clouds such as AWS or Azure

  • Prior experience in Snowflake

Top Candidates Will Also Have:

  • Ability to work on increasingly more complex assignments and will have demonstrated strong leadership, initiative, interpersonal skills, and the ability to communicate effectively

  • Experiences with different types of database tools such as DB2 and Snowflake

  • Ability to work independently

  • Must have ability to communicate effectively with team members and others in the work group, as well as other internal partners

  • Iterative, agile development style to support adapting business requirements
