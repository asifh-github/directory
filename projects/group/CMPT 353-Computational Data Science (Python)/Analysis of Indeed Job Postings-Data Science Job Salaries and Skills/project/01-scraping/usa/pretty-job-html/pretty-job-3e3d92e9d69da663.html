Overview:
Bechtel Plant Machinery, Inc. (BPMI) is seeking a data engineer for its Monroeville, PA location, east of Pittsburgh. This position is open to recent graduates as well as experienced candidates.

Bechtel Plant Machinery, Inc. (BPMI) is a project engineering organization supporting the Naval Nuclear Propulsion Program. BPMI provides design, manufacturing, and supply chain management of the fabrication, testing, delivery, installation, and field support of nuclear power plant components installed in submarines and aircraft carriers. In addition, BPMI provides upgraded replacement components in support of refueling and overhaul activities, and aids in the ultimate storage and disposition of spent naval fuel. BPMI also delivers information technology and operational solutions to the Nuclear Navy, enabling them to fulfill their critical mission.
Responsibilities:
  • This position is responsible for leading data analytic projects in the lines of business with an emphasis on predictive analysis through use of data science techniques.
  • This position will also support the development of data structures (data lakes, pipelines, warehouses, marts) for existing and future data for use with both technical (analyzing everything from system development concepts through the end-of-life system support) and business (business and logistics data analysis) data sets.
  • Specific job responsibilities include interfacing with data owners to scope problems and consult on solutions; building data infrastructure; helping grow a culture focused on data analytics; staying current on data engineering trends and techniques; extensive communications with internal and external stakeholders.
Qualifications:
  • B.S. or M.S. degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Engineering or related field from an accredited college or university. Previous experience as an engineer in an industrial or manufacturing organization is a plus.
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 is preferred for newer college graduates.
  • Must be a U.S. citizen due to government security clearance requirements. In order to work at BPMI, applicants must complete a Federal background investigation and be granted a security clearance.
  • Experience using SQL Server Tools (SSIS, SSAS, SQL Server Management Studio).
  • Knowledge of data science techniques specifically related to machine learning and automation.
  • A proven track record of effective written and verbal communication skills is preferred.
  • Creative problem-solving capabilities are also preferred.
  • Experience with data lakes, cloud-based data warehousing tools, data factory, Databricks, and Oracle are a plus.
