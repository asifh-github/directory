Data Scientist I

NoneNoneNone

Data Scientists are responsible for making value out of data. This role will proactively obtain information from various sources and analyze it for better understanding about how the business performs, and to build AI tools that automate certain processes within the company.

The duties of Data Scientist positions within the Data Analytics group include creating various machine learning-based tools or processes within NYPA, such as recommendation engines or automated lead scoring systems. The positions within this group also have a wide range of technical competencies including: statistics and machine learning, coding languages, databases, machine learning, and reporting technologies. #LI-AE1

Responsibilities

  • Undertake programming, data analysis, and testing tasks, working within a team.
  • Support application software by troubleshooting problems, making suggestions to resolve concerns and implementing changes to address issues.
  • Assist in the design, specification, implementation and maintenance of systems applications.
  • Close supervision in the directing, preparing, coding, testing and debugging of computer programs.
  • Assist in the discussion and preparation of solutions to the day-to-day user queries.
  • Ensure adherence to business policies and procedures.
  • Assist with development by gathering information, performing analysis, and eliciting the details of user requirements in discussion with users enhancements.

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

  • General Information Technology (IT) knowledge, and a basic understanding of the utility industry.
  • General understanding of project management methodology and project work plans
  • Ability to meet deadlines set by leadership
  • Strong written communications skills with the ability to document facts, summarize analysis findings and compile weekly and monthly reports

Education, Experience and Certifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree in computer science, engineering, or related field or equivalent work experience
  • Internship experience preferred

Physical Requirements
