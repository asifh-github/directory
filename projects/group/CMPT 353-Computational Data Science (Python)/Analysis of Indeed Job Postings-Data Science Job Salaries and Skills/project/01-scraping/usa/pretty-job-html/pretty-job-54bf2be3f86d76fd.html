Position Summary: *
This position will report to the SVP, Statistical Modeling and Chief Economist and will be responsible for complex data modeling, performance analysis, and reporting. You will be a part of our Modeling Team. This role will require mentoring fellow team members, working cross-functionally with other teams, and working on independent projects. You will be responsible to create predictive models from large datasets to support areas such as accurate property valuation, photo services and scoring, and real estate market forecasting.

*
Principal Responsibilities: *

  • Work on continually enhancing our property valuation models and forecast models
  • Create complex models used for feature identification and classification with our Computer Vision processes and other products
  • Full hands-on experience with evaluating, testing, and coding data science algorithms. Work with the teams who cleanse the data and assess the performance of algorithms
  • Design, develop, train and validate machine learning models using Spark ML, Python, or Snowpark
  • Design and implement Deep neural network models using Tensorflow, Keras, or PyTorch
  • Responsible for selecting the appropriate modeling strategies to meet defined business objectives and in making key business decisions.
  • Mentor and lead the development of team members. Set long-term strategy and growth objectives for the team.
  • Manage issues that arise by leading troubleshooting efforts, providing technical support, creating a timely solution, and communicating results to internal and external stakeholders
  • Maintain expertise with respect to all data sources
  • Partner across the organization with key stakeholders in Database, Modeling, Product Development, and QA to drive optimal results
  • Assist in product requirements definition, planning, and design of new features and enhancements which may include business requirements and functional specifications documentation

*
Qualifications and Requirements: *

  • B.S. or M.S. in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, or Mathematics (or equivalent) with a GPA of 3.5 or greater
  • 5 to 8+ years of relevant total work experience (knowledge of Housing or Real Estate data and modeling is a plus)
  • 2 to 3+ years of managing or leading data science teams or projects and mentoring team members
  • Strong command of Predictive Analytics, Data Mining, Machine Learning Algorithms, Multivariate Statistics, and Forecasting
  • You have strong machine learning and deep learning background and are passionate about transforming data into ML models using Big Data
  • Knowledge of Statistical Modeling including Regression Analysis, Classification/Discriminant Analysis, and Time Series
  • Strong Analytics and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to critically evaluate Build vs. Buy decisions
  • Experience analyzing and working with RDBMS systems (Oracle/MySQL/NoSQL)
  • Familiarity with APIs and microservices
  • Familiarity with cloud-based data platforms and associated tools for data access and analysis (Snowflake, S3, Redshift, AWS Lambda, Sagemaker, etc.)
  • Ability to optimize code to run on GPUs
  • Proficient in R or SAS. Knowledge of Python is a must.
  • Understanding and experience working with cloud infrastructure services like Azure, Google Cloud, or Amazon Web Services
  • Ability to setup the deployment of models using cloud services a plus
  • Experience working with different query languages (i.e. Spark-SQL, T-SQL)
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills with ability to translate complex findings into clear communications for a variety of audiences
  • Experience at delivering results in a timely manner
  • At ease in situations with limited direction with high level goals and objectives
  • Bias toward action
  • Attention to detail is critical to success
  • Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
