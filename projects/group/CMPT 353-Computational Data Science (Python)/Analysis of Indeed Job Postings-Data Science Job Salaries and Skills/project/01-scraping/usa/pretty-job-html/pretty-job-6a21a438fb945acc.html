Full Job Description

Summit Technologies, Inc. is seeking a Data Scientist for a newly awarded 5-year contract. In this position you will turn complex data sets into useful information to solve global challenges. You will focus on the development of Analytics, Knowledge Management artifacts, Information Sharing tools, Data Transformation, and Malware Analysis. This position is located in Fort Meade, MD and requires a TS/SCI security clearance w/ Poly.

Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Devise strategies for extracting meaning and value from large datasets.
  • Use analytic modeling, statistical analysis, programming (coding), or another appropriate scientific method to develop and implement methods for exploring and assessing large datasets, in various states of organization, cleanliness, and structure that account for the unique features and limitations inherent to key intelligence community data holdings.
  • Employ some combination of the following areas:
  • Foundations (Mathematical, Computational, Statistical)
  • Data Processing (Data management and curation, data description and visualization, workflow and reproducibility)
  • Modeling, Inference, and Prediction (Data modeling and assessment, domain-specific considerations)
  • Translate practical mission needs and analytic questions related to large datasets into technical requirements.
  • Work with project teams to support ongoing intelligence focused projects.
  • Develop, code, program software to support ongoing projects with minimal supervision.
  • Communicate principled conclusions gained from data using elements of mathematics, statistics, computer science, and application specific knowledge.
  • Ask questions and communicate issues clearly to peers and management.
  • Effectively communicate complex technical information to non-technical audiences.
  • Make informed recommendations regarding competing technical solutions.
Required Experience and Skills:
  • Experience designing and implementing machine learning, data science, advanced analytical algorithms.
  • Experience performing statistical analysis (variability, sampling error, inference, hypothesis testing, EDA, application of linear models).
  • College-level Algebra or other math courses intended to meet a basic college level requirement, or upper-level math courses designated as elementary or basic do not count.
  • Experience in data management (e.g., data cleaning and transformation) including data mining, data modeling and assessment.
  • Experience with artificial intelligence/machine learning including software development (coding) and operating AI/ML systems and tools.
  • Experience with at least one of the following computer languages: Python, Java, R, C, C++ or any other one that would be relevant.
  • Experience designing/developing/using/evaluating mathematic models, methods, or techniques (e.g., algorithm development) is a plus.
  • Ability to understand and resolve issues.
Education:
  • Bachelors degree in Math, Science, Engineering or a technical field and 3 years of relevant experience; Or
  • Associate degree plus 5 years of relevant experience.
  • Note: A broader range of degrees will be considered if accompanied by a Certificate in Data Science from an accredited university.
Security Clearance:
  • TS/SCI security clearance w/ Poly
