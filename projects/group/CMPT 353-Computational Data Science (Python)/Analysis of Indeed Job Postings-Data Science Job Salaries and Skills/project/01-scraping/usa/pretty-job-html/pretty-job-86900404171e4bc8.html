Job Title
Sr. data scientist
Brighterion, a Mastercard company, was founded in 2000 and acquired by Mastercard in 2017. We deliver a leading artificial intelligence and machine learning platform that provides real-time mission critical intelligence from any data source, regardless of type, complexity or volume. Our AI solution secures billions of transactions monthly and is used and trusted by many of the world’s leading organizations and governments in payments, compliance, financial markets, security and defense, healthcare, Internet of Things, marketing and more. Currently we serve 74/100 of the largest U.S. banks and more than 2,000 customers worldwide, processing more than 100 billion transactions annually.

You will be joining a team of Data Scientists and Engineers working on innovative A.I. and M.L. models and engaging in fast execution projects. We are pursuing a highly motivated individual with strong problem solving skills to take on the challenge of structuring and engineering data and cutting-edge A.I. model development processes and technologies.
Job Description
Job Description
Role

We are currently seeking a talented and passionate data scientist to join our growing team. You will turn product ideas into production A.I model. In this role, you will lead projects and design new or improve existing AI models with an understanding of business and technical issues. Also, you will have opportunity to work cross-functionally with development, QA and business partners to tackle variety of challenges.

  • Support and improve current deployed AI models.
  • Design and implement data driven AI models for different use cases.
  • Research alternative techniques and technologies. If not available in the existing framework, design and implement an approach in Java or Python.
  • Communicate results to the customer and provide recommendations
  • Research best configuration parameters of the new AI and ML technologies to provide the optimal outcome for a given vertical.
  • Establish metrics that measure the output of the model in ways that are familiar to the customers

All about You/Essential Skills:

  • Strong Mathematics and Statistics background
  • Strong experience in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Proficient in Python. Familiarity with algorithms, data wrangling tools (pandas, dask, etc.), plotting libraries, machine learning libraries (scikit-learn). Must be able to automate tasks using Python and any open-source tools at scale. Familiarity with any deep learning framework (TensorFlow, Keras, or PyTorch) is a huge plus.
  • Strong experience with SQL language and large data manipulation
  • Problem solver
  • Team Player, sense of urgency and detail oriented
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills.
  • Proficient in Excel, Word, PowerPoint.
  • Prior experience in customer-facing engagements is a plus
  • BS in computer science or a related field.
Preferred skills:
  • MS and 3+ years of experience in AI related field
  • Prior experience in customer-facing engagements is a plus
