Algorithm/Data Scientist

Who We Are

Biolinq is a venture-backed clinical stage medical device company developing a wearable biosensor platform that measures biomarkers important to the management of many clinical conditions including diabetes. Our products are reliable, affordable and designed with a core focus on user experience. Our team is driven to develop products that will change the way patients manage their health. We are dedicated to the personal and professional growth of all our team members and strive to create a positive and creative work environment.

The Opportunity

We have multiple openings within R&D for designing and developing health information and sensor algorithms for novel intradermal biosensor technology. These are highly visible roles within the Company and candidates will work as a members of technology development team and collaborate and communicate with other stakeholders including firmware/hardware, sensor/membrane, mechanical engineering, manufacturing, and clinical affairs to facilitate monitoring of key metabolic biomarkers.

A competitive compensation package (salary + equity) commensurate with experience is offered in conjunction with the opportunity to support the Company’s efforts in developing its first product line. You will have the unique opportunity to work in an exciting startup environment and contribute to the Company’s goals and objectives.

WHAT YOU WILL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR

  • Develop signal and data processing algorithms for continuous sensing of metabolic health parameters including but not limited to glucose
  • Develop tools for leveraging bench and clinical data to develop novel insights based on sensor data and implementation of these insights into algorithm parameters
  • Develop early-stage analyses to support algorithm optimization and facilitate assessment of the performance of Biolinq sensors and help with root-cause analyses
  • Run simulations and analyses for predicting sensor performance
  • Processing large datasets using state of the art methods
  • Making presentations and writing reports for business and scientific audience

EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE AND KEY SKILLS

  • Master’s degree in a technical field (Science, Technology, Engineering, or mathematics) with a minimum of seven years of experience required (PhD preferred)
  • Scientific or Engineering discipline required
  • Minimum of 5 years’ experience in signal processing, time series data analysis and/or algorithm development, preferably in a medical device setting
  • Experience in glucose sensing is preferred
  • Experience with advanced data analysis tools and methods; demonstrated understanding of statistics and statistical analysis of data
  • Experience with advanced computing languages such as Python, R, or Matlab
  • Experience with machine learning and data mining methods
  • Ability to interface with both clinicians and R&D team and work in a fast-paced, multi-disciplinary environment
