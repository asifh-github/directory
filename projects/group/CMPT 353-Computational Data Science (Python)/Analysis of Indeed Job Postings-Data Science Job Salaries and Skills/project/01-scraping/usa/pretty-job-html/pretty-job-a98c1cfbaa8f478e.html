ABOUT MIM

MIM is a New York based institution that offers management and technology courses since 1984. Given the growing demand for skilled data scientists, MIM is looking for experienced and motivated instructors for its Data Science Bootcamp. This program will allow our graduates to enhance their data analytics and technology skills hands-on by working with data, writing code and building machine learning models. It will help them find many new career opportunities for make career changes.

Role Description

The part-time (15-20 h per week) Data Science Instructor will share his expertise in Python, R, SQL, or Data and Software Engineering via both online and on-campus lectures. He/she will also partner with other instructors to deliver best-in-class Data Science education to students. The MIM data science instructor will empower students with the skills to become Data Analysts and Data Scientists and at the same time supplement his/her income
