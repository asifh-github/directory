The Boeing Defense Space & Security (BDS) organization is seeking a highly-skilled, innovative Senior Data Scientist to work remotely for our industry-leading Enterprise Insider Threat Program group based in Annapolis Junction, MD.

In this role, you will work closely with a skilled team of analysts, software engineers, data engineers, and investigators. You will design, develop, test, deploy, and maintain new and existing, innovative machine learning and analytical capabilities and products to support the mission of The Boeing Company’s Insider Threat Program, and to identify, detect, assess, investigate, address, and mitigate potential insider threats.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Employ extensive knowledge in machine learning algorithms including: data mining , inferential statistics , mathematical optimization, natural language processing ( NLP ), text mining , visual analytics , and graph databases.
  • Work independently to learn the mathematical and statistical approaches and models already employed by the Program, and create and adapt new models to support their mission.
  • Maintain ability to design and create new algorithms for the analysis of data to identify potential threats.
  • Exhibit proficiency in evaluating the performance of existing algorithms, and make improvements to enhance the fidelity and accuracy of them.
  • Thoroughly assess business requirements to select appropriate algorithms and build descriptive statistical and mathematical models to solve analytical problems using insider threat data in support of mission.
  • Evaluate and validate correctness of complex models and analysis; assess and confirms performance of models and analytical products; and ensure that models meet business objectives.
  • Prepare data for analysis (e.g. cleansing , conditioning , transforming , filling in missing fields, identifying new feature variables and handling multi-variate data)appropriate for the volume of structured and unstructured data; identify potential trouble areas and suitability of data and provide mitigation recommendations.
  • Perform research into the application of new methods for identifying insider threat patters.
  • Interpret the outputs of complex data analysis to translate into concrete results and visualize trends and patterns.
  • Understand the insider threat mission in order to set goals that align with the Program’s mission (an understanding of cognitive learning and human behavior is a plus).
  • Engage with other data scientists in the field and stay current on published works on data analytics including predictive .

This position is identified as a virtual opportunity (work remote) and does not require you to live in Annapolis Junction, MD.

This position requires an active U.S. Secret Security Clearance (U.S. Citizenship Required). (A U.S. Security Clearance that has been active in the past 24 months is considered active.)

Basic Qualifications (Required Skills/Experience):

  • 1+ years of experience developing and maintaining relationships and partnerships with customers, stakeholders, peers, and partners.
  • Experience with analytical programming languages, techniques, and tools including R ( RStudio , shiny , devtools, package development, unit testing , and documentation) or Python ( pandas , numpy , jupyter , scikit-learn , pytorch , tensorflow ).
  • Experience developing data science algorithms to aid and assist in optimization and predictive analysis.
  • Experience do you have with Source Control Management tools (like Git).

Preferred Qualifications (Desired Skills/Experience):

  • An active Top Secret Security Clearance.
  • Ability to be detail-orientated and be an independent self-starter.
  • Communication skill to collaborate clearly, concisely, and consistently with team members, partners, customers, and business stakeholders.
  • Familiarity using the command line to interface between Linux and Windows environments in a remote setting.
  • Experience working in geographically-dispersed teams.
  • Maintain a detailed understanding of corporate security , cyber security , and insider threat problem domain and associated technologies and analytical methodologies.
