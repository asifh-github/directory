Full Job Description

EdPlus at ASU is hiring a motivated and creative Data Science Associate to join our high-performing Applied Data Science and Technology team. This role includes developing and maintaining complex tables relating to user web behavior analytics, prospect outreach efforts from the CRM and student information data and conducting descriptive and predictive data analysis tasks.

This opportunity might be perfect for you if you are passionate about statistics, data analysis and machine learning, and love working on big data with real world impact.

EdPlus is a unit of the ASU Enterprise focused on the design and scalable delivery of digital teaching and learning models to increase student success and reduce barriers to achievement in higher education. We advance the economic, social, cultural, and overall health of the local, national and international communities served by ASU.

Essential Duties

  • Processes and cleans raw web behavior data to build descriptive visualizations and reports.
  • Maintains tables from external data sources such as Salesforce CRM, Student Information System, etc.
  • Performs complex joins between different data sources to create single unified views.
  • Builds machine learning models to identify likelihood of different critical funnel events such as application start, submit, admit and enrollment.
  • Provides effective communications to leadership regarding model performance, project status, and other key findings.
  • Develops and implements plans to use predictive modeling, trend analysis and other data analysis techniques to explore data and to answer specific research questions.
  • Leads the secure collection, cleaning, standardization, transformation and storing of data from various sources.
  • Ensures the data preparation and presentation processes result in products that meet project requirements.
  • Evaluates the impact of innovations and interventions intended to improve student outcomes.
  • Utilizes effective project planning techniques to break down projects into tasks and supervises their accomplishment and ensures deadlines are met.
  • Prepares and oversees the preparation of reports, dashboards and presentations.
  • Collaborates with the team in order to improve the effectiveness of computational models using data analysis and feedback from education stakeholders.
  • Assumes or coordinates other duties or projects as assigned or directed.

Minimum Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Computer Information Systems, Business Information, Economics or a related field and one (1) year of related data analysis experience, OR, any equivalent combination of experience and/or education from which comparable knowledge, skills and abilities have been achieved.


Desired Qualifications

  • Evidence of a Bachelor's degree or higher in a related field.
  • Ability to maintain current understanding of the ever-changing AI and ML landscape.
  • Experience in training and deploying machine learning models.
  • Experience in the Google AI platform.
  • Experience in the curation of 1st-party behavioral data.
  • Experience in Google Analytics 360. Experience in SQL.
  • Experience in Python/R. Experience in visualization tools such as Google DataStudio and Tableau.
  • Experience with Google Tag Manager or like systems.
  • Demonstrated knowledge of common data science languages.
  • Experience in the concept of client-side data layer orchestration and enrichment.
  • Ability to effectively lead group sessions of diverse teams.
  • Ability to delegate tasks and lead complex projects.
  • Experience with learning management, customer relationship management, and student information system type data.
