Job Summary


  • Answer business questions using advanced statistics, predictive models, experimental design, and innovative data visualizations. Present information in a way that is easily understood by business leaders throughout the Service Operations organization, enabling them to make sound data driven decisions.

Essential Job Functions


  • Engage with business leaders to understand priorities and business questions
  • Collect needed data from a variety of sources and formats (e.g. databases, flat files, web, structured and unstructured) and prepare it for analysis
  • Perform advanced statistical analysis and modeling (e.g. traditional statistical hypothesis testing, GLM, tree-based models, Monte Carlo simulations)
  • Develop written presentations and other deliverables
  • Engage with business leaders (in writing and verbally) to effectively communicate findings and recommendations

Education Requirements


  • High school diploma or equivalent required. Bachelor’s degree preferred in Mathametics, Science or Computer Science.

Experience Requirements


  • One to two years of relevant analytics experience required
  • Proficiency in at least one statistical coding language is required: R, Python
  • Basic Proficiency in SQL is required
  • Experience communicating with 1st and 2nd level managers preferred

Special Skill Requirement

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills required.
