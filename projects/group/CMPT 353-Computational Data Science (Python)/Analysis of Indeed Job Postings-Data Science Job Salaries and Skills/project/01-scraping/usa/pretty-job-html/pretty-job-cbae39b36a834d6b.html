Working as a member of the Software Engineering team, you will build data pipelines for our predictive plant breeding platform and architect infrastructure to scale our rapidly growing data set. You will combine raw data from scientific experiments, high-throughput plant screening systems, and customer and partner data into consistent and machine-readable formats. You will develop and test architecture that enables data to be easily extracted and transformed for predictive modeling. Data is the centerpiece of our long-term vision and our day-to-day operations.

Major Responsibilities

  • Own maintenance and development of core SQL databases that contain genetic, phenotypic, and environmental data about growing plants
  • Implement innovative quantitative solutions that advance plant breeding alongside our team of data scientists and software engineers
  • Transform raw data produced by our R&D farm, customers, and partners into authoritative sources for all facts about the business
  • Use the latest machine learning data stack technologies to deploy services that enable predictive breeding that produce better vegetables
  • Explore ways to enhance data quality, reliability, and usability
  • Identify opportunities for data acquisition
  • Collaborate with data scientists and architects on several projects

Required Qualifications

  • Previous experience as a data engineer or in a similar role writing production-quality code
  • Technical expertise with data models, data mining, and segmentation techniques
  • Experience working with fast-growing data sets that quickly scale and how to keep the data healthy as it grows
  • Strong programming experience in SQL and Python
  • You are passionate and experienced with data pipelines, ETL, exploratory data analysis, and/or data visualization
  • Degree in Computer Science, IT, or a similar field; a Master's is a plus
  • Data engineering certification (e.g., IBM Certified Data Engineer) is a plus

Desired Qualifications

  • Experience with Google Cloud Platform, Airflow, and Looker
  • Working experience in Agile Scrum environments
  • Experience with source control tools such as Git.
  • Direct experience working in a start-up environment, or a mature appreciation for the opportunities and challenges arising in such an environment
