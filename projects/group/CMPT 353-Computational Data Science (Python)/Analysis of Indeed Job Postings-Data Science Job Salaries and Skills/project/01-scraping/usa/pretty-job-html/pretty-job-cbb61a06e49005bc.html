As a Prove Data Analyst you'll be analyzing data that are generated from our authentication APIs and present your analysis to the respective stakeholders. You'll work closely with sales, customer success, and product teams to lead customer & internal data studies to provide insights and educate on our state-of-the-art frictionless authentication platform. You'll work in a fast-paced and collaborative environment to build the next generation of authentication products.

What you'll be doing:

  • Lead customer data studies, providing valuable insight to Sales & Customer Success teams both during pre & post sale.
  • Synthesize data learnings into compelling stories and communicate to stakeholders.
  • Monitor and help refine metrics for product efficacy and customer success.
  • Assist in prototyping new analytics & machine learning models that improve both our insights and the product directly.
  • Work with product and engineering teams to aid testing of new product ideas and analyze results to provide actionable recommendations.
  • Perform deep analysis and build models to understand customer and product behavior, and extract key insights that impact product decisions.
  • Work with the broader Data team to find ways to scale our insights through better systems and automation.
  • Act as a strategic partner to product and engineering leaders to help prioritize opportunities and inform product strategy.

What we look for in you:

  • Demonstrate our core cultural values: clear communication, team player, continuous learning, and efficient execution.
  • Experience in R or Python.
  • Enjoy solving mysteries, have a desire to explore unknowns and act with little information.
  • Be able to independently create plans for analytics projects.
  • Confidently ask questions and share opinions without concern of being wrong or in the minority.
  • Be comfortable sharing your analysis to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
  • A desire to continuously grow and learn.
  • A desire to work in a high speed information environment that encourages you to be collaborative, communicative, and think fast and slow.
  • Highly team centric with a desire to express and receive feedback to/from the team.
  • Understanding of high level statistical concepts and experience in applying them.
  • Bachelor's Degree or education equivalent with 2+ years relevant experience or Masters in related field

Nice To Have:

  • Experience in a scripting language (C-shell or Bash).
  • Working knowledge of basic data science concepts such as:
    • Setting up a supervised modeling problem
    • Evaluating model performance through common metrics such as AUC, GINI, KS, etc.
    • Advantages and disadvantages of various supervised modeling techniques such as Linear/Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, Ensemble Modeling, etc.
    • Unsupervised modeling techniques such as K-means, PCA. LDA, etc are a plus
