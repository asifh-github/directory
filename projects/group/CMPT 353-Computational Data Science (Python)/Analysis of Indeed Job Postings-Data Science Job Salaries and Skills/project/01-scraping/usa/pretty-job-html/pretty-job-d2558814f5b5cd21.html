The Role at a Glance
You will provide subject matter expertise and direction for the pricing and risk management activities for the Group Protection division’s Dental and Vision products.
What you'll be doing
  • As a member of the Group Pricing team, consult on Dental & Vision pricing initiatives.
  • Identify and develop necessary analytics and reporting to monitor segment performance; communicate complex findings to various audiences
  • Support actuarial rate study and state filing work
  • Partner cross-functionally with implementation areas to assure proper administration of the business and maintenance of mainframe rate applications.
  • Coordinate with the finance area to ensure consistency of assumptions and to assist with identifying drivers of emerging experience and timely recognition of trends.
What we’re looking for
Must-haves:
  • Bachelor’s degree (minimum requirement) or master’s degree in Data Science, Mathematics, Computer Science, Actuarial Science, Statistics, or equivalent degree
  • 3 - 5+ years of experience in Data Science, Actuarial Science or similar relevant field that directly aligns with the specific responsibilities for this position
  • Expertise in SAS and/or SQL
  • Group Insurance product knowledge and general insurance/business knowledge
  • Strong analytical, problem solving, and communication skills
Nice-to-haves:
  • Experience preparing state regulatory filings
  • Experience with data engineering platforms such as Dataiku
  • Progress toward ASA designation
