Responsibilities

The Data Analyst is a member of the Ascension Data Science Institute’s Population Health Visualization team within HDI (Health Data Intelligence) department, supporting the collection, organization, and measurement of key performance measures. The position will also support improvement activities with payers, providers, internal consultants and technical teams to support all performance improvement efforts in Population Health contractual arrangements between Ascension and the payers.

Responsibilities:
  • Compiles analytical and statistical reports as needed.
  • Oversees provision of ad hoc management reports.
  • Reviews information for accuracy and reconciles data on a regular basis.
  • Proficiency with data extraction, manipulation, and interpretation using multiple data sources (EMR, claims, payer data)
  • Develops and prepares the required and/or requested reports, charts and graphs Analyzes claims and clinical loss data for trends and identifies opportunities for additional evaluation and focus.
  • Analyzes data for trends and identifies opportunities for additional evaluation and focus.
  • Conducts data analysis to assist internal and external consultants, Health Ministries and System Office staff in defining key performance indicators, measurement and management improvement
  • Collaborates with IT Division regarding the system requirements and necessity for accurate, timely reports and access to claims information
  • Develops approaches to presenting information including presentations and communications.
  • Supports advanced project management, improvement initiatives and tactical decisions/plans.
  • Analyzes healthcare data from conceptualization through presentation of data and proficiency with analytical tools, knowledge of data analysis methodology, use of presentation software and strong communication skills.
  • Develops and provides visual, spoken and written education related to data analysis, data presentation, including information/education related to maximizing the use of data products and functions across Ascension.
  • Supports database applications and analytical tools including evaluation of data quality, appropriate use of applications and maximizing internal and external database product functions across Ascension.
