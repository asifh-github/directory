Company Description

Chubb is the world’s largest publicly traded property and casualty insurer. With operations in 54 countries, Chubb provides commercial and personal property and casualty insurance, personal accident and supplemental health insurance, reinsurance and life insurance to a diverse group of clients. The company is distinguished by its extensive product and service offerings, broad distribution capabilities, exceptional financial strength, underwriting excellence, superior claims handling expertise and local operations globally.

Key Objectives

The position offers college students the opportunity to work within one section of the Global Analytics department during the summer. Interns are exposed to many facets of analytics work and techniques and are introduced to insurance terminology and concepts. As a member of the Analytics team, students will help analyze data to derive meaningful insights. Tasks may include building, testing, validating and maintaining models for pricing, underwriting or claims functions. Conclusions will be shared with top management to make strategic decisions about Chubb’s business. In addition to working closely with their supervisor, interns are given an opportunity to network with other members of the Analytics Department. Positions are located primarily in Philadelphia, PA and Jersey City, NJ.
