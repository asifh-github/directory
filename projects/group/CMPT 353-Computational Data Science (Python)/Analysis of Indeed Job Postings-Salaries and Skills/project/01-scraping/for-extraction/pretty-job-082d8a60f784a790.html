Data Scientist

Are you looking for a patient-focused, innovation-driven company that will inspire you and empower you to shine? Join us as a Data Scientist in our Lexington, MA location.

At Takeda, we are transforming the pharmaceutical industry through our R&D-driven market leadership and being a values-led company. To do this, we empower our people to realize their potential through life-changing work. Certified as a Global Top Employer, we offer stimulating careers, encourage innovation, and strive for excellence in everything we do. We foster an inclusive, collaborative workplace, in which our global teams are united by an unwavering commitment to deliver Better Health and a Brighter Future to people around the world.

Here, you will be a vital contributor to our inspiring, bold mission.

As a Data Scientist working on the Advanced Analytical Technologies team, you will be empowered to support and lead activities related to designing, building, and deploying industry-leading smart algorithms and predictive models from various analytical data including spectroscopy.

POSITION OBJECTIVES:

  • Work closely together with the automation and process analytical technology (PAT) teams
  • Learn, develop, plan and optimize data workstreams directly from laboratory sources
  • Work closely together with analytical experts in development and application of PAT technology
  • Plan and coordinate the setup of at-line analytics with Analytical Development team
  • Coordinate with other development teams to promote collaborative data management efforts, interoperability, and shared infrastructure
  • Knowledge sharing with global partners, internal and external
  • Present accomplishments within the team, across other functions, internally and externally – cooperation partners, conferences etc.

POSITION ACCOUNTABILITIES:

  • Collaboration with multiple multi-functional teams of scientists, analysts, and engineers.
  • Implement mechanistic and statistical modeling and simulation
  • Support development and implementation of digital twins
  • Define modeling and simulation work packages as standard for future development projects in an AQbD environment
  • Evaluate technology/software with internal and external partners

EDUCATION, BEHAVIORAL COMPETENCIES AND SKILLS:

  • Bachelors degree in Data Science, Statistics, Bio-Informatics, Mathematics, Chemometrics, Chemistry, Biology, Engineering or related pharmaceutical science and 4+ years relevant industry experience
  • Masters degree in Data Science, Statistics, Bio-Informatics, Mathematics, Chemometrics, Chemistry, Biology, Engineering or related pharmaceutical science 2+ years relevant industry experience
  • PhD in Data Science, Statistics, Bio-Informatics, Mathematics, Chemometrics, Chemistry, Biology, Engineering or related pharmaceutical science and 0+ years relevant industry experience
  • Background in DoE, continuous processing, automation and digitalization of bioprocesses
  • Experience with regression and classification modeling, data pre-processing, statistical concepts, and the ability to work in multiple languages (Python, R, Scala, C/C++, SQL).
  • Experience with various analytical software (JMP, SIMCA, Minitab)
