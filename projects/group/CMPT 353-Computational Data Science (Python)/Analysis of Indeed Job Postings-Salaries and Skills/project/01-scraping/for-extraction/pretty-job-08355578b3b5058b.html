Why Badger Meter?

Badger Meter is a leading global water technology company, with a mission to preserve and protect the world’s most precious resource. For more than 100 years, cities and businesses have utilized our innovative and trusted solutions to enhance operational efficiency and conserve water while making it more affordable, clean and resilient. Just like every drop counts, at Badger Meter every employee counts because our contributions have a lasting impact on the world!

What You Will Contribute:

The Intern – Systems Engineering will work under the mentorship of other Systems Integration team members to develop predictive models in AWS Sagemaker that generate alerts when a product is deviating from the expected behavior. The specific intern project will be to develop a model relevant to the product performance, and impacts and correlations related to weather, battery performance, manufacturing characteristics, etc.
Essential Job Duties:
  • Monitoring dashboards for alerts and following up on the alerts
  • Completing small development tasks
  • Creating, testing, and deploying models
  • Aggregating, cleaning, and importing data sets
  • Reporting and escalating anomalies
  • Document test results
  • Participate in follow-up investigations in response to an anomaly
Qualifications:
  • Currently pursuing a graduate (MS, PhD) degree in Data Science, Machine Learning or related fields
  • Experience with reading, debugging, and writing code in one or more programming languages
  • Experience with creating machine learning models
  • Basic understanding of engineering fundamentals
  • Sound troubleshooting and technical skill set
  • Intermediate skills with Microsoft Office suite
  • Ideal candidate will have experience with one or more of the following: Amazon Web Services, Elasticsearch, Python, C#
  • US Work Authorization required (we do not provide sponsorship for internships or full-time roles)
