Full Job Description

Responsibilities

  • Candidate successfully designs, develops, and programs methods, processes, and systems to consolidate and analyze data sources in order to generate actionable insights and solutions for DoD and Federal clients.
  • Candidate builds predictive models and uses data mining, text mining, SAS programming, and large-scale data management to compete tasks. Successfully analyzes quality improvement data using NCQA Health Plan Employee Data and Information Set (HEDIS) and AHRQ-QI measures.
  • Works to develop and deploy clinical performance metrics using BASE SAS or similar systems.
  • Interacts with government clients and team to identify requirements for data analysis, experiments, and solutions. Identifies meaningful insights from large data and metadata sources; interprets and communicates insights and findings from analysis to government clients and internal team.
  • Analyzes qualitative and quantitative clinical and defense health business data and provides stakeholders with data quality reports, dashboards, program data, and information to inform continuous improvement and reduce redundancy.
  • Provides solutions to research and evaluation requirements at the program level, assisting projects with data manipulation and usage across platforms and systems.
