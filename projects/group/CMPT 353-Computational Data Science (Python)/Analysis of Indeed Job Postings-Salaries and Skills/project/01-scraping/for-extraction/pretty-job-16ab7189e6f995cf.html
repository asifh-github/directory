Job Title: Data Engineer

Company Overview

Cloudmed, a leading healthcare technology company, helps health systems get healthier by ensuring they get paid for the care they provide. Using our powerful CloudmedAI™ Platform and deep subject matter expertise, we help clients solve the complex problem of accurate payments for hundreds of millions of patient interactions each year.

Our mission and our people make a difference by uncovering funds that can be used to hire doctors, nurses, and other essential practitioners, build healthcare centers such as birthing or cancer centers, and fund outreach programs to improve the health of local communities. Last year we helped over 3,100 hospital partners across the U.S. recover over $1.5 billion of underpaid or unidentified revenue.

Cloudmed is committed to creating an inclusive employee experience. We hire diverse professionals with all kinds of backgrounds and experiences, and together we work toward the best outcomes for our clients. Regardless of race, gender, gender identity, religion, sexual orientation, age, disability, or if you’re parenting the next generation of problem-solvers, we believe we work best when everyone feels free to bring their most authentic self to work. Cloudmed is a flexible, remote-enabled company.

Description:

We are seeking a Data Engineer to join our Data Warehouse team. This role will report to the Director of IT Reporting and Analytics and be involved in the planning, design, and implementation of our centralized data warehouse for reporting and analytics across all applications within the company.

Qualifications:

To be successful in this role the candidate needs to have the following qualifications:

  • Knowledge of SQL and Scala
  • Experience working with SQL and NoSQL database systems
  • Experience with cloud environments (Azure Preferred)
  • Experience with acquiring and preparing data from primary and secondary disparate data sources
  • Deep knowledge of modern data warehouses including Azure Synapse and Snowflake
  • Ability to write complex ETL processes using SQL and Spark notebooks
  • Ability to perform data analysis, data mining and data mapping
  • Healthcare industry experience preferred

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with and across Agile teams to design, develop, test, implement, and support technical solutions
  • Work with other team with deep experience in machine learning, distributed microservices, and data science domains to understand how to centralize their data
  • Share your passion for staying experimenting with and learning new technologies
  • Perform thorough data analysis, uncover opportunities, and address business problems
  • Summarize, group, and visualize data sets and information for inclusion in the data warehouse
