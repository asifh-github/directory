JOB SUMMARY:

The Jr Software Engineer will work as a part of a team responsible for evolving and supporting Quest’s Managed Services Clients and Operations Center. The candidate will contribute to projects and collaborate with the team to address the challenges that emerge as we evolve services, platforms, and client offerings. The candidate will be responsible to implement industry standard best practices for our build and deployment pipelines leading to continuous integration and continuous delivery under the direction of the Senior Software Engineer and Senior SQL Engineers. The candidate should be self-driven and possess good analytical and troubleshooting skills. The candidate will work with Development, Quality Assurance, Infrastructure, security and other technical and operational teams to continuously improve the quality, speed and methodologies used in delivering our capabilities.

Essential RESPONSIBILITIES and ACCOUNTABILITIES:

  • Write modular, reusable code and have experience building and maintaining internal libraries.
  • Create applications that can interface with vendor APIs and SQL for reporting.
  • Create webpages for presentation of commonly requested information.
  • Configuration, implementation and support of applications used to monitor and manage client infrastructures (e.g. monitoring, reporting services, antivirus, etc.)
  • Effectively communicate technical issues to senior engineers and management staff.
  • Participate in assigned projects and communicate progress and challenges effectively
  • Grow your knowledge of development methodology, processes, and programming languages.
  • Take part in Quality Assurance efforts to ensure comprehensive test coverage, effective performance and load tests, timely resolution of issues, and other actions to assure delivery of quality software.
  • Other projects and responsibilities may be added as needed.

JOB SPECIFICATIONS – Knowledge, Skills and Qualifications:

  • Some work or college experience in software development or data science.
  • Experience with the Microsoft technology stack, particularly C# and .Net frameworks.
  • Experience with object-oriented languages such as C# or Java
  • Visual Studio proficiency
  • Experience with a relational database like Microsoft SQL Server or MySQL.
  • Familiarity with Git or other versioning control system.
  • Prepare written and oral reports and documenting related to software development
  • Ability to perform independent research to solve problems.
  • Experience with Linux administration and or development is a plus.

Working Conditions / Environment & Physical Demands:

  • Required to work in an office environment with moderate noise and moderate temperatures.
  • Incumbent may be asked to perform other duties as required.
  • Sit for long periods of time.
  • May lift/carry/push/pull up to 50 pounds
  • May require minimal walking, climbing, stooping, crouching, and/or bending
  • Must be able to work flexible hours, including hours beyond the normal schedule when necessary
  • May be required to work off-hours, weekend & holidays as needed.
  • Position may require Travel to customer and Quest locations.
