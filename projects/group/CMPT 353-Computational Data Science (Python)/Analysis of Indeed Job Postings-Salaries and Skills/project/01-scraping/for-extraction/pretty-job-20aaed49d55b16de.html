Job Title - Data Scientist 1
Location - Beltsville, MD

Ion Storage Systems, Inc. is looking to add a full-time data scientist to our team in Beltsville, MD. This position reports to the Principal Technical Program Directory and will be responsible for helping ION understand and make connections through thorough data analysis on all areas of work at ION. In this role, you will work in a cross-functional environment as a resource to various technical teams and partner with other scientists and engineers. We expect this candidate can apply scientific principles to a range of technical projects and make decisions based on sound scientific reasoning.


In addition to a competitive salary, we offer the following company benefits:
  • Medical, dental, vision, disability, and life insurance, with the company paying more than 75% of the premiums
  • Stock options in an early-stage company
  • Hybrid work model
  • Company and floating holidays
  • 17 days of PTO to start
  • 401k plan with a 4% company match
  • Wellness allowance of $1k annually
Responsibilities:
  • Conduct data analyses across all departments within ION to help us transform data into valuable technical insights
  • Implement and validate various models with the goal to help our scientists and engineers better understand technical behaviors
  • Assess different statistical or machine learning techniques as needed
  • Participate in discussions to specify new data collection processes/tools for new projects
  • Communicate with internal clients to understand and help define technical needs
  • Evaluate results and communicate the results to a range of audiences (both technical and non-technical)

Required Experience:
  • 1-3 years of working experience as a data scientist, preferably in a corporate environment
  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics or equivalent field
Required Skills:
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
  • Strong interpersonal skills to facilitate smooth and efficient inter-disciplinary collaboration
  • Familiarity with scripting languages such as Python and database scripting languages such as MySQL
  • Strong understanding of statistical programming environments like R and experience with machine learning in environments like TensorFlow.
  • Exposure to analytical software such as MATLAB or SAS
