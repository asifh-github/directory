Full Job Description

Amick Brown is seeking an experienced Research Data Scientist II, Health Economics & Outcomes Research (HEOR) for our direct client.
Location: Remote
Duration: 6 Months

Description
  • Joining our clients means joining a team dedicated to using technology to benefit patients by improving surgical efficacy and decreasing surgical invasiveness, with patient safety as our highest priority.

Primary Function of Position
  • Research Data Scientist II will be responsible for supporting analytical research activities for scientific publications, customer analytics solutions, hospital channel marketing and market access.
  • The senior data scientist will be the data technical expert in algorithm development and research tool solutions around RWD databases.
  • Responsibilities include setting up the analytic data files for further analysis, documenting/validating algorithm, and platform development/versioning, managing the analytic tool in various analytic platforms including R, SQL, Snowflake and assuring their integrity.
  • Additional responsibilities include training users of the analytic platforms, the various RWD databases and investigation of database use case feasibility assessments as well as working/managing external vendors for database and analytic platforms.

Roles and Responsibilities
  • Design tools and algorithms for evidence needs of HEOR and Global Access, Value & Economics (GAVE).
  • Performs analyses of large healthcare databases (e.g., insurance claims, electronic medical records).
  • Hosting retrieve, maintain, and update large databases. Work with Database Engineering and Analysis (DE&A) team for hosting the external large database in house.
  • Continued database maintenance, ongoing data updates and algorithm development.
  • Develop analytical infrastructure to enable the automation process of analytic and research solutions.
  • Develop, validate, and document programming for different algorithms in RWD database assets.
  • Develop, manage, and track the Snowflake, SQL, R code banks.
  • Develop working instructions/ training guidelines and oversee revisions /updates to current WIs/training guidelines for programming activities
  • Training and education of analytical infrastructure and RWD assets for new hires.
  • Work with regional GAVE, Market Access & Customer Analytics (MACA), HEOR teams to identify RWD in support of research strategy in region.
