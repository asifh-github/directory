Who We Are

We are the Services and Logistics (S&L) Data, Insights and Analytics team. We love all things data related! Our team is building a revitalized analytics foundation that will provide business analytics services including self-service access to data via Snowflake, federated dashboard development via PowerBI and Tableau, and practical business insights via data analysis and data science projects. We are seeking to add an expert data scientist to perform predictive modeling and to develop analytics-based decision support for our S&L business.

What You Will Do:

  • Complete full lifecycle data science projects including problem identification, data collection, data and feature engineering, data modelling, model deployment, insight sharing and executive-level presentations.
  • Solve for supply chain business problems and test hypothesis related to supply chain opportunities. As a shared-service team member, you will provide solutions across several business functions including demand planning, repair operations, inventory management, and product quality.
  • Apply appropriate analytics techniques for operations research, machine learning or advance data science algorithms. Synthesize and apply relevant insights from industry and academic literature to Cisco problems. Develop decision support systems, tools, and models to improve Cisco Service and Logistics capabilities.

Who You'll Work with:

Data scientists work with a team of other data scientists, business intelligence analysts, data stewards, and IT experts. You will work collaboratively with cross-functional business clients. Your analysis and recommendations will be delivered to senior operations leaders, directors and vice presidents across multiple business functions. You will also collaborate with data scientists across Cisco.

Who you are:

As a data scientist, you have the ability to:

  • inspect complex data sets and identify relevant insights
  • design experiments, test hypothesis, and interpret models for business consumers
  • deliver projects in collaboration with technical teams
  • analyze databases using Oracle and Snowflake
  • define extract, transform and load processing
  • participate in data science community forums

You have professional experience with:

  • Multi Echelon Optimization, demand forecasting, inventory management, or production scheduling
  • Python/R development using Pandas, SciKit-Learn, MatPlotLib and other libraries.
  • Facilitating model discussions using Jupyter Notebook navigation
  • Machine Learning algorithms (Regression/ Clustering/ Classification etc.).
  • Solving large optimization problems using linear / non-linear / integer / dynamic / stochastic programming, as well as heuristic approaches.
  • Applying data science techniques, such as machine learning, statistical modeling, and artificial intelligence that can inform or improve operational systems.

Other Relevant Experience:

  • Supply Chain, logistics, or services business experience
  • ASCM SCOR or related metrics frameworks
  • PTC SPM, Oracle ERP, or related systems experience a plus
  • Cisco IT experience is a plus

Minimum Requirements For This Role:

  • Bachelor’s, Master's or PhD degree in Operations Research, Industrial Engineering, Data Science, Management Science, or related field.
  • Bachelors + 5 years of related experience, or
  • Masters + 3 years of related experience, or
  • No experience required if you have a PhD
