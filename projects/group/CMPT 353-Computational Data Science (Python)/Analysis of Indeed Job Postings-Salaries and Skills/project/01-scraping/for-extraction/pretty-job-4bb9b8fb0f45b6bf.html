Job Description:

WHY YOU SHOULD INTERN WITH LENNOX

During your time with Lennox, you will be given real-world projects and will partner with our business leaders to accomplish mission critical goals. We will look to YOU to help promote innovation, creativity and excellence in everything we do. As an intern you will be invited to attend lunch and learns to discover different business segments within the organization. Additionally, you will be invited to participate in fun activities with other interns, management and executives. Previous interns have experienced events such as bowling, Top Golf, community outreach activities and more! You will get to immerse yourself in our culture, all the while learning unique skills and developing yourself as a professional. High preforming interns may be given opportunities to join Lennox as a full-time associate upon their graduation.

Students who will be relocating during their internship / co-op, will have their travel expenses reimbursed along with being eligible for a housing allowance.


WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING
As an IT intern, you’ll be responsible for designing, configuring, and supporting corporate IT applications to support Supply Chain and manufacturing functions. You’ll assist in requirements gathering and leading discussions with the business partners. In addition, you will be testing and documenting the changes related to the application changes and utilize data analysis to support scenarios that needs to be included in the various user stories. An internship with us will reveal to you the ‘ins-and-outs’ of this industry!
Qualifications:

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR

  • Students pursing a degree in IT, IT Management, Computer Science or another related field
  • Technical skills in SAP IBP, SAP ECC MM PP LO, SAP BI
  • Ability to work well in a team setting


WHICH COMPENTENCIES DRIVE SUCCESS

Having the right qualifications is only a part of what it takes to be a successful intern. At Lennox International, we believe in following a set of behaviors and competencies to achieve your full potential. In this position, it is vital that you embody these core competencies:

Decision Quality: You exemplify the ability to make good and timely decisions related to prioritizing and organizing time and approaching classes and assigned work.

Drives Results: You display commitment to classwork and projects, go above and beyond to ensure deadlines are met and consistently achieve the highest standards in your work.

Communicates Effectively: You demonstrate excellent oral and written communication skills through presentations and written assignments. In addition, you practice active listening with the ability to work with team members, sharing ideas like you may see in a lab setting or through group assignments.
