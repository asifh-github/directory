Full Job Description

Data Scientist

Business Line Description

This vacancy is in the MCU/MPU R&D organization, within the design and quality group responsible for end-to-end Quality for SoCs in the Automotive Processors and/or Advanced microcontrollers in NXP. We ensure Customer and execution quality starting from product introduction and definition, through the NPI SoC development and successful launch and throughout the product lifetime. We are seeking a self-motivated engineer who enjoys problem solving and is comfortable challenging the status quo.

Job Summary

  • Technical leadership in the R&D Quality & Infrastructure organization to implement ML/AI solutions for the next generation of products

  • Development of tools, processes and systems to analyze large data sets (“big data”) to generate actionable insights and solutions

  • Analysis and visualization of design data to make data-driven recommendations to improve design, quality, and yield

  • Communication of results and conclusions to design and management teams

  • Participation in problem solving to drive root cause understanding of design, yield or quality issues

  • Development of systems to improve ability of Design and PE/TE engineers to analyze and interpret data efficiently

Key Challenges

  • Identifying meaningful insights from large datasets from multiple sources

  • Influencing global IT teams to align with solutions developed

  • Work closely with senior engineers in the Technology Enablement team, PE/TE organization

  • Collaborate with extended global teams, including IT, TEA, Product and Test Engineering, Quality and Manufacturing

  • Engage and share knowledge with global Design and Product and Test Engineering teams

  • Be Result Oriented. Focus required on achieving aligned end goals

  • Independent and have the ability to excel in a global and flexible organization style

  • Excellent written and verbal communications skills in English

Job Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree plus Masters/PhD in data analytics with 3-5 years’ industry experience, or equivalent

  • Demonstrated understanding and experience in applying traditional statistical, machine learning, deep learning, NLP, and/or graph techniques to address business problems

  • Hands on experience in Python/R, SQL and Shell scripts, able to build web interfaces, familiar with SQL and other databases

  • Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure) will be advantage

  • Experienced in applying the right ML/Statistical models for a business context

Job location

  • Austin, TX
