Full Job Description

Airbnb was born in 2007 when two Hosts welcomed three guests to their San Francisco home, and has since grown to over 4 million Hosts who have welcomed more than 1 billion guest arrivals in almost every country across the globe. Every day, Hosts offer unique stays and experiences that make it possible for guests to connect with communities in a more authentic way.

Airbnb is one of the world's largest marketplaces for unique, authentic places to stay and things to do, offering over 7 million accommodations and 40,000 handcrafted activities, all powered by local hosts. An economic empowerment engine, Airbnb has helped millions of hospitality entrepreneurs monetize their spaces and their passions while keeping the financial benefits of tourism in their own communities. With more than half a billion guest arrivals to date, and accessible in 62 languages across 220 countries and regions, Airbnb promotes people-to-people connection, community and trust around the world.

About the Team:

Airbnb is a global platform that connects travelers and hosts from over 81,000 cities. Our mission is to enable a world where anyone can belong anywhere. Using data to help ensure a world class experience for our users is critical to our mission.

We are looking for talented Data Scientists to join our Search & Personalization Data Science team. The team works on building a world class search experience for our guests, from the homepage, to search and listing pages, and checkout. You will join a team with 3 other data scientists, responsible for building the data products, inference frameworks, and intelligence to create a personalized search experience.

Responsibilities:

You will work centrally, with other data scientists, partnering cross-functionally across the search experience. Your initial focus areas in the team:

  • Developing a framework for evaluating the performance of supply categories in search and partnering with product, marketing, engineering and operations to create new categories.
  • Operationalizing a system to detect suboptimal user paths in the product leveraging user behavioral logs.
  • Developing a framework of conversion drivers, connecting input metrics to outputs, to inform strategies, decision-making, and track progress on team goals.
  • Providing technical mentorship to other data scientists, holding a high bar for data products architecture.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of relevant industry experience
  • Expert in at least one programming language for data analysis (Python or R)
  • Experience with inference and machine learning techniques
  • Experience in high-dimensional data modeling
  • Proven ability to succeed in both collaborative and independent work environments
  • Passion for scaling data science work

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience working on a consumer search product or recommender systems
  • Experience with causal inference and structural models
  • Strong skills in SQL and distributed system optimization (e.g. Hadoop, Hive, Spark, Presto)
  • Experience with an ETL framework like Airflow
  • Python skills preferred
  • Experience with technical mentorship of other data scientists
  • Experience with prototyping and writing production code
  • Experience with system design
