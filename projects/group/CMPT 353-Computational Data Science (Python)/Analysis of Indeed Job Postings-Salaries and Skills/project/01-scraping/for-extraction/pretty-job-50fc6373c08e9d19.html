QinetiQ Inc. is seeking a junior Data Labeler to support the identification and labeling of video image data at our customer site in Fort Belvoir, VA.

Your Contribution to the Mission:
Working with video image data from leading edge technology systems to identify and lable objects within the image

Assist with the development of machine learning algorithms by identifying and marking objects within still and video images

Candidate responsibilities include drawing and adjusting boxes around objects of interest on video imagery

Candidate will be provided training on labeling tool and instruction on how and which objects in a scene should be labeled

Candidate should be comfortable working on a computer/ looking at a computer screen all day

No programming experience required. Candidate will launch application and use a mixture of GUI interaction and hotkeys to utilize labeling tool

To Land This Job, You Need:
High School Diploma, Bachelor's degree preferred but not required

Experience woking on computer systems including opening and running applications, tabbing between windows, and basic data entry

Must be comfortable working by themselves

Must be willing to commute to Fort Belvoir full-time

DOD Secret or Higher Clearance

Job Type: Full Time
