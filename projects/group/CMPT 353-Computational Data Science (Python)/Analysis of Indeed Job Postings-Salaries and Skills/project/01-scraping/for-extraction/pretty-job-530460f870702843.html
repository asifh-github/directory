Who You Are

The Data Analyst will have a diverse set of responsibilities supporting the design / build of data platforms that enable revenue generation, operational execution, reporting, strategic analysis and next generation Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning. You will have the opportunity to leverage your data analysis and design skills to help Guaranteed Rate deliver upon the organization’s Data Strategy and Data Management Program. You will work cross-functionally across departments and enhance existing data platforms and build new information infrastructure that helps the business uncover insights, make clear visualizations, tell compelling stories, and drive action and outcomes. The ideal candidate is action-oriented and leverages their experience and academic training to pragmatically tackle complex data problems, including addressing regulatory obligations. The Guaranteed Rate Data Office engages with a diverse array of stakeholders including both business and technology teams. The Data Analyst will deliver data platforms that help the organization realize data-driven decisions and operational optimization.

What You Do

Analyze customer data to develop actionable insights to drive customer behavior and operational efficiencies

Partner with Technology and Data Engineering resources to design, build and implement data platforms in support of analytics, reporting, marketing, operations, etc.

Conduct root-cause data analysis to identify patterns and trends

Explain data anomalies, develop data visualizations and reporting to drive key messaging and recommendations

Deliver data-centric process improvement both individual teams and cross-organization / multi-stakeholder projects

Assists delivering the design, development, and maintenance of databases, data marts and dashboard tools; Orchestrates data quality / integrity improvements for critical data elements

Support strategic projects related to new product deployment and customer-level aggregation deliverables

Support Marketing data extracts, campaign execution including segmentation analysis. Provide observations and business context to deliver meaningful insights to business leads related to campaigns/offers

What Makes You a Perfect Fit

4+ years of progressive professional work experience building data platforms; expertise supporting/executing reporting & analytics, including working in teams of data analytic resources

Experience and a working understanding of data profiling tooling and data analysis methodologies to understand data quality and identify data concerns

Expertise with campaign execution tools and business processes, including data integration and data extraction processes

Advanced understanding and proven experience using data management concepts, data normalization and ETL development

Experienced building presentations with actionable insights

Strong verbal and visual communication skills to present and explain data strategies, data deliverables and data analytic insights

Excellent attention to detail and accuracy

Proven experience with on premise and cloud-based data solutions (SQL Server, Azure, AWS, GCP, Snowflake), data modeling, metadata management, database design/build/implementation/management and data visualization tools/platforms

Sound understanding of data governance concepts

Hands on experience with SQL, Python, or R
