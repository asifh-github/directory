Full Job Description

The Phia Group, LLC is a thriving, service-oriented company headquartered in Canton, Massachusetts. As consultants, we represent health plans nationwide, providing our clients with innovative cost-containment solutions through our unique service offerings. As a result of our most valuable resource, our talented and committed team, we continue to enjoy high levels of growth.
In addition to helping hard working employees and their families access high quality, affordable health care, while enjoying unrivaled benefits for yourself, you will also enjoy being part of a team that has been recognized by The Boston Globe as a Top Place to Work . In its 14th annual employee-based survey, The Boston Globe determined that The Phia Group is one of the most rewarding, admired and meaningful employment experiences in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, as voted upon by the people who know them best – their employees
We are currently seeking a Healthcare EDI Data Engineer. Ideally the candidate will have significant experience parsing EDI files to understand the nuances of various segments of EDI files. The candidate will also have experience working with healthcare claims data and an understanding of databases and ETL processes.
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following, and other duties may be assigned.

  • Use your healthcare EDI expertise and help interpret EDI files to support Phia products and services
  • Provide support on the design and build of new Products that utilize EDI files for data exchange
  • Manage and drive multiple projects to completion in all phases within company-specified deadlines
  • Interface with clients and vendors over the phone and through emails
  • Build and cultivate strong working relationships with clients to gain their support and solidify their continued business
  • Troubleshoot and assist clients and vendors to resolve issues with varying degrees of complexity
  • Provide exceptional customer service and follow up to clients and vendors
  • Collaborate with various functional groups including sales, Recovery, and third-party vendors to provide client issue resolutions in a timely manner
  • Perform analysis to identify data anomalies and implement adequate and efficient solutions

Experience and Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree preferred
  • 7+ years of experience working with databases and Data integration tools. Informatica and SQL experience a plus
  • In-depth knowledge and technical understanding of the various segments of healthcare EDI files like 834, 835 and 837
  • Experience using various tools to parse, interpret and modify above EDI files using available tools and EDI companion guides
  • Ability to train data engineers and developers on the details and nuances of various segments / elements of the EDI.
  • Experience working with different EMR vendors, Providers and Clearing Houses
  • Strong communication skills; both written and verbal
  • Possess strong project management, organizational, and customer service skills
  • Comfortable working under minimal supervision
  • Ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously
  • Ability to work independently or in a team environment
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access)
  • Ability to complete a short interview work assignment to demonstrate EDI 837 parsing.
