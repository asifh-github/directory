Full Job Description

Who are we looking for?
Choice Hotels, one of the world’s largest lodging franchisors, has an exciting new opportunity as our Data Scientist 3 in the Decisioning and Data Science team. The Decisioning and Data Science team is the team that partners with leaders across the enterprise to design and deliver end-to-end data science solutions for use cases in Marketing, Loyalty, Global Sales, Finance, Franchise Sales, and Brands. Examples of spatial models include revenue modeling, point-of-interest analysis, and “hot spot” modeling. As a key member of our Decisioning and Data Science team you will play an integral role in developing spatial analytics capabilities in support of key corporate initiatives.
Are you passionate about building spatial models and spatial data? We invite you to apply today for our Data Scientist 3 role today and #MakeItYourChoice.
Your Responsibilities
  • Execute modeling initiatives that drive key decisions and insights across the enterprise. Work closely with stakeholders to address strategic and operational needs with spatial models such as:
    • Geographically based RevPAR and occupancy models
    • Hotel “hot spot” models
    • Point of Interest clustering models
  • Provide pre-processed spatial data to the department for modeling use.
  • Research cutting-edge methodologies and contribute to upskilling the overall team’s capabilities. Ensure that the team is maturing its data science and analytics knowledge and understanding.
  • Work with IT architecture and data delivery team to enhance data infrastructure to support real-time, machine learning modeling capabilities, and deploy throughout Choice.
  • Support data ingestion efforts from third parties.
Your Experience, Skills & Competencies
  • Master’s Degree in Economics, Mathematics, Statistics, Operations Research, Geographic Information Systems (GIS) or another quantitative field.
  • 2+ years of analytical experience is required with a background in spatial modeling. Examples include spatial clustering, spatial regression, and spatial autocorrelation.
  • Knowledge of AWS environment and AWS Sagemaker preferred.
  • Experience with basic data engineering tools (e.g. pandas, geopandas, numpy, and SQL).
  • Experience with basic version control (e.g. Git, BitBucker) and project management tools (e.g. Jira).
  • Experience with Machine Learning approaches and techniques such as decision trees, neural networks, and collaborate filtering is a plus.
  • Must be an energized self-starter who can work independently and who drives for results.
  • Ability to evaluate business trends and develop successful solutions that enhance business performance.
  • Effectively partner with business stakeholders to ensure integrated and timely insights to the enterprise.
  • Has strong intellectual curiosity.
  • Active learner – able to enhance personal, professional, and business growth through new knowledge and experiences; pushes the organization to learn from other industries’ standards and practices.
  • Results oriented; delivers results under difficult conditions and demonstrates balanced judgment under pressure.
  • Good communication skills – includes oral (one on one and presentations to large audiences) and written.
Your Team
This is an individual contributor role that will report to the Manager, Predictive Analytics and Data Science. You will have multiple peer teammates and collaborate with cross functional departments on a regular basis.
Your Work Location
As our Data Scientist 3 you will be based out of our office in Rockville, Maryland. This position offers the opportunity to either work hybrid or full-time remote from your home. If remote, you will need to be located on the east coast, as you will be required to connect virtually with Choice team members and leadership on video via Zoom, and occasionally travel in-person to our beautiful, state-of-the-art worldwide offices in Rockville, Maryland or Scottsdale, AZ.
We are focused on safety and wellness in our offices, and as such, as a Choice associate you will be required to disclose your COVID-19 vaccination status and provide a copy of your vaccination card. If you are not vaccinated or elect not to disclose your status, we will require a weekly negative COVID-19 test if/when working in the office.
