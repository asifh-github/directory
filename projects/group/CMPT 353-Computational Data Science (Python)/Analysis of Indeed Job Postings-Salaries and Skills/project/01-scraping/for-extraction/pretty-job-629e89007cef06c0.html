OpenStore is Keith Rabois' Miami-based Series B startup, founded in March 2021, and reportedly valued at more than $970M. OpenStore is reimagining the future of e-commerce. Today, we're building the technology and team to identify, value, acquire and scale long-tail e-commerce brands. We envision a future where emerging e-commerce brands have access to instant liquidity for their businesses and where consumers have ready access to the best small-brand products.

Behind the scenes, we're a team of experienced founders, operators, and software engineers who saw a chance to change the e-commerce landscape. We have grown our team 5x in size within the first year and are eager to continue expanding our team with humble achievers committed to excellence.

ABOUT THE OPPORTUNITY:
As an early Data Scientist, you will be a critical part of our company's direction and future. Data is the core of the OpenStore strategy. We're stitching together granular Shopify sales records, product characteristics, bank statements, ad placements, web traffic and more to construct a detailed view into a vast array of digital merchants. You will be building the bones of our company and making decisions that define our destiny.
WHAT YOU'LL DO:
  • Invent new ways of leveraging our data asset. As a founding member of the Data Science team, you will build the forecasts, analytics and automated decision making at the core of our business.
  • Contribute to many areas. Your work will extend to sourcing, pricing, acquiring and operating e-commerce businesses. You will work with stakeholders from each of these domains to execute on our vision of data-centric automation.
  • You will find yourself stretching beyond your comfort zone and wearing many hats.
  • You'll be working with top-notch people who all love to teach and learn.
  • Shape product strategy. You will get to use your own product, talk to stakeholders who use it and have a direct impact on how our company leverages data to create a competitive advantage.
WHO YOU ARE:
  • You like to build and ship. You have opinions and can drive forward to delivery. You think strategically about prioritization.
  • You've done it before. You've worked with messy data, conducted principled research and shipped models to production. Your experience will help us avoid pitfalls and move faster.
  • You thrive in fast paced environments. At this stage we're still learning. You will iterate with engineers, analysts and product managers to focus on high impact work.
  • You are curious. You want to understand the application of your work and learn how to build a high growth company.
