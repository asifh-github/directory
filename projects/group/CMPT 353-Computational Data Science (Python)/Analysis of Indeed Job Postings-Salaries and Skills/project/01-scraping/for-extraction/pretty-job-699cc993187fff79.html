Lockheed Martin Aeronautics in Fort Worth, Texas is seeking a full-time Early Career Data Science / Data Analyst. In this role, you will support the Data Quality & Integration Management (DQIM) organization in support of Lockheed Martin Aeronautics Sustainment. We are seeking world-class talent to apply technical standards, principles, theories, concepts, and techniques in the data quality engineering discipline to resolve complex problems. The candidate must be able to concurrently handle multiple and widely varying assignments in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment.
  • Must have the ability to work independently as well as being a team-player and willing to support other team members on a proactive basis
  • Must engage professionally and effectively with senior management
  • Must be able to successfully meet deadlines and execute to a strict timeline

The successful candidate will have experience and/or knowledge of Bachelor of Science Degree in Engineering or a related technical discipline: Industrial, Mechanical, Aerospace, Electrical, Data Science, Data Analysis, Physics or Mathematics (use of stochastic and deterministic data models) or related technical disciplines with an emphasis on modeling, data manipulation, and/or the application of mathematical and statistical methods.
  • Experience working to integrate and deliver data and provide data forensics/analysis
  • Techniques in data analysis, data extraction, data mining.
  • Manipulation of data using Microsoft Excel and/or Access
Coursework or experience using databases and/or visualizations (MS Access, Tableau, SQL/Oracle
  • Coursework or experience in formal presentation development, PowerPoint construct and delivery

Must be a US Citizen. This position will require a government security clearance. This position is located at a facility that requires special access.
