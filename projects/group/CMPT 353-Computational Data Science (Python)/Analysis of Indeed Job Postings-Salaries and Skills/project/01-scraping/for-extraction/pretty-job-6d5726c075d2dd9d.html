Job Summary

As a Data Scientist you will be responsible for conducting projects involving the application of advanced statistical methods to find actionable results and target marketing messaging across various channels. Their duties include the collection of data requirements, selection and validation of methodology, code and process development and maintenance, and interpretation and presentation of results for businesses.

Essential Functions:

  • Identifies data requirements and acquires data, cleanses data for statistical methods and lists, applies data visualization techniques to evaluate data for further analysis
  • Builds custom quantitative solutions by applying advanced statistical methods such as predictive or causal modeling, data mining, forecasting, segmentation, cluster analysis, or factor analysis; to find actionable results and target marketing messages across all available channels.
  • Creates, coordinates, and maintains automated analytical processes to accommodate advanced reporting and analytical needs
  • Interprets results, identifies trends and issues, and develops alternatives as needed to support business objectives
  • Analyzes large quantities of data to extract impactful insights to help inform senior leadership/ customer experience teams and drive strategic client decisions
  • Develops visualizations, presentations, reports, and proposals for business uses
  • Presents results to internal and possibly external clients

Competencies:

  • Good technical writing skills
  • Ability to communicate analyses in a simple, actionable way.
  • Ability to create data visualizations to communicate analysis results in the most effective way possible for a given project or study.
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Experience with machine learning and AI
  • Coding skills in SAS, Python, R, SQL
  • Familiarity with Azure ML or Synapse a plus
  • Knowledge of Cloud technologies such as Azure, Google, or AWS a plus
  • Ability to work independently and with team members from different backgrounds.
  • Good attention to detail

Requirements:

  • Minimum of 2 years of relevant experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in a quantitative field such as data science, statistics, operations research, quantitative social science, applied mathematics, information systems preferred not required
  • Basic knowledge on software development principles and architecture.
  • Good analytical and problem-solving abilities.
  • Experience with programming for data analysis; ideally SAS, SQL, Azure ML, Python, R.
  • Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI.
  • Solid oral and written communication skills, especially around analytical concepts and methods.
  • Strong work ethic and intellectual curiosity.
  • Must be a team player with excellent communication and problem-solving skills and have experience working with customers across all levels.
