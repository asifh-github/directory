Full Job Description

As a Data Acquisition Analyst at Frost, you would:

  • Provide thought leadership and guidance in deriving value from the organizations' data resources through the effective integration of data warehouse technology and knowledge
  • Enable the acquisition and conversion of data into insights that support key business initiatives that impact LOB and customer relationships
  • Act as a liaison in projects or task forces to provide direction, expertise, and support to Data Warehouse Analysts and the Data Community
  • Facilitate ingestion of external data feeds into the Data Warehouse to supplement current Data Warehouse assets such as credit bureaus, county and state agencies, or third-party vendors
  • Function as a liaison between end users and vendors to determine business needs and collaborate on workable solutions
  • Establish standards for requirements gathering, coding, and testing of Data Warehouse deliverables
  • Provide expertise in a variety of SQL tools and techniques to improve the performance of critical applications and queries
  • Provide guidance to less experienced Data Acquisition Analysts and ETL programmers
  • Always take action using Integrity, Caring, and Excellence to achieve all-win outcomes

To be successful, you will need:

  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Data Analytics, Statistics or MIS discipline
  • 2+ years of experience in data analytics
  • Advanced knowledge and skill in SQL tools and techniques
  • Experience working in agile teams
  • Experience in the banking industry
  • Strong organizational, analytical, and interpersonal skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft computer applications

An exceptional candidate might have:

  • Competency with data acquisition methodologies and concepts
  • Competency with metadata creation and maintenance
  • Competency with Advanced Analytics or data science
