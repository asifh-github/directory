Full Job Description

As an intern, you'll have access to our data, and you'll have the resources and independence to delve into problems that you find interesting in addition to assigned projects. Thanks to our robust engineering infrastructure, you'll get to spend less time data munging and more time science-ing.

THE ROLE

As a data science intern, you will be collaborating with cross-functional teams of biologists, chemists, software engineers, and data scientists to help improve our understanding of the biology present in our imaging data. We will give you real-world data to work with, and expect you to come up with creative approaches to solve the problems you're presented with. You will work closely with both your intern mentor and the rest of the data science team.

Our Intern program run for 3 months and can start any time, depending on your availability.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Passion for the company mission and comfort with answering open-ended questions
  • Ability to brainstorm and communicate with cross-functional teams
  • Strong foundation of statistics, probability, and machine learning
  • Strong programming experience, preferably in Python
  • Experience working with large, high-dimensional datasets (preferred)
  • Knowledge of biology/life sciences (preferred)
  • Projected graduation from degree program takes place after the completion of your internship

THE DATA YOU'LL WORK WITH

  • Fully automated experimental protocols
  • Images and extracted features from > 10 billion cells
  • >9 petabytes
  • Hundreds of thousands of biological and chemical perturbations
  • Clean and easily loaded
  • More info and a small (for us) public release can be found here

PERKS YOU'LL ENJOY

  • 3 months of full-time, paid internship
  • Ongoing mentoring and training from our Data Science team

More About Recursion

Central to our mission is the Recursion Operating System, or Recursion OS, that combines an advanced infrastructure layer to generate what we believe is one of the world's largest and fastest-growing proprietary biological and chemical datasets and the Recursion Map, a suite of custom software, algorithms, and machine learning tools that we use to explore foundational biology unconstrained by human bias and navigate to new biological insights which may accelerate our programs. We are a biotechnology company scaling more like a technology company. Recursion is proudly headquartered in Salt Lake City.
