Overview: The Data Scientist position involves analyzing complex business problems, uncovering insights, and identifying growth opportunities using data science, machine learning, data mining, visualization, and statistical analysis. The Data Scientist will collaborate with cross functional teams and external partners to discover and model operational efficiencies that will enable Master Electronics to better serve its customers.
Responsibilities:

  • Utilize statistical approaches to build predictive models
  • Develop quantitative analysis, ad hoc reports, and statistical models to support critical business decisions
  • Independently solve analytical problems, effectively communicating results to non-technical audiences
  • Monitor and maintain production data pipelines and machine learning models
  • Effectively partner with teams to understand business requirements and the desired outcomes of their analytical needs
  • Ability to effectively answer a wide variety of high business impact questions, while presenting key data science and quantitative insights in a concise and effective manner to a variety of audiences
  • Have a high degree of creativity and independence, using a variety of data science algorithms, integrating a wide number of structured and non-structure data sources, to model and simulate effective solutions to challenging business issues
