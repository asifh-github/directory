Description and Requirements

Lenovo Infrastructure Solutions Group (ISG) is a smart infrastructure solutions provider to organizations of all sizes. We are looking for an eager and passionate Summer 2023 Intern to join our team as a Data Scientist. In this role you will work with the Software & Solutions Organization to analyze presales testing data. You will work with Senior level Executives and be tasked with supporting theories and outcomes. This role will allow you to gain hands on experience within leading edge go to market development. The successful candidate will be a self-starter who is passionate about learning and enjoys math & analytics. This role is based out of Morrisville, NC.

Requirements:

  • Pursing a Bachelor’s degree in Data Science or related field
  • Experience with open source programing such as R/Java/Go
  • Experience with zero code
  • Familiar with front end development such as JavaScript/HTML/CSS
  • Ability to work through the SDLC (Agile & Waterfall)
  • Ability to hypothesize and defend work
