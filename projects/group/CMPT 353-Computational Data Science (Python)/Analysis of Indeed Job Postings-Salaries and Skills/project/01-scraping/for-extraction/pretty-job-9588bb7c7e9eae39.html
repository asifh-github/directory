Full Job Description

At Modern Hire, we are innovators transforming hiring experiences around the globe. We believe in the power of science and data to create technologies that have real impact, and we believe in getting it right. We are trusted by nearly half of the Fortune 100 and more than 700 companies worldwide for seriously better hiring.

Join us and Change Hiring. For Good.

As a Data Science intern on our Modern Hire labs team you will have the ability to support ongoing research initiatives that keep our products and our clients hiring processes on the cutting edge. Partnering with our data scientists, you will have the opportunity to work with and create machine learning and deep learning models along with the opportunity to conduct research on systems and processes to continuously improve our natural language processing capabilities. This is a great opportunity to continue to develop and showcase your skills and help our labs team change hiring for good.

What you'll do as a Data Science Intern:

  • Support ongoing research initiatives that keep us on the cutting edge
  • Work with machine learning and deep learning models and conduct research on natural language processing
  • Drive the preparation of data used to train deep learning models and score unstructured data
  • Prototype code and processes to improve our capabilities
  • Assure quality of our software in terms of content, scoring, and functionality
  • Performance modeling and data analysis

If you can answer "yes" to the following, then we want you to apply!

  • Do you have a Bachelor’s degree with a Master’s degree or PhD in progress in a quantitative and programming focused field such as Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, Psychology, Economics, Data Science, or other related field?
  • Do you have an understanding of statistics and machine learning/deep learning?
  • Are you aware of the latest research in machine learning, deep learning, and/or NLP?
  • Do you have an understanding of technology and the ability to be adaptable to new systems as they are introduced within the company?
  • Do you have a experience with Python?
  • Do you have a experience with version control processes?
  • Do you have the ability to analyze big data and draw meaningful insights?
  • Do you practice active listening and learning skills – always processing what others are saying, asking questions for clarification, and update your work based on things learned?
  • Do you have a proven ability to solve complex problems – take in information from multiple sources and apply them to solve problems?
  • Do you have an ability to prioritize; arrange tasks in an order so that everything is done on time?
  • Do you have a high level of creativity; necessary to work with data scientists and I/O Psychologists and come up with new ideas for assessments?
  • Are you a team player; do you have the ability to work in a highly collaborative environment to share your ideas and listen to those of others?
  • Are you self-motivated; can apply yourself to accomplishing a task without supervision?
  • Do you enjoy processing information; verifying, calculating, categorizing, and coding data?
  • Do you practice deductive reasoning; using general statements to reach a logical conclusion?
