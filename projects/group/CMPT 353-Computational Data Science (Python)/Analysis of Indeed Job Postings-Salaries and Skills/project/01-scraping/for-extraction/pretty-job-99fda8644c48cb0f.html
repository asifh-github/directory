ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
Position Summary:
The Data Scientist will work with the Data Engineering team, on challenging opportunities such as: working with the operations team to understand the decisions they would like to make by leveraging data to improve Back of the House operations, and building a data warehouse for OTG with initial pipeline of product, flights, and weather data.
Responsibilities:
  • Work with the Data Engineering team to design and develop data models, ETL pipelines and Data Science projects (such as recommendation engines)
  • Take responsibility for the planning, execution, and success of complex data projects
  • Contribute to projects in the form of writing code, debugging, architecting, designing, and maintaining data and analytics platforms
  • Work along with software, product management, IT, and operations teams to ensure we are building and delivering the best products for OTG

Qualifications and Requirements:
  • 3+ years of experience in Data Science, advanced analytics, or similar experience in applying statistical methods and machine learning techniques with high levels of proficiency.
  • Degree (Bachelors) with a specialization in Statistics, Computer Science, Data Science, Economics, Mathematics, Operations Research or other quantitative field or equivalent.
  • Mastery of SQL, Python (and / or R) and other scripting/automation techniques.
  • Experience with AWS, analytics tools and building data models.
  • MS in Computer Science or related degree.
  • 3+ years of hands-on technical experience in a Data Science role.
  • Deep knowledge and experience with building Machine learning models.
  • Experience with ETL/ELT data pipelines.
  • Excellent understanding of database data models designs and principals.
