Full Job Description

The Fraunhofer USA Center Mid Atlantic (CMA) is seeking an applied researcher in software engineering with solid background in computer science and experience with software and mobile app development for applied research and experimentation. We are seeking a motivated individual with innovative technical ideas to contribute to the growth of our Center while revolutionizing industry and research in the field of app-supported experimentation. CMA has acquired several projects in the space of applied research related to automation of shellfish farming, health information personalization, RFID scanning and management, detection of defects in software requirements, and experimentation related to AI-Human interactions that this position will support, and therefore background and experience with one or more of these domains is highly prioritized. This position also serves a key role in our knowledge acquisition and dissemination program and involves researching and evaluating new technologies and presenting results and recommendations to staff as well as clients. Our clients range from private companies to federal customers such as NASA, DARPA, IARPA, NIH, and NSF.

Past work experience is required in:

  • Development of scientific solutions and prototyping ideas with cutting-edge technologies combining computer science, software, data science, machine learning into systems related to the projects listed above.
  • Researching, analyzing, and improving software and associated data on the projects listed above.
  • Collaboration with PhDs, principal investigators, project managers, universities and industry on applied research projects
  • Modern development and testing tools to maximize automation, e.g. copilot and cypress.
  • Successfully working with and understanding the needs of software users related to the projects listed above.

. Documented ability to quickly research, evaluate and present new technologies.

Required Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated to be excellent programmer with skills in languages such as C++, Java, JavaScript, typescript, and Python.
  • Skilled in using libraries and frameworks such as react native, flask, DynamoDB, SQLAlchemy, electron, Vuetify, spacy, and nltk, UI-Kitten
  • At least a BSc in Computer Science or Software Engineering or similar.
  • Aptitude to understand research and software related to related to the projects listed above
  • Publications in empirical studies in software engineering
  • Working knowledge of developing tools for detecting defects in requirements, developing software for RFID scanners, monitoring of empirical studies in software engineering etc.

This is an onsite position.

Located adjacent to the University of Maryland and the College Park metro station, Fraunhofer USA Center Mid Atlantic (CMA) is a non-profit applied research and technology transfer organization affiliated with the University of Maryland. CMA is part of Fraunhofer USA, Inc., collaborates as a partner to Fraunhofer-Gesellschaft, and conducts joint research with the University of Maryland. Our mission is to advance real-world software practices via empirically validated research into software engineering technologies and processes.
