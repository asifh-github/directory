The Data scientist will use data tools to drive deep understanding of companys lead data with the goal of improving our ability to convert more leads into opportunities. They will help solve challenging problems using data science techniques.

  • Job Category: Entry to senior level positions available.
  • Work locations: In office, remote, hybrid schedules available.
  • Salary Range: $60,000 - $200,000 commensurate with experience and education.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Analyze data to extract valuable insights with goal to improve marketing techniques, and increase sales
  • Help the team develop analytics on the entire business cycle from Lead generation to Installation
  • Design and build predictive customer behavior models
  • Support leadership teams to help make better decisions.
  • Identify data to be collected and devise methods to extract the information and develop a process to transform raw data into business knowledge.
  • Lead the development of predictive models and machine-learning algorithms.
  • Enhance data collection procedures by assessing the effectiveness and accuracy of data sources and data gathering techniques.

Qualifications & Experience:

  • MS/PhD degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or related technical experience.
  • Industry experience working with data and building statistical models and developing algorithm.
  • Knowledge of best practices and new techniques in data science.
  • Experience in statistics, data mining and predictive modeling.
  • Solid written and verbal communication skills.
  • Self-driven, pro-active.

About Trinity Solar

Since 1994, Trinity Solar has proudly provided energy with integrity to families in our area. Trinity started in the solar industry with two core fundamental principles, to make renewable energy accessible and to make the solar journey seamless. These guiding beliefs have led Trinity Solar to become the United States largest privately-held residential solar installer, composed of more than 1,700 team members and boasting over 70,000 installations. Currently, Trinity offers solar installation in Connecticut, Delaware, Florida, New Jersey, New York, Massachusetts, Maryland, Pennsylvania, and Rhode Island.
