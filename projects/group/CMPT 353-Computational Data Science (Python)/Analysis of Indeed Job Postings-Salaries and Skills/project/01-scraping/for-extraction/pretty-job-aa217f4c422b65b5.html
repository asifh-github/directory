Your Work Shapes the World

Whether it be groundbreaking products, best in class solutions or creating a lifelong career, you can do the work that matters at Caterpillar. With a 95-year legacy of quality and innovation and 150 locations in countries around the world, your impact spans the globe.

When you join Caterpillar, you are joining a team of makers, innovators and doers. We are the people who roll up our sleeves and do the work to build a better world. We don’t just talk about progress and innovation. We make it happen. And we are proud of that because it helps our customers build and power the world we live in – the roads, hospitals, homes and infrastructure. Without a dedicated workforce Caterpillar could not effectively meet our customer’s needs.

The Caterpillar Enterprise Strategy Group is seeking a Commercial Data Engineer to manage aggregation and consumption of multiple data sets throughout the enterprise. This position will effectively apply automation and ETL techniques to combine, visualize, and continuously improve data analysis in support of our enterprise metrics and goals. The Commercial Data Engineer offers a unique learning and development experience by supporting a variety of projects spanning various Caterpillar industries and our dealer network.

Job Responsibilities:
  • Manage existing and develop new data connections
  • Report and explain data flow metrics and discrepancies
  • Manage, develop, and continuously improve data visualization suite
  • Analyze and interpret data to provide data-driven analysis to projects
Basic Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in a business or quantitative field
  • 4+ years’ experience in data engineering or data management
  • Relational database experience (SQL)
Top Candidates Will Also Have:
  • Data visualization experience (PowerBI, Tableau, etc.)
  • Analytical Programming experience (Python, R, etc.)
  • ETL tool experience (Alteryx, DataStage, etc.)
  • Attention to detail
  • Strong communication skills
  • Intellectual curiosity
