Internship Summary: We are actively hiring for a Data Science Intern! As an intern you will work with our data science team on innovation projects focused on artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Essential responsibilities

  • Collaborate with our data science team and business leads to pursue research in areas of high business impact.

  • Drive CSAA’s knowledge of our customers through research projects from data collection to model development.

  • Provide recommendation based on well-documented, reproducible analysis from large datasets.

  • Work on a capstone project throughout the summer related to data analysis within the insurance industry and present findings to senior management

Ideal Qualifications

  • Current undergraduate or graduate student pursuing a quantitative or analytically focused degree

  • Rising juniors and seniors ideally graduating December 2023 or later

  • Interested to begin a career with a focus on data or statistical analysis.

  • Familiar with manipulating raw data with Python or R.

  • Creative problem solver with a strong interest in machine learning.

  • High-energy, high-impact individual who can provide statistical insights with data

  • Self-motivated and results-oriented professional with strong written or verbal communication skills.

  • Able to influence and effectively manage multiple projects while communicating effectively in front of others

  • Enthusiastic to learn new concepts and committed to self-development

  • Has a keen analytical mind with a sharp eye for details
