Job Title: Summer 2023 Data Analyst Co-Op/Intern

JOB LOCATION: Austin, Texas

ONSITE: This is an onsite position – the intern/co-op is required to relocate if necessary and work full-time (40 hours) from the location office.

PROGRAM TERM: Summer 2023 (May 15 or May 22, 2023 – August 11, 2023)

WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING:

As a Co-op/Intern you can get hands-on experience and make an immediate contribution to AMD's next generation of technology innovations. You will have unique opportunities to develop your career, connect with AMD leaders, receive one-on-one mentorship, attend amazing networking events, and much more.

The Global Operation Business Intelligence team is responsible for data analytics, reporting and dashboard creation across multiple business domains within among Global Operations. We work with our business partners to integrate and automate data into insight for our customer.

This position will provide you with an excellent steppingstone to advanced graduate and/or industry work opportunities.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Work on an agile team of data analysts building reports and dashboards.
  • Collect requirements from business stakeholders to prioritize development work.
  • Communicate specific business needs to IT resources for ongoing projects.
  • Document best practice and process flow.
  • Help resolve production related issues.

KEY QUALIFICATIONS:
  • SQL, Python programming language
  • Database design and data modeling
  • Snowflake, SQL Server data platform
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of data

WHAT WOULD SET YOU APART:
  • Data warehousing and ETL pipeline knowledge
  • Data Engineering background

WHO WE’RE LOOKING FOR:

  • Senior (BS) or (MS) student at an accredited university - Information technology, Software Development or Engineering.
  • Attention to detail and accuracy; good interpersonal and written communication
  • Be flexible and adaptable to the ongoing needs of the business
  • Drive to succeed, we expect our team members to engage with a passion!

BENEFITS:

  • Healthcare coverage, dental and vision
  • Paid holidays
  • Relocation stipend
  • Education assistance for required Co-op/Intern course
