About Lowe’s Innovation Labs:
Lowe’s Innovation Labs (www.lowesinnovationlabs.com) imagines, explores, and accelerates the future of home improvement retail. Our work supports Lowe’s ability to thrive in a future where the digital and physical worlds seamlessly blend. Our work ranges from 3D visualization, AI/ML, robotics, digital twins, and other things we can’t talk about right now.

Job Summary:
Lowe’s is undergoing a transformation – and at the center of it is a new experience between people and their homes through emerging data and technologies.
The Lowe’s Innovation Labs team plays a critical role in driving this transformation.
Home improvement is complex. Most home improvement projects require a fundamental understanding of the space in your home – before you even get started.

And if you’re like us, much of the critical info about your home is all over the place, written down and stashed in a folder, or simply nonexistent. We’re building a future that leverages the rise of emerging technologies like LiDAR, computer vision, AI, and mixed reality hardware: technologies that will not only sense and understand your home’s space, but will also put all of that data into your hands in useful ways whenever you need it, wherever you are.
We’re looking for a Data Scientist to partner with our Data and Machine Learning Engineers to build and validate datasets and data annotation strategies. The Data Scientist will develop, train, and refine machine learning models based on performance and feedback.
This is a role for a person interested in Data Science and ML, who has a solid engineering foundation, a hunger for learning new technologies, and is a strong communicator, innovator, and collaborator.
What you’ll do:
  • Identify and validate business use cases that can be solved with AI
  • Analyze and visualize data at all stages of the modeling pipeline
  • Design and develop custom algorithms and data science models
  • Identify additional datasets for use in models and algorithms
  • Develop custom tools and libraries to optimize the data science modeling workflow

Minimum Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s Degree in a Computer Science related field, or equivalent industry experience
  • 5+ years of applicable experience in software engineering
  • 3+ years of experience with statistical models, machine learning and analysis
  • Ability to write code in Python, Java, R, or similar language
  • Expert proficiency with multiple Data Science related technologies, e.g. Fast.ai, OpenCV, HuggingFace, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Jupyter, Scikit-Learn, Pandas, JAX
  • Experience with database and storage solutions, e.g. Postgres, GraphQL, Google Cloud Storage, Microsoft Azure
  • Strong Computer Science and programming fundamentals
  • Understanding of data science, analytics, visualization, supervised and unsupervised Machine Learning
  • Ability to communicate effectively with engineers, product managers, and stakeholders in both business and technical contexts

About Lowe’s:
Lowe’s Companies, Inc. (NYSE: LOW) is a FORTUNE® 50 home improvement company serving approximately 20 million customers a week in the United States and Canada. With fiscal year 2020 sales of nearly $90 billion, Lowe’s and its related businesses operate or service more than 2,200 home improvement and hardware stores and employ over 300,000 associates. Based in Mooresville, N.C., Lowe’s supports the communities it serves through programs focused on creating safe, affordable housing and helping to develop the next generation of skilled trade experts. For more information, visit Lowes.com.
