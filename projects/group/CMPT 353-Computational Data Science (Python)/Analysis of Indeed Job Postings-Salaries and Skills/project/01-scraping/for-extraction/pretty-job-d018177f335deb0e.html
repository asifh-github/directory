2023 AI & DATA SCIENCE FULL TIME ANALYST PROGRAM

Delivering excellence at the intersection of data science, research and industry expertise, our AI, Machine Learning and Data Analytics teams go beyond where any bank has gone before. We ask the questions and find the answers to create solutions and technology that help to solve the world's most pressing problems, improving and protecting our customer and client experiences every day. Whether you're working with artificial intelligence, big data, machine learning or robotics, our entrepreneurial team environment challenges you to push the limits of your expertise in the pursuit of impactful and commercial real-world applications.

Our areas of focus include Big Data Analytics, Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Natural Language Processing, Speech/Voice Analytics, Time Series, Computer Vision, Cryptography, Explainability and Interpretability.

About You

Our team of global experts is always working to keep us ahead of the curve. That's why we're looking for fresh thinking like yours-to design, build, deploy and run tomorrow's innovative technology and analytics. Key Qualifications include:

  • Pursuing a Bachelor's in Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM) with an expected graduation of December 2022 - August 2023.
  • Knowledge of machine learning / data science theory, techniques and tools
  • Experience in mathematics and statistics modelling
  • Programming experience with one or more of Python, R, C++, Java, C#
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to present findings to a non-technical audience
  • Authorization to work permanently in the U.S.
