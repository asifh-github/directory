What we're building and why we're building it.

Fetch is a build-first technology company creating a rewards program to power the world. Over the last 5 years we've grown from 0 to 18M active users and taken over the rewards game in the US with our free app. The foundation has been laid. In the next 5 years we will become a global platform that completely transforms how people connect with brands.

It all comes down to two core beliefs. First, that people deserve to be rewarded when they create value. If a third party directly benefits from an action you take or data you provide, you should be rewarded for it. And not just the "you get to use our product!" cop-out. We're talkin' real, explicit value. Fetch points, perhaps.

Second, we also believe brands need a better and more direct connection with what matters most to them: their customers. - Brands need to understand what people are doing, and have a direct line to be able to do something about it. Not just advertise, but ACT. Sounds nice right?

That's why we're building the world's rewards platform. A closed-loop, standardized rewards layer across all consumer behavior that will lead to happier shoppers and stronger brands.

Fetch Rewards is an equal employment opportunity employer.

The Role:

The Data Science & Analytics team embodies these values and works with a laser focused objective to enable data driven decision making for both internal stakeholders and external partners. We are looking for a Deep Learning Specialist who can seize upon industry and academic research breakthroughs and transfer those results to real-world systems.

You will develop new machine learning algorithms that will help Fetch achieve scale on solving a diverse and challenging set of problems, which include fraud detection, UX personalization, A/B testing, data quality, document understanding, and more. Success in this role requires the ability to translate business needs to areas of ML research such as recommender systems, auto encoders, and graph neural networks, among others. This hire will be expected to implement these models with a high standard of technical quality, as the goal is to deploy these models to power Fetch's products.

The ideal candidate:

  • PhD or Master's with equivalent experience in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science or any other Quantitative field. Publication record is a plus.
  • 2+ years of work experience in data science
  • Fluency in Python and at least one ML framework (Tensorflow, PyTorch, JAX, Keras, etc)
  • Experience developing + deploying a ML model based on tabular data, such as Recommender Systems, Anomaly Detection, Bandit testing, Graph Networks.
  • Entrepreneurial and big-picture bend of mind for applications of machine learning in a consumer tech world
  • A penchant for fast prototyping and deployment and ability to pivot quickly to pursue impactful solutions
  • Curiosity and passion for solving new problems with creative approaches
  • Ability to successfully collaborate with both business users and engineers in solution development, deployment, and maintenance
  • Bonus points for:
    • Experience in CPG/Retail domain and/or analytics at app-based B2C companies
    • Familiarity with Big Data frameworks like Snowflake, Google Cloud, and AWS services
    • Effective communication, ability to translate and explain technical issues to non-technical team members
