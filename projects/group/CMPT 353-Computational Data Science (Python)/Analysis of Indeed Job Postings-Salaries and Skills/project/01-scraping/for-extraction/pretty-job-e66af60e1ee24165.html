WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR
Our Data Analyst will be a self-starter, flexible problem solver, and have a deep curiosity for learning new and evolving techniques, exploring solutions and delivering results. You will have a curiosity about pattern recognition, connecting the dots between disparate methodologies and data sources. You will be an excellent communicator with the ability to quickly build trust with colleagues and cross-functional partners.

We are looking for an individual who is a critical thinker that thrives in a data driven environment. You will have a passion for finding answers to ever changing problems, challenging norms with innovative solutions to help deliver a world class digital loss prevention for Nike.



WHAT YOU WILL WORK ON
This role will provide support for Nike Direct Digital Loss Prevention for NA territories. You will use data to reduce risk while reducing friction for Nike consumers. You will be encouraged to learn and deliver insights and solutions to improve operational efficiency. You will be considered a SME within the department and will be able to provide mentorship and assistance to internal and cross-functional teammates.



WHO YOU WILL WORK WITH
You will be part of North America Nike Digital Direct Operations, working collaboratively with Nike Digital Commerce, Nike Direct, and external partners.

The role will partner closely with peers who are leading advanced analytics and data product delivery teams to ensure that requirements from key partners are being translated to analytical capabilities, You will apply various data modeling, visualization and analysis techniques to mine data to identify trends that suggest new ways to mitigate fraud.


WHAT YOU BRING
  • Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science, Data Science, Mathematics, Economics, or related field or equivalent combination of education and experience
  • Experience with Data Visualization Platforms (Tableau preferred)
  • Familiarity with analytics tools (Spark, Git, Jupyter) and cloud computing platforms (AWS preferred)
  • Advanced SQL, Python or similar experience in analyzing large structured and unstructured datasets
  • Experience with Snowflake, Databricks and working with moderate to large-scale data sets (>100GB) will be a plus
  • Understanding of web development, API calls, Splunk, Airflow is not necessary but will be useful
  • Remote Work Option – Open to remote work except cannot work in the following locations: SD,VT, and WV. These candidates will be required to relocate.
