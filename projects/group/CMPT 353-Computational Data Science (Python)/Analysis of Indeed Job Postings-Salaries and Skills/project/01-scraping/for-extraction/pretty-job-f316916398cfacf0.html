Position Summary:

The Jr. Data Analyst completes various organization and planning tasks to maintain high performance of the Company’s distribution operations, shipment processes and transportation execution. The selected candidate will work with the Sr. Data Analyst to help create and optimize business applicable dashboards and internal reporting. Candidate must be able to analyze data to see where improvements can be made and any potential gaps. Successfully demonstrate accountability, flexibility and adaptability to handle multiple and changing priorities and be able to successfully collaborate with development teams, technology groups, and stakeholders.

Required Qualification:

  • Bachelors, Masters, or Doctoral degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Statistics, Finance, Mathematics, Economics, Data Science, or any other related quantitative field (preferred but not required).
  • 1 to 2 years’ experience in a data analytics and/or data science role, using quantitative analysis to solve challenging, real-world problems (background in e-commerce, supply chain, finance, or pricing is a plus).
  • Basic knowledge of SQL Server Manager Studio (preferred but not required).
  • Basic background in Forecasting and Financial Analytic (preferred but not required).
  • Basic analytical understanding of, and ability to transform and integrate, existing data sets.
  • Proficiency in Excel, Power Query, and Power BI, including proficiency in Power BI tools suite and publishing reports/data to internal systems and to the web with an understanding of security and access to the reports.
  • Transportation shipment optimization or operations research ideal.
  • Excellent problem solving and analytical skills.
  • Good organizational, prioritization and project management skills.
  • Must be able to work within a team environment.
  • Must be able to effectively interact and communicate with all levels of management and employees within the company.
  • Possess a basic knowledge of business operations (principles, practices, and procedures.)
  • Excellent written, oral communication and presentation skills.

  • Continuous Improvement: Be willing to continuously learn and implement ideas for process improvement in a forever changing environment.
  • Working with a team to oversee Power BI dashboard development and maintenance; support various data analytics projects to drive decision-making and results; ensure dashboards and reports are clear and compelling.
  • Have excellent communication skills and Share analyses and project insights to peers and senior leadership to enhance interpretation of results and promote data-based decision-making. Have the ability to convey complex topics through effective documentation as well as presentation by being able to explain results to internal and external stakeholders.
  • Some ability to coordinate with IT to clean and merge data from multiple sources.
  • Proactively identify/suggest areas for additional analysis and opportunities for self-service analytics.
  • Some ability to design, develop, and maintain centralized data repository and metric dashboards.
  • Own assigned tasks and resolve general problems with minimal guidance. Act as subject matter expert.
