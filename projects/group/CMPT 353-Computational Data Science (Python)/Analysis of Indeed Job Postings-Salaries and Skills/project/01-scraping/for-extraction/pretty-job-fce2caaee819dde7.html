Built on Willis Carrier's invention of modern air conditioning in 1902, Carrier is a leading provider of heating, ventilating, air conditioning, and refrigeration systems, building controls, and automation, and fire and security systems leading to safer, smarter, sustainable, and high-performance buildings. We constantly build upon our history of proven innovation with new products and services that improve global comfort and efficiency. Today, Carrier needs your talent to build upon this legacy and create solutions that matter for people and our planet!
We are seeking a motivated, results-oriented college student to join our team for the summer of 2023. The Carrier internship experience offers interns an environment that provides practical, challenging, and meaningful on-the-job work experience, and the opportunity to apply knowledge and demonstrate strengths in efforts to assist Carrier in achieving its business strategy.
As a Data Science Intern you will gain experience to include, but not limited to, the following activities and tasks:
  • Working with a multi-disciplinary team to develop high-priority Machine Learning-enabled product features that improve the lives of our customers. The individual will work across a broad portfolio of Carrier digital products, including Abound, a cloud-native platform that unlocks siloed building data to create smarter and more resilient spaces that improve occupant wellness
  • Assisting with machine learning initiatives as part of the Digital Foundry team.
  • Engaging stakeholders across the organization to identify and prioritize projects.
  • Assisting with building data science workflows from the ground up using AWS Services.
  • Collaborating cross-functionally across different disciplines, including engineering, design, and research. Focusing on predictive and prescriptive data science-enabled product features.
Experience/Qualifications
  • A proactive and results-driven individual who thrives in a fast-paced and dynamic environment
  • Excellent analytical and communication skills
  • Preferred strong technical skills in any of the following: UI design, software development, data analytics, data science, Cloud infrastructure, networking, or cybersecurity
  • Relevant internships or co-op experiences within Information Technology or Engineering functions
  • Team player with a positive, collaborative work ethic
  • Digitally savvy with an interest in cutting-edge digital trends
