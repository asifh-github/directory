Full Job Description

Tenneco is one of the world’s leading designer, manufacturers and marketers of automotive products for original equipment and aftermarket customers, with approximately 78.000 team members working at more than 300 sites worldwide. Through our four business groups, Motorparts, Ride Performance, Clean Air and Powertrain, Tenneco is driving advancements in global mobility by delivering technology solutions for diversified global markets, including light vehicle, commercial truck, off-highway, industrial, motorsport and the aftermarket.


We are looking for a talented student to join our Regional and Global Analytic teams in a full-time remote capacity. The analytic interns are responsible for transformative Analytics within the enterprise. This intern will:

  • Own project plans and deliver for large analytics projects
  • Communicate results to the business audience using static and dynamic graphic and tabular displays of data, written reports, and presentations
  • Manage a significant initiative and prioritize deliverables according to business needs
  • Collaborate with cross functional teams including but not limited to IT, Finance, Trade, Operations, and Product Development to support the needs of the business segment
  • Collaborate with business partners to develop new approaches to meet business goals using analytics and effectively communicating your analytics approach

Required Skills

  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills to work across multiple groups within the organization
  • Ability to resolve complex problems and break them down to analyze possible solutions
  • Ability to advocate for analytics approach to business problems where use of data may be scarce
  • Organized approach to project work and engagement with the business
  • Proficient in Sequel Query Language (SQL)
  • Proficient in PowerBI (preferred) or Tableau

Required Experience

  • Pursuing a Bachelor’s degree or Master’s Degree in Data Science, Data Analytics, Computer Science, Statistics, or related field
  • Must be a Junior/Senior Undergraduate or Graduate student
  • Focus on analytics, creative problem solving
  • Proven understanding of Excel for school projects
