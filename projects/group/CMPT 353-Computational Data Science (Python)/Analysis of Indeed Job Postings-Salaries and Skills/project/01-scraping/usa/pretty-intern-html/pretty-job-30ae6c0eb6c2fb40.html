Technology Intern - Technology Summer Internship Program

Chubb is looking for individuals passionate about technology to join our Technology Summer Internship Program. Our Technology Department consists of a number of disciplines where we will have internship opportunities including:

Application Development (Full-Stack)

Security

Data Analytics

Infrastructure

Data Engineering

Process Automation

Project Management and Scrum

Software Engineering

Cloud Engineering

Duck Creek Engineering

UX/UI Design

The IT summer internship will run from June through August and is offered in several locations. It will provide the opportunity to participate in one of the above disciplines and gain an awareness of the systems, projects, and technologies in a corporate technology organization.

What about you?

You are a highly collaborative, creative, and intellectually curious individual who is passionate about technology.

You have the ability to learn technical concepts quickly.

You are organized and able to adapt to change.

You are a natural leader and work well independently and in a team.

You are a self-starter and motivated to learn and succeed.

You pay attention to the details without losing sight of the big picture.

Qualifications - External

Pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Data Science, or related field with GPA of 3.0+ required.

Ability to work effectively with key stakeholders in a fast paced, multi-national work environment.

Strong time management and organizational skills.

Possess strong verbal and written communication skills.

Ability to present ideas and information to team, leaders, and peers.
