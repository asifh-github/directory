Position Summary
A career in Data Analytics at South Jersey Industries will provide the opportunity to design, develop, test, deploy, and monitor reports, dashboards, and databases. The Data Analytics Intern will gain hands-on exposure with data visualization, data warehousing, and data mining. This intern will learn to apply data analytics tools and technologies and assist the Data Services team with projects and initiatives. As part of the comprehensive internship program, interns will participate in events which help them build a strong network within the SJI community and with fellow interns. Interns enjoy the flexibility of a hybrid work environment throughout this 11-week summer program. The Ignite Internship Program also offers participants mentorship, educational seminars, volunteer opportunities, and visibility within the organization.
Essential Functions:

  • Document user requirements
  • Participate in development and testing of Power BI reports and dashboards
  • Develop ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) jobs with Informatica Integration Cloud
  • Create routines to enable data mining
  • Prepare and collaborate on a cross-functional project with the opportunity to present to the SJI Executive Leadership Team
  • Regular meetings with assigned mentor and various organizational leaders to discuss goals and professional development
