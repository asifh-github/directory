This position is located in the Cincinnati office. Candidates in Ithaca NY may also be considered. Due to current COVID19 restrictions, temporary remote work is required.

Position Summary

The Data Science Intern will have opportunities to gain knowledge and experience in developing, enhancing, and implementing predictive models and data methodologies for syndicated and custom targeting solutions. The Data Science Intern will be able to work with a collaborative team of skilled and experienced Data Scientists, Data Analysts, and Data Engineers to create quality data products and deliver useful insights for Claritas clients.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Work with and manipulate data, and develop models and analyze raw data from a variety of sources
  • Conduct and define in-depth analysis to make business recommendations
  • Help provide sound methodology and approaches for data product enhancement and new product development
  • Assist in data product updates and quality checking using statistical analysis and Claritas proprietary methods
