The Opportunity
The Adobe Data Science team is looking for summer interns. The team applies AI and machine learning techniques to big customer data to address and challenge business problems, such as driving go-to-market strategies, sales prioritization, increasing product engagement and customer service improvement.

All 2023 Adobe Interns will be ‘co-located hybrid’, meaning that you will be assigned to an Adobe office location but in-office schedules will be flexible (2-3 days per week). All interns must live in the same state, country, and within commuting distance of their assigned Adobe office so they can be on-site as needed. Interns visiting Adobe sites must follow all applicable COVID-19 health and safety protocols.

What You’ll Do
Work with cross-functional partners in developing topline metrics ; performing exploratory analysis on customer, marketing and sales data, quantifying impacts for any product improvement or launch; and build & own analytical frameworks that guide the financial roadmap
Extract and analyze data to understand the customer product life cycle and value realization from the product
Help various businesses and teams achieve operational and execution efficiencies using data What You Need to Succeed
Currently enrolled full time and pursuing a Master’s in Statistics, Mathematics, or Engineering is desired; or equivalent experience required
Solid understanding of SQL and R/Python
Understanding of statistical modeling/machine learning/ data mining concepts, and a track record solving problems with these methods.
Ability to apply statistical/machine learning techniques such as hypothesis testing, time series forecasting, linear and logistic regression, random forests, etc.
