Responsibilities:
This internship provides students the opportunity to join one of our engineering teams where you will have the opportunity to:
  • Participate in the development of cutting-edge machine learning technologies in scientific areas like biology, physics, and materials.
  • Following SOTA methods, gains deep understanding of broad areas in scientific area.
  • Develop and test new ideas, end-to-end solving scientific problems.
  • Currently pursuing a Undergraduate/Graduate/Master/PhD in Machine Learning, Computer Science or a related technical discipline
  • Able to commit to working for 12 weeks during Summer 2023
  • Must obtain work authorization in country of employment at the time of hire, and maintain ongoing work authorization during employment.
  • Familiar with many deep learning algorithms (CNN/RNN/LSTM/Transformer, etc.).
  • Proficient in python or C/C++, and have solid programming skills.
  • Familiar with at least one of the mainstream deep learning frameworks, like TensorFlow or PyTorch.
  • Able to solve problems independently, good teamwork and communication skills.
Preferred Qualifications
  • Graduating December 2023 onwards with intent to return to degree-program after the completion of the internship.
  • Demonstrated software engineering experience from previous internship, work experience, coding competitions, or publications
  • High levels of creativity and quick problem-solving capabilities
  • Experience in applying machine learning to scientific areas such as biology, materials, battery, quantum chemistry or molecular simulation.
  • Experience with distributed computing, large-scale machine learning pipelines.
  • Experience working as a core developer of existing large-scale deep learning research pipelines.
