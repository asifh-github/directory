Full Job Description

ULTRA MOBILE is seeking a talented Data Scientist II to join the Enterprise Information Management Team! Individual reports to Manager, Data Science . Role is remote; must reside in the United States.

ABOUT THE ROLE

The Data Scientist II role is part of the Data Science team with responsibility for modeling complex enterprise problems, discovering enterprise insights, and identifying opportunities for business growth using statistical, algorithmic, mining and visualization techniques.

Your usual day of awesomeness includes:

  • Design experiments, test hypotheses, build analytical frameworks and machine learning models against specific business problems of varying complexity.
  • Develop and leverage processes to maintain and enhance model quality and performance.
  • Conduct data analysis against design algorithms of advanced complexity.
  • Partner with cross functional stakeholders to define expected outcomes and provide scenarios and measurements of success.
  • Communicate insights and explain statistical approaches to business stakeholders in an effective manner.
  • Mentor Data Scientists on the team and champion the use of best practices.
  • Maintain a current awareness and understanding of data science technologies, methods and trends.

WHAT YOU BRING

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in computer science, information systems, engineering, mathematics, statistics, or a related field.
  • 5+ years in a data science or data analyst position.
  • 3+ years experience in a general-purpose programming language such as Scala, Python, Java or R.
  • 3+ years working with non-relational database systems.
  • Equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Advanced level understanding of Python or R.
  • Experience in the use of statistical packages.
  • Experience with a variety of machine learning algorithms.
  • Advanced level understanding of both relational and non-relational data structures and SQL.
  • Familiarity with the basic principles of distributed computing.
  • Knowledge of one or more business/functional areas.
  • Expert Microsoft Excel skills required.
  • Ability to work through a problem logically, identify root causes, provide practical effective solutions while being detail and process oriented.
  • A self-starter who can work without continuous supervision and is highly organized.
  • Programming experience and a demonstrated interest in statistical analysis, informatics and analytics.
  • Experience with information visualization tools such as Tableau preferred.
  • Strong communication skills including written and interpersonal.
  • Wireless Telecommunications experience greatly preferred.
