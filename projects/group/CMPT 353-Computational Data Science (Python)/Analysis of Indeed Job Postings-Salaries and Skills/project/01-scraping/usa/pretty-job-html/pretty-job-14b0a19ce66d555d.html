JOB SUMMARY


The Data Engineer is responsible for planning projects and building data models to meet organizational goals. Responsible for leading the development of data-focused products and utilizing cutting edge data infrastructure and systems to develop models that align with organizational priorities.


MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES :


  • Responsible for building data systems and models that align with business objectives
  • Responsible for managing the full-life cycle development process for Data engineering projects
  • Teach, lead, and counsel colleagues on new techniques or solutions
  • Lead data mining and collection procedures
  • Collaborate with data science and software engineers to enable deployment of sciences and technologies that will scale across the company’s ecosystem
  • Responsible for the implementation of data projects
  • Provide support to inexperienced analysts with high-level expertise in an open-source language (e.g., SQL)
  • Adhere to stringent quality assurance and documentation standards using version control and code repositories (e.g., Git, GitHub, Markdown)
  • Interpret and analyze data problems and guarantee data quality and integrity
  • Ensure that data projects align with organizational goals

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS & ABILITIES :


  • Possess extensive relevant work experience
  • Advanced working knowledge of SQL, Python/R, or other languages and technologies for performing complex data modeling and analysis.
  • Strong understanding of Data Architecture principles.
  • Strong communication skills to relate with the team and convey information effectively.
  • Ability to present technical solutions to non-technical audience in a simple and clear manner
  • Business mindset, with ability to quickly identify risk situations and opportunities, and able to generate creative solutions to business problems
  • Ability to flex as needed, challenge the norm and embrace change
  • Ability to collaborate with various groups of individuals from the data scientists, machine learning engineers, Big Data Specialists and software engineers they manage to other groups including upper management in order to enable deployment of sciences and technologies that will scale across the organization’s ecosystem.

EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS


  • Minimum of Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, or Statistics, or in a relevant discipline, Master’s Degree and/or PhD preferred
  • 3 + years’ experience developing analytical solutions using Data Engineering methods
  • Hands-on knowledge of data wrangling, data cleaning and prep, dimensionality reduction; and experience querying data from relational databases using SQL and NoSQL;
  • Data integration experience
  • Preferred but not required: Expertise with Big Data concepts, tools, and architecture (e.g. Hadoop, Pig, Hive, Spark, Sparkling Water)
