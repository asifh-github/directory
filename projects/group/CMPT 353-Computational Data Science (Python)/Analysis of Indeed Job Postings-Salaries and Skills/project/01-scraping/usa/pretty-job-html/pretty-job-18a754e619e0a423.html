Data Scientist - San Diego, CA (La Jolla/UTC area)

Hybrid Work, Full time M-F 8am-5pm PST, Working Onsite in San Diego 1-2 days/week

Being authorized to work in the U.S. is a precondition of employment.

National Funding is not considering candidates requiring 1099 or C2C.

National Funding is continuing to grow its Data Science Department and has an exciting opportunity for a Data Scientist .

Data Scientist Major Responsibilities
  • Apply data science techniques in credit risk and marketing analytics domains.
  • Work closely with stakeholders to understand business problems and requirements.
  • Manipulate structured and unstructured datasets from disparate data sources. Perform data pre-processing, integration, cleansing, descriptive analysis and visualization.
  • Identify data issues, perform root cause analysis when necessary, and communicate with data owners.
  • Develop models and algorithms to automate and improve business decisions.
  • Identify and test hypotheses, ensuring statistical significance, as part of building predictive models for business application.
  • Monitor and analyze model performance for validation and troubleshooting of issues that may arise.
  • Participate in R&D work to further advance the analytics platform.
  • Contribute to improving the overall analytic capabilities of the team.

The Ideal Candidate
  • Quantitative background in statistics, mathematics, computer science, engineering, or related discipline.
  • 3-4 years of experience in a related field.
  • Understanding and application of various statistical analysis and machine learning methods.
  • Fluency in programming languages such as R, Python, SQL.
  • Proficiency in working with SQL databases. Experience with cloud technology (AWS) a plus.
  • Strong programming practices utilizing version control systems (e.g. Git)
  • Demonstrated machine learning experience.
  • Attention to detail.
  • Strong communication skills across technical and non-technical audience.
