Description

We are looking for a highly motivated Data Scientist to make a difference in data analytics and drive critical decisions at TBC. In a fast-paced and ambiguous environment, the data scientist will perform multiple large, complex and business critical analyses that will drive the business priorities. They will design and build business analytics tools and models to allow inspection and deep business understanding of various use cases. The data scientist will build in house tools and systems working collaboratively with other data scientists, engineers, product managers, and business stakeholders across the company. They will be at the forefront of optimal decision-making for B2B as well as Internal productivity projects.

Job Responsibilities

  • Use innovative technologies such as machine learning, predictive analytics and textual analysis to build models
  • Leverage Deep Learning / AI / NLP and develop text analytics solution to extract information
  • Be a core contributor to our Alteryx workflows and develop custom applications to hand off to business unit analysts or strategy leads
  • Design machine learning pipelines that curate datasets, train, test and validate models, compare model performance to existing or previously catalogued models, and then package models and either deploy them to a real-time serving layer or handle batch scoring activities
  • Work with data engineers to understand the data landscape and handle changes that impact the data infrastructure
  • Collaborate with the different business units on large scale projects and sub-projects
  • Staying abreast of current business and industry trends relevant to the tire business
  • Provide directional guidance and recommendations around data technology, data models, data storage and data analytics
  • Work with large amounts of structured and unstructured data
  • Apply complex analytical techniques to derive actionable insights
  • Design and implement data science applications
  • Design, build, automate and deploy machine learning production pipelines
  • Implement machine learning model lifecycle management framework
  • Build model performance tracking tools, to monitor month over month model performance, identify drivers and provide recommendations for model performance improvement

Qualifications

  • Bachelor or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Physics, Economics, Engineering, Data Science or field with project experience in ML and statistical modeling
  • 5 years of work experience with 3+ years are in Data Science/Machine Learning
  • Ability to communicate complex analysis to key stakeholders
  • Development experience in at least one programming language
  • Working knowledge of one or more SQL languages
  • Experience with visual science / dashboard design principles and tools
  • Proficient in Data Mining, prep, and cleansing tools
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Tools
  • Skilled at applying business math
  • Selecting and employing statistical methods to test hypotheses, develop insights, and build multivariate predictive models
  • Supervised Classification, Supervised Regression, Unsupervised clustering, dimensionality reduction, local interpretable model explanation, automatic machine learning (Random Forest, GLM), K-Means and UMAP
  • Interactive and static visualizations, ggplot2 and plotly

Company Overview

For 65 years, TBC Corporation (TBC), one of North America’s largest marketers of automotive replacement tires, has been a tire company ahead of the curve. Through worldwide operations spanning wholesale, retail, and franchise, TBC also provides automotive maintenance and repair services with best-in-class brands. TBC meets the needs of consumers in search of total car care at more than 3,200 franchised and company-operated tire and automotive service centers under the brands NTB®, Tire Kingdom®, Big O Tires® and Midas®. TBC serves wholesale customers in the United States, Canada, Latin America, Mexico and the Middle East through NTW, TBC Brands, TBC Dealer Group, TBC International and TBC de Mexico.
