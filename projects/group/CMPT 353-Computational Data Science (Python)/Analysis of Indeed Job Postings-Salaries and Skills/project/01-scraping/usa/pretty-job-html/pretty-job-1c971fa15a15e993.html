Full Job Description

Position Responsibilities:

  • Concierge connected vehicle data use cases through the current process and enablers to solutions/answers.
  • Support and enhance the supply side of Connected Vehicle Data.
  • Measure success through the improvement of the CV Data journey and process.
  • Leverage domain network to identify/categorize/rank the connectivity usefulness for all vehicle data elements encountered during customer engagements.
  • Design "data readiness" per use case, understand data nuances and populate feedback mechanisms to document lessons learns and future data use.
  • Capture retrospectives from each CV Data experience for assisting future use cases (what worked/what didn't, extra meta-data needed to come to solution, demystify tribal knowledge).
  • Document CV Data experiences in Data Library and Widget Catalogue to make them shareable to other Customer Success/Data Users.
  • Translate pain points and opportunities of the CV Data journey into user stories for co-located product team to build into software solutions.
  • Collaborate with co-located product team in maturing low-fidelity process improvement prototypes into Self-Service tools for end-users.
  • Collaborate within GDI&A, Enterprise Connectivity, PD, OGC, EPLM, GDIA, and related tech partners to ensure alignment and best possible benefit to the enterprise.


Skills and Experience Required:

  • Knowledge around data documentation, decoding specifications, and tribal knowledge associated with understanding the nuance of Vehicle Data.
  • Knowledge in GPDS process drives the design and delivery of Vehicle Data.
  • Vehicle Launch or Control System development experience a plus.
  • Highly motivated with excellent problem-solving abilities.
  • Highly effective communicator, ability to work with a cross-functional team comprised of internal and external stakeholders across professional levels including senior management.
  • Start-Up mindset (e.g., agility, flexibility, fast pace, no hierarchy, out of box thinking, proactive approach, not afraid to ask questions).
  • Ability to capture customer wants and 'fuzzy concepts' into tangible user epics, stories, and tasks that can be cascaded to appropriate development teams.
  • Familiarity with Agile project management and PDO model a plus.
  • Experience with computer programming or computer data science tools a plus.
  • Lifelong learner with the ability to expand knowledge in a variety of disciplines including engineering, analytics, decision science, data management, and big data.
  • This is a very hands-on role not a 9am-5pm job or a do thing by delegation role. Be a part of a dynamic team that's shaping the future of the Company.


Education Required:

  • Bachelors in Computer Science or related field of study.
