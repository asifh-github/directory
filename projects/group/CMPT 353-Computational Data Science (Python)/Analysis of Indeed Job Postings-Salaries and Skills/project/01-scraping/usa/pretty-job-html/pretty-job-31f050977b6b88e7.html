Full Job Description

As the Director - Data Science, you will direct activities related to the strategy, design, development, and implementation of Data Science, Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) deliveries for enterprise application functions. You will frequently interact with C-Level leaders, lead and create new Machine Learning Operations (MLOPs) and data science capabilities. You will manage multiple Data Scientist teams, supporting different products or lines of businesses to build innovations around AI.

Responsibilities:

  • Create strategies and objectives for the sub-function and develop budgets, policies, and procedures to support the organization
  • Direct and guide managers, and review different AI/ML modelling requests, timeline, and cost
  • Collaborate with business functions/stakeholders on discovery for data science idea generation, provide ideas and influence as well as drive changes
  • Establish and run the Data Science Office and develop, distribute, support, enforce, and integrate standards, best practices, innovative and automation modeling techniques, and procedures
  • Define, build, and lead a team of Data Scientists to help design and implement data modelling processes to develop predictive models, evaluate, and optimize ML models
  • Lead and manage Data Scientists and Engineers that work closely with other teams on large datasets to identify the data patterns and to bring in insightful conclusions for business decisions
  • Manage activities that take place during solution ideation, solution design, solution implementation, optimization, and operation
  • Coordinate enhancements and maintenance of information systems to ensure highly reliable and cost-effective operation

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree - Math, Statistics, Science, Data Science, Computer Science, Electrical/Computer Engineering
  • Master’s Degree - MS in Data Science, AL/ML preferred
  • Licenses/Certificates/Designations - Data Science, AI/ML preferred
  • 10-12 years of IT applications/operational experience, including 5+ years in Applied AI/ML and Data Science
  • Experience in building and leading a team from the ground up, including hiring and ongoing people development
  • Experience in building Machine Learning-enabled products or working with large complex data sets
  • Experience in overseeing, developing, or implementing MLOps processes
  • Strong working knowledge in ML techniques and AI, along with a strong experience/understanding of statistics
  • Strategic planning experience that adapts to communication between technical and business stakeholders
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills, interpersonal skills, and organizational skills
  • Ability to effectively manage multiple work streams, along with demonstrated creativity and latitude in problem solving, including the ability to prioritize and execute multiple competing goals
  • Strong presentation skills with both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Strong team leadership and/or project management experience with multiple simultaneous initiatives
  • Skilled in the management of complex systems and decision-making under uncertainty
  • Excellent time management skills and strong attention to detail, with experience in adhering to best practices of managing the life cycle of data-intensive ML projects
  • Exceptional crisis management skills
  • Ability to travel up to 25%
