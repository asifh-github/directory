Full Job Description

The purpose of this role is to join and be an integral part of the data science function, planning, and leading projects to deliver the Powered By Data project across Mars Petcare divisions using the ecosystem of data available. In this role, you will apply knowledge and experience of data science / ML techniques to our unique data sets and lead data science projects. This will involve building a clear understanding of the potential benefits that connected data and analytics can bring and supporting this vision with stakeholders. The role involves hands-on delivery of analytics and insight as well as leading and mentoring others. Collaboration with stakeholders will be essential throughout to ensure the insights are actionable and that the business is ready to apply them to deliver the benefits. The role requires end-to-end expertise from data, analytics and insight delivery to stakeholder engagement and support of benefit realization. Being self-starting and self-sufficient while also communicating and collaborating effectively within the team and beyond is key.

What are we looking for?

  • 8+ years varied technical experience in delivering statistical analytics, data science and insight on large-scale consumer data sets across multiple sectors.
  • Strong preference for candidates with experience in packaged goods, or retail / DTC, or healthcare
  • Demonstrable experience of using data science and advanced analytics to generate business value and change
  • Demonstrable experience of planning and managing multiple projects in parallel
  • Excellent knowledge of Python (NumPy, pandas, etc.) is required; Familiarity with PySpark is also desirable
  • Capacity and enthusiasm for coaching and mentoring less experienced data scientists
  • Must have excellent communication skills, and interact effectively with all levels of internal / external business stakeholders to engage and Influence
  • Superior planning, project management and organizational orchestration skills and a strong attention to detail
  • Truly inspired by, and want to live, our purpose of creating a better world for pets.
  • 4 + years experience writing productional level code
  • Can estimate time required to productionise code
  • Understands potential pitfalls in code productionization
  • Review and update production ready code
  • Understands the performance issues for performance code

Also, desirable:

  • Passion for growing and strengthening a business using data driven / ML approaches
  • Enjoys explaining how models and systems work to both non-technical and technical stakeholders.
  • Experience working on Azure cloud computing

Personal traits:

  • Comfortable with ambiguity, with a passion for collaboration to achieve objectives
  • Quick learner, ability to understand and simplify highly complex scenarios.

What will be your key responsibilities?

  • The role reports into the Head of Data Science - North America. The primary responsibilities are:
    • Plan and lead data science projects to understand and predict pet and pet owner behaviors
    • Review work of team members identifying optimum methodologies, advising on implementation, and checking business logic
    • Use machine learning techniques, visualizations, & statistical analysis to gain insight into various data sets – some readily available, and some you create and curate yourself
    • Collaborate with internal and external teams to ensure we focus on pet-centric product and service recommendations and be a key player in our network of talent
    • Mentoring, coaching, and inspiring the team
    • Manage multiple projects
    • Develop compelling stories that provide insight into the drivers of business performance and Pet/Pet Owner behavior
    • Contribute to a high performing data science function
