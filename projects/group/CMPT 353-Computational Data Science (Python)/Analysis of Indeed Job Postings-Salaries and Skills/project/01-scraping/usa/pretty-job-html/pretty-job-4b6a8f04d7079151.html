The Minneapolis Heart Institute Foundation® (MHIF) is seeking a Data Scientist to contribute to MHIF’s vision of creating a world without heart and vascular disease. To achieve this bold vision, we are dedicated to improving the cardiovascular health of individuals and communities through innovative research and education. Now in its 40th year, MHIF is recognized globally as a research leader in the broadest range of cardiovascular medicine with cutting-edge science centers.

The Investigator Initiated Research (IIR) program is looking for a kind, curious, and dedicated person to join our team to help drive innovations and advance medical discoveries. As a data scientist, you will be responsible for managing a large and diverse portfolio of research data resources. Working together with clinical investigators, research and informatics teams, you will help improve and streamline data management standards and practices.


Essential Functions:

  • You will collaborate with clinical investigators to design and develop new as well as maintain and support existing data resources to enable data collection, management, extraction, and analysis.
  • You will work with data informatics teams for systematic extractions of data from electronic medical records.
  • You will assist with data abstraction from clinical records.
  • You will help improve and advance the enterprise data strategy to ensure quality and integrity of data resources and help establish, document, and maintain data requirements and standards.
  • You will manage a portfolio of data resources while prioritizing efforts in accordance with projects’ deadlines and institutional milestones.
  • You will work with research teams to develop self-service analytical tools and dashboards to stream-line processes, reporting, and visualization
  • You will use data visualizations, written and verbal communications to share insights with investigators and team members.
  • You will assist with research reports and scientific publications by providing relevant data summaries and displays.


Relationships :

This position reports to the Program Director, IIR and Data Science. In this role you will be working closely with clinical investigators, research scholars, the IIR team and informatics experts. This individual will also place an active role in supporting research efforts during the annual summer internship program.


Qualifications :

Required:

  • Undergraduate degree in data science, statistics, informatics, or related field.
  • Experience in clinical research and REDCap (Research electronic data capture) database administration.
  • Work experience in a data-science role ideally working with clinical data.
  • Strong knowledge and hands on experience of data processing as well as developing visualization and reporting tools in R (tidyverse, shiny, RMarkdown, ggplot).
  • Highly organized with ability to work independently, take initiative and adhere to deadlines while prioritizing the workload across multiple projects
  • Critical thinking, ability to understand and interpret methodological, clinical, and scientific information.
  • Knowledge of and experience with using basic statistics to summarize and present data
  • Ability to communicate complex ideas in an engaging and informative way
  • Work effectively as a team member
  • Basic knowledge of data architecture and SQL
  • Must have strong verbal and written English
  • Must be able to work onsite at the Minneapolis office


Physical Demands :

Ability to work at a computer for long periods of time.


Additional Comments :

This job description represents the major functions of the position but is not intended to be all-inclusive.
