Product Data Scientist

NoneNoneNone
The Product Scientist is responsible for developing and documenting credit scoring models and other complex algorithms which are the foundation of the products used by customers to improve their credit scores.

The Product Scientist will be equal parts Data Scientist and Quantitative Researcher the ideal candidate must have the research skills to understand how traditional credit scoring models work as well as how new data can be applied to quantify the drivers of credit scores. With quantified drivers of credit scores, downstream gains such as optimal scenarios can be constructed.

Essential Functions:
As the Product Scientist, you will

  • Develop novel, new methods of credit scoring and other complex algorithms as well as documenting and explaining that process
  • Interaction with Executive Leadership
  • Work with engineers to ensure the model is properly translated to production
  • Run statistical analysis
  • Run tests and experiments to ensure quality
  • Mentor team members
