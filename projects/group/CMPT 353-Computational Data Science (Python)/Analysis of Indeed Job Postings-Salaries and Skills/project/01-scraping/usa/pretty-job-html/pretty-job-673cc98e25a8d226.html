Job Description

POSITION PURPOSE

The Data Officer, Manager is responsible for developing and governing Veolia’s data and information strategy in order to drive business decisions and growth. This role will develop data procedures and policies, and work closely with various departments to collect, prepare, organize, protect, and analyze data assets while ensuring that the company meets industry best practices. The role oversees the Data Platform team with Data Steward, Business Intelligence, Data Engineering, Data\Governance & Data Science. Duties will include leading interdisciplinary teams, improving and streamlining data and data systems within the company, and driving innovation. The candidate shall have a proven track record in data management, data science, leadership, and information technology systems and tools. A strong background in IT, business, and mathematics is advantageous.

PRIMARY DUTIES / RESPONSIBILITIES

Data Strategy

  • Play an analytical role where they define and drive analytics and business intelligence initiatives in the Data Platform team. Defines appropriate analytical models necessary to support use cases and leverages the power of predictive insights and analytics.
  • Work towards enabling stellar business performance by utilizing not only the data already available to the business, but also tapping into new and innovative sources of data. In this capacity, s/he is tasked with providing the data analytics infrastructure that supports and achieves operational and strategic business goals and targets. Play a leading strategic role where s/he defines and oversees how the business captures, maintains, and applies data and information to support key business processes. S/He establishes the strategic direction for building the business’s data management program, technology stack, and optimizes how the business uses data both internally and externally.
  • Task with developing, coordinating, and implementing strategies that support the business’s development plans to capture new opportunities. In this capacity, s/he acts as a thought leader defining the business’s data strategy and data roadmap.
  • Determine how best to leverage data assets that support the business’s strategy. S/He leads in creating and sustaining a vibrant data organization, technologies, processes, and policies within the department and across the business.

Data Governance

  • Play a highly collaborative role and in this position, s/he will oversee cross-functional data governance while simultaneously ensuring adoption and adherence to data quality and process governance in the
  • relevant collaborating departments.
  • Ensure data is being used according to relevant regulation and best practices.
  • Help lines of business acquire the data they need and use it effectively.
  • Define standards to be used in all aspects of data management, including data and technology architecture, data modeling, functional objects, data supply chain and integration.

Team Management

  • Manage & Coordinate team of Data Steward, Business Intelligence, Data Engineering, Data Governance & Data Science.
  • Structure, prioritize flows of Data projects & priorities for the differents streams
  • Report to Domain CIO
