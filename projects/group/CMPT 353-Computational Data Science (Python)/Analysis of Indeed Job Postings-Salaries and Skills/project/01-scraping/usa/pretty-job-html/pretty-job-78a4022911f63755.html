Full Job Description

Supports a high-performance data science team in its efforts to build innovative solutions for the business by applying the skills, tools, and technologies of data science and artificial intelligence. Team members must be able to manage data to be used in visuals, develop ad-hoc analysis based on business requirement needs, and use appropriate investigative and analytical technologies to interpret and verify results.
Job Description:
BlueCross, as a federal contractor, may be required to implement a COVID-19 vaccine mandate .
Job Responsibilities:
  • Assist in applying Machine Learning algorithms (NLP, DL, SVM, NLG) for business applications and use cases.
  • Help to design, extract, normalize, analyze, review and automate analysis utilizing our Data Warehouse, extracts and data exploration tools; coordinate with appropriate subject matter experts on data source requirements.
  • Ability to transform data using machine learning or statistical techniques (i.e. NLP, embeddings, T-SNE, PCA, etc.) to provide meaningful insight.
  • Assist in designing & developing data visualizations for data storytelling.
  • Help to design and develop ad-hoc analysis based on business requirement needs.
  • Actively participates in the completion of department initiatives to support the development of a best-in-class Data Science function.
