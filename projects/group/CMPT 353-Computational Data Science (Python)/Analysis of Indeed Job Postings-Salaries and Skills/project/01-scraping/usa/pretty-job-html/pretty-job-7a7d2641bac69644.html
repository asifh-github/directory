Data Analyst

Sevenstep is a leading global provider of outsourced talent solutions that help companies around the world solve their most difficult hiring challenges and gain the competitive advantage they seek. As a top-ranking provider on HRO Today’s “Enterprise Baker’s Dozen”, Sevenstep has established a strong reputation for exceptional results, true partnership, and transformative business impact.
We are seeking interested candidates to come onboard in a virtual/remote setting. Our client dedicated teams are on the cutting edge of recruiting, delivering quality candidates for the hardest-to-fill positions around the globe. This global, virtual network is supported with all the necessary resources and technology to foster collaboration and ensure both client and employee satisfaction.

As a Data Analyst within the Sevenstep Analytics & Intelligence team, you will review, compile, and analyze company data for a portfolio of accounts. Each year Sevenstep processes hundreds of thousands of jobs seekers and our mission is to help make sense of those interactions and help Sevenstep in the pursuit of hiring top-tier talent for our clients. It’s a complex human process and difficult to predict. You’ll use data that helps shape our business strategy, identify growth opportunities, understand applicant and hiring behavior, and provide our stakeholders with actionable intelligence.

In this role you will:
  • Create and develop reports, data models, analysis, and statistical calculations as needed.
  • Support client and internal reporting needs, such as running monthly/quarterly reports as well as ad hoc reports.
  • Analyze past and present operations trends, identify root causes, and create predictive analysis that drives process improvement
  • Interpret data and communicate findings with executive stakeholders, management, business partners as well as other employees up to and including those in leadership positions.
  • Ongoing Innovation and Improvement - maintain current knowledge of business intelligence trends and recommend improvements to processes for measurement, control and analysis.

Required:
  • Analysis – 1 to 2 years relevant experience or training in business intelligence, data analytics, data science, or reporting & analysis
  • Problem Solving – Demonstrate excellence at troubleshooting, problem solving, and critical thinking skills.
  • Communication – Present information confidently and effectively in person, over the phone, and through writing.
  • Accuracy – Possess a high attention to detail.
  • Independence – Ability to self-govern and project manage without constant oversight
  • Teamwork – Develop strong relationships with stakeholders and colleagues.

Preferred:
  • Education/Experience with Looker (or other data visualization tools like Qlik, Tableau, etc)
  • Education/Experience with Excel
  • Education/Experience with SQL (or other data management systems or database)
  • Education/Experience with Data Science techniques and tools such as data mining, machine learning, and linear regression
  • Education/Experience performing Ad hoc reporting tasks with Business Objects

What’s in it for you?
  • Fully remote position – an opportunity for you to gain more value, flexibility, and work-life-balance in your career
  • Highly competitive benefit package including health, vision, dental, paid time off (includes vacation and sick time), medical and dependent flexible spending (FSA), health savings (HSA), critical illness coverage, volunteer and activism time off, paid parental leave (even pawternity leave!), 401k match with pre and post-tax options as well as an annual wellness reimbursement
  • Training, training, and more training – our virtual training program offers a library of 100+ courses with classes-based, as well as self-led content that will build the necessary foundation for you to succeed and flourish in your role, as well as continue your learning throughout your time at Sevenstep
  • Ongoing one-on-one support and mentorship in all aspects of your role and development
  • Fun, energetic, team-oriented work environment that makes you excited to go to work every day
