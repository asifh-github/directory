Job Description


Duties: Identify the underlying principles, reasons, or facts of information by breaking down information or data into separate parts. Profile, explore, connect and analyze extensive datasets from various sources, including databases, flat files, product data, user information data, research data, social commentary and other platform data. Devise methods for identifying data patterns and trends in available information source using inferencing, unsupervised learning methods (clustering/segmentation analysis), predictive modeling (regression, decision tree, neural networks) to identify new customer or explore characteristics of existing customers. Communicate and present insight and business improvement suggestion to internal stakeholders. Build customer/audience persona by using syndicated tools and designing surveys, controlled experiments and sampling techniques to in order to help clients understand their target audience, and help the strategy team build more accurate audience segmentation and creative team to create more customized message to the target audience. Understand the goals of clients for advanced analytics capabilities, media measurement, and media optimization. Apply valid scientific techniques and use information obtained from current media partners and in-house solutions.
