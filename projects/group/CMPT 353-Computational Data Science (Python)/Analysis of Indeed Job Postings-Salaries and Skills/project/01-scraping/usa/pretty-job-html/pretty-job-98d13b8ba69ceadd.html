Abbott Rapid Diagnostics (formerly Alere ) is part of Abbott’s Diagnostics family of businesses, bringing together exceptional teams of experts and industry leading technologies. The position of Forensic Scientist Data Review I, (NDOT) is within Toxicology located at Gretna, LA laboratory. In this role, the individual will be responsible for reviewing and ensuring work is performed on an as needed basis in accordance with the SAMHSA mandatory guidelines and those of other regulatory organizations.

WHAT YOU’LL DO

  • Final review of analytical data
  • Operation of the Gas Chromatograph/Mass Spectrometers and initial review of data.
  • Extraction of samples
  • Prepare validation samples for new controls, calibrators and reagents.
  • Storage of supplies
  • Adhere to all security procedures for ensuring confidentiality of donor information
  • Must comply with company policies

Hours: Tuesday-Saturday 7:00 pm-3:30 am

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE YOU’LL BRING

Required

  • High school education
  • BS degree in science (i.e., Chemistry/Forensics/Biology) or equivalent related field

Preferred

  • Advanced ability to understand analytical techniques and procedures
  • Advanced ability to understand laboratory instrumentation
  • Ability to understand certification requirements for SAMHSA, CAP-FUDT, and other regulatory bodies
  • Advanced ability to understand the function of a Laboratory Information System (LIS) and other laboratory computer systems
  • Advanced ability to communicate with clients and MRO’s on drug metabolism
