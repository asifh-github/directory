Full Job Description

Description:


ClaimLogiq is a leading provider of healthcare software and technology, innovating the path forward for proactive payment integrity. We make claims logical, because to us - it's personal .

Job Summary:

The Data Scientist will apply their analytical, statistical, and programming skills to collect, analyze, and interpret large data sets. They will use this information to develop data-driven solutions to difficult business tasks.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for the creation, modification, and maintenance of medical data extraction rules (80%-90% of their time)
  • Work independently creating and assessing their rules using proprietary integrated rules engine framework
  • Required to test new tools and utilities that are to be integrated into the platform
  • Monitor data quality during the development life cycle (10%-20%).
  • Direct support of customers or for use by data science team as foundational units of our product offerings
Requirements:
  • Experience with rules-based NLP technologies and or data extraction from unstructured text
  • Experience with Java regular expressions
  • Experience with terminologies/ontologies
  • Knowledge of any cloud environment
  • At least one of the following: Healthcare data analyst, clinical data specialist or medical background

Preferred:

  • Medical Terminology Knowledge of either ICD 9, ICD 10 or SNOMED or medical coding
  • Experience with Programming (Java, Python)

Personal/Business Traits:

  • Research Oriented, Self-driven and detail-oriented, able to take ownership of Data
    Science and other assigned projects.
  • Comfortable working in remote culture (WFH) and with minimal supervision.

Computer Equipment and Software

  • Strong computer skills: Microsoft Office suite and LMS systems
