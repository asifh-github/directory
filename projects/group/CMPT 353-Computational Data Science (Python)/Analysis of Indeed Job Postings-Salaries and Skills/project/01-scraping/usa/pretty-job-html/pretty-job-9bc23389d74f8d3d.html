Full Job Description

Kadiak LLC , a Koniag Government Services company, is seeking an experienced Data Scientist with a Public Trust Clearance to support Kadiak LLC, and our government customer. This position is Remote. The Salary is 65k-76K.

We offer competitive compensation and an extraordinary benefits package including health, dental and vision insurance, 401K with company matching, flexible spending accounts, paid holidays, three weeks paid time off, and more

Essential Functions, Responsibilities & Duties may include, but are not limited to:
  • Knowledge of a wide variety of analytical techniques used to determine and communicate trends and patterns, fill gaps in information and project events, identify anomalies, ascribe meaning to events or information from disparate sources, and develop defensible judgments and conclusions based on accepted research and analytical methodologies.
  • Thorough knowledge of data management concepts, principles, and methods for database logical and physical design, development, and maintenance of information management systems.
  • Experience in various Database technologies, Python scripting and Structured Query Language (SQL).
  • Skill in information storage and retrieval, sorting theory, requesting variance analysis, and data sampling.
  • Technical expertise regarding common data controlling and manipulation techniques, such as data conditioning, structuring, standardizing, ‘data wrangling’, de-duplication; ETL (Extract/Transform/Load); data exploration; and data recycling/repurposing; and data visualization applications.
  • Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting and analyzing packages using scripting languages.
  • Knowledge of relevant federal, state, and local laws and codes to ensure consideration of and consistency with those laws/codes as they relate to the work performed.
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Highly developed oral and written communication skills required to present findings or translate the data into an understandable document. Must be skilled and able to prepare and present highly complex matters, material, and/or issues to others.
  • Must be able to work effectively under the pressure of tight time frames and rigid deadlines on investigative, intelligence, and operational matters.
  • Knowledge of data normalization and relational/non-relational database design techniques and practices, including database logical and physical design.
  • Knowledge of data stores, data analytics and data warehousing concepts, methods, and technologies sufficient to participate in the design of data analytic and data warehousing systems.
Work Experience, Knowledge, Skills & Abilities:
  • 6+ years of total experience in the Information Technology field
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Data Science or Statistics required.
  • 3+ years of Data analysis and Data manipulation experience.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills, a high level of integrity, critical thinking, and leadership courage.
