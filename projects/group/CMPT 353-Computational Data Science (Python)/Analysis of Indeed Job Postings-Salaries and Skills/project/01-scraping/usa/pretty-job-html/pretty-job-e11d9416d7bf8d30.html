Pharmacy Benefits Reinvented- As the industry’s alternative pharmacy benefit manager (PBM), we’re committed to making prescriptions more affordable for you and your members. Here at Navitus, our team members work in an environment that celebrates creativity, fosters diversity and encourages growth. We welcome new ideas and share a passion for excellence.

The Director of Data Governance champions and enables data as a strategic asset for the organization and is responsible for building out at a tactical level the Navitus data fabric and enabling a well-structured data ecosystem and associated governance for the organization, under the guidance of the digital data products and platform leadership.

The primary responsibility of the Director will be aiding in the implementation of the Navitus Enterprise Data Operating model and governance which includes the data management strategies and associated use cases, policies, and standards. The Director will create and maintain an accurate enterprise master data assets inventory. Finally, this role serves as the primary business engagement lead working closely with leaders across the enterprise to best leverage Navitus master data assets and enable key Navitus growth use cases.

Success in this role requires experience in the implementation of an organizational data strategy and associated operating model that fosters a data-driven culture. The Director will enable all forms of business outcomes by envisioning the relevant data-enabled strategies and establishing data governance. To do so, the Director must also possess organizational team skills and competencies required to build and modernize applications and associated data essential for the Navitus digital transformation.

Is this you? Find out more below!

How do I make an impact on my team?

  • Responsibilities are summarized below but are not limited to the following areas.
  • Collaborate with subject matter experts, functional owners, data owners and analysts to define and implement data strategy, policies, controls, and programs to ensure key enterprise data is accurate, complete, secure, and reliable.
  • Support the leader for digital data products & platforms in leading key data initiatives for Navitus including managing the day-to-day activities of the Enterprise Data Operating Model and Governance implementation.
  • Coordinate the day-to-day activities of Master Data Management (MDM) processes while owning the implementation and execution of enterprise data governance/initiatives.
  • Implement processes and governance to ensure data integrity, data quality, data standards, and change management of data across multiple groups/business functions and systems.
  • Assume oversight for data accuracy, completeness, consistency, integrity, timeliness, conformity, and validity within the established data governance policies.
  • Coordinate all master data activities with other organizational Governance teams, working within established protocols and policies.
  • Develop data standards which align with enterprise requirements and priorities.
  • Maintain appropriate data documentation, including master data management, data quality report inventory, enterprise data definitions, policies & procedures, etc.
  • Assess the current master data management processes, identify challenges, quantify the business value, establish processes to address the challenges and design the future state model and vision.
  • Define, develop, and measure data quality and key performance indicators (KPIs) to continuously monitor data quality.
  • Continuously review and validate master data for accuracy and quality; regularly monitor for missing and inconsistent data.
  • Perform root-cause analysis on master data problems then recommend and execute corrective actions.
  • Provide an accurate and timely response to all requests to create or modify item master information.
  • Manage changes to master data successfully.
  • Leverage strong problem solving and data analysis and manipulation skills to analyze, cleanse, and monitor large amounts of data.
  • Develop communication and training plans needed to implement governance policies.
  • Research and remain current with Cloud computing and MDM methodologies.
