Full Job Description

For 20 years, Insight has provided high-quality, nationally recognized research and evaluation services to government, philanthropic, and nonprofit clients. Our focus is on issues affecting at-risk and vulnerable populations. Key practice areas include education, family support, food and nutrition, health, and military and veteran support. Insight’s innovative Data Science and Analytics group helps integrate research with data science and process improvement methods.
Headquartered in Arlington, Virginia, we offer outstanding potential for growth, competitive salaries, and comprehensive benefits in a collegial, family-friendly environment. For more information on the type of work we conduct and our corporate culture, please visit our website: www.insightpolicyresearch.com.
Position Summary
This position will support project teams in planning and implementing a broad range of data visualization and dashboard design tasks using survey and administrative data to support food and nutrition assistance, health, and education projects. Candidates should be familiar with quantitative data cleaning, data analysis, and data visualization. The ideal candidate will have demonstrated skills in at least one data visualization software (e.g., Tableau, Power BI, QuickSight, ArcGIS, R Shiny), familiarity with best practices in data visualization and dashboard design, and excellent oral and written communication skills. Candidates should be interested in learning additional data visualization software and programming languages (e.g., R, Python, SAS).
Responsibilities

  • Contribute to the design and development of interactive graphics and dashboards
  • Assist in developing dashboard wireframes and prototypes for client review
  • Assist in creating and editing data visualizations, including geospatial analyses
  • Assist in compiling, editing, and structuring data for data visualizations and dashboard development
  • Be willing to learn new data visualization software and programming languages
  • Be willing to explore and research best practices in data visualization and dashboard design
  • Produce written documentation for analytic datasets and dashboards
  • Develop user-friendly resources and guides for conducting data analysis activities and interacting with dashboards/data visualizations
  • General project management tasks and other duties as needed
  • Perform activities under supervision of senior staff

Education

  • Bachelor’s degree in public policy, data science, social science; 1–2 years of experience

Qualifications

  • Base-level experience with one or more data visualization software (Tableau or Power BI is preferred)
  • Base-level experience with designing dashboard wireframes and developing dashboard prototypes
  • Base-level experience synthesizing and communicating quantitative research for professional and lay audiences via data visualizations and dashboards
  • Strong interest in public policy or safety net research
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Proven oral and written communication skills
  • Flexibility to handle multiple tasks and work on numerous projects
  • Willingness to contribute to other project tasks as needed
  • Experience in data cleaning, data manipulation, and data analysis in one or more programming languages (e.g., SAS, R, Python) a plus
  • Experience with UX and usability testing a plus
