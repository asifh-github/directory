About the Role

We are looking for a Data Scientist to join our data science team on a permanent basis. As a data scientist, you will be helping to improve Altoida’s machine learning models using your knowledge of statistics, Neural Networks, and Bayesian Inference. Upcoming projects you will be working on are speech analysis, genetic analysis, and optimizing test sequences using Bayesian inference. #LI-Remote

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with the data science team to continuously improve Altoida’s machine-learning models using your understanding of the problem domain
  • Envision, implement, and evaluate new features and approaches to help improve Altoida’s machine-learning models
  • Lead the implementation of methods for new feature domains such as speech analysis
  • Translate data science requests from clients into novel classification models and statistics
  • Use state-of-the-art methods to validate newly developed models and procedures
  • Communicate with the clients and sales teams about Altoida’s data science offerings
  • Monitor incoming data to meet Altoida’s quality requirements
  • Review merge requests in data science repositories.
  • Assist in writing reports, publications, and patent applications
  • Look for opportunities to improve the current processes and implement best practices from the industry

Preferred Qualifications

  • Master's degree in data science, machine learning, artificial intelligence, operations research, or an equivalent field
  • 3+ years of industry experience in the field of data science or machine learning
  • 3+ years of industry experience in python programming
  • Strong knowledge of tools like scikit-learn and TensorFlow
  • A thorough understanding of neural networks, Bayesian networks, ensemble learning, feature engineering, and feature selection methods
  • Knowledge of advanced statistical techniques and concepts (regression, properties of distributions, statistical tests, proper usage, etc.) and experience with their potential applications
  • Previous experience with voice analytics and time series analysis is a plus.
